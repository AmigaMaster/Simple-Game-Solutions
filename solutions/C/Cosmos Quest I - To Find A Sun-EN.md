# Cosmos Quest I: To Find A Sun - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 03.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [itch.io](https://cosmosquest.itch.io/cosmos-quest-i)

## 1. Zhena

### 1.1. Lake

- USE shuttle
- EXIT east

### 1.2. Sandy Area

- EXIT south

### 1.3. Plants

- USE `Knife` on tent remains (`Piece of cloth`) (2/2)
- EXIT north, west

### 1.4. Lake

- LOOK AT hole (left side of hose) (1/3)
- USE `Piece of cloth` on hole (`Oily piece of cloth`) (3/6)
- EXIT west

### 1.5. Volcanic Rock

- EXIT south

### 1.6. Mud Puddle

- USE `Oily piece of cloth` on plant (when you enter the screen, the plant is exactly at your head position) (3/9)
- EXIT south and directly come back
- PICK UP shell (from the plant) (`Shell`) (2/11)

### 1.7. Lake

- USE `Shell` on cold liquid (`Shell with liquid`) (2/13)
- USE `Shell with liquid` on shuttle (4/17)
- USE shuttle
- USE hatch

## 2. Shuttle

- LOOK AT writing (at the top screen, above the shuttle window, left to teh small red button) (1/18)
- LOOK AT writing (at the top of the screen at the very left) (1/19)
- LOOK AT monitor
  - **NOTE:** The coordinates `22:5|8:1|27:4|31:2|19:3|38:6|20:1|43:5`
- LOOK AT `ID card`
  - Look for the coordinates from the monitor inside this numbers, where `22:5` means line 5, character 22 (`2`), and so on
  - The complete code: `29097626`
- USE keyboard
  - ENTER code `29097626`
  - SET COURSE
- USE button (above the keyboard) (1/20)
- USE button (at the top right, above the shuttle window) (5/25), (14/39)
- TALK TO Cpt. Hurghatha (2/41)
- USE (top) button (2/43)

## 3. Jaro Motor Inn

### 3.1. Front of Motel

- WALK TO entrance

### 3.2. Entrance

- TALK TO guard
- USE `Oily piece of cloth` on guard (2/45)
- EXIT

### 3.3. Front of Motel

- LOOK AT planet (in the top left corner) (3/48)
- EXIT to (left) oxygen tank (right of the tank)

### 3.4. Left Door

- USE `Oily piece of cloth` on hole (top left corner of door) (6/54)
- EXIT

### 3.5. Front of Motel

- EXIT to (right) oxygen tank

### 3.6. Air Control Center

- Adjust the settings so the air goes to the guard at the front:
  - USE air direction (clockwise)
  - ENABLE **both** vents of the oxygen tanks
  - ENABLE vent in the lower right
  - DISABLE (X) all other vents
  - USE activation button (on the right side)
  - EXIT

### 3.7. Entrance

- TALK TO guard (16/70)

### 3.8. Bar, Front

- PICK UP card on bar stool (`Business Card`) (6/76)
- TALK TO lady (5/81)
- TALK TO bartender
- TALK TO lady (again) (3/84)
- EXIT northwest (behind the bar)

### 3.9. Bar, Backside

- TALK TO Ayatolian
- USE trash bin (`Peeled boiled egg`) (10/94)
- EXIT south

### 3.10. Reception

- TALK TO girl
- LOOK AT certificate (on the wall) (7/101)
- USE `Peeled boiled egg` on certificate (`Peeled boiled egg with seal`) (20/121)
- USE `Peeled boiled egg with seal` on `Business card` (`Business card with license`) (25/146)
- USE `Business card with license` on girl (14/160)

## 4. Ayatolia

### 4.1. Base, Western Area

- PICK UP rock (in your cell) (`Rock`) (10/170)
- USE `Rock` on valve (above the pipe just outside your cell where the energy bars are) (20/190)
- EXIT east

### 4.2. Base, Entrance Area

- LOOK AT chamber (the one right next to the stairs) (25/215)
- LOOK AT folder (in the second last chamber) (9/224)
  - **NOTE:** The frequency of your conversation `2760 Hz, Day 1`
- EXIT north

### 4.3. Desert

- EXIT east

### 4.4. Desert, Openings

- USE `Shell` on opening (12/236)
- EXIT west **3 times**

### 4.5. Base, Western Area

- USE door (15/251)
- EXIT east, north, east **3 times**

### 4.6. Horizon

- USE transmitter
  - ENTER frequency from the information you got from the folder notes `2760`
  - SET FREQUENCY (24/275)

### 4.7. Base, Western Area

- WALK around through the screens for some minutes and walk into the Base, Western Area. Eventually you hear the pod landing. (25/300)
- EXIT all the way to the east (10/310)

## 5. Space Station

### 5.1. Shuttle Bay Lift

- EXIT southeast

### 5.2. Shuttle Bay

- USE intercom (10/320) (20/240)
- EXIt west

### 5.3. Shuttle Bay Lift

- EXIT east (the upper of both paths to the east)

### 5.4. Shuttle Bay

- EXIT east (using the top of both paths) **2 times** (10/350)
