# Cedric And The Revolution - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 18.11.2021 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game at [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/site/games/game/663-cedric-and-the-revolution) or a patched version for higher screen resolutions from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/cedric-and-the-revolution_dxwnd)

## 1. Village

#### 1.0.1. Blacksmith

- PICK UP matches (small hotspot in the shelf at the right side)
- TALK TO Victor
  - "Could you get those matches up that shelf?" (`matches`)
- PICK UP Hammer (`hammer`)
- PICK UP bellows (`bellows`)
- EXIT south

### 1.1. Village Square

- PICK UP fishing net (`fishing net`)
- USE `hammer` on nails (`nails`)
- EXIT north through the passage to the castle

### 1.2. Entrance to the Castle

- PICK UP bouncy ball (`bouncy ball`)
- EXIT south again

### 1.3. Village Square

- EXIT east to the harbor

### 1.4. Harbor

- USE `bellows` on Tax Collector
- TALK TO Captain Howard
- PICK UP monocle
- PICK UP crab (`pinchy the crab`)
- PICK UP monocle (`monocle`)
- USE `monocle` on Captain Howard
- ENTER into the bar at the left side

#### 1.4.1. Bar

- PICK UP honey (in the shelf on the left) (`honey`)
- PICK UP glass (`empty glass`)
- PICK UP bag with coffee beans (`coffee beans`)
- TALK TO Barman
- LOOK AT coffee machine
  - unlit candle
  - no water
  - no coffee powder
- USE `pinchy the crab` on `coffee beans` (`coffee powder`)
- USE `coffee powder` on coffee machine
- USE `matches` on coffee machine
- EXIT bar and walk west to the

### 1.5. Village Square

- EXIT west

### 1.6. Backstreet

- TALK TO Louise
- USE `fishing net` on `honey` (`sticky fishing net`)
- USE `sticky fishing net` on hole (in the door)
- TALK TO Victor
  - "Victor, think you can block the passage?"
- USE `bouncy ball` on hat
- PICK UP bouncy ball (`bouncy ball`)
- EXIT and enter the

#### 1.6.1. Blacksmith

- USE `empty glass` on bucket with water (`glass with water`)

#### 1.6.2. Bar

- USE `glass with water` on coffee machine (`empty glass`)
- USE `empty glass` with coffee machine (`glass with coffee`)
- USE `glass with coffee` on Barman
- EXIT bar and walk to the Village Square

## 2. Castle

#### 2.0.3. Cell

- TALK TO Guard
  - "Hey, can I have something to eat?" (`pretzel`)
- USE `pretzel` on Victor
- TALK TO Guard
  - "Hey, can I have something to eat?" (`soup`)
- USE `soup` on toilet (`spoon`, `bowl`)
- USE `bowl` on guard
- USE `spoon` on key (beside the guard) (`keys`)
- USE `keys` on lock
- EXIT cell

### 2.1. Hallway

- USE drawer
- LOOK AT drawer (`burger`)
- EXIT east

### 2.2. Candle Room

- PICK UP candles (`candles`)
- TALK TO rat
- PICK UP plank (`plank`)

### 2.3. Hallway

- USE `plank` on desk
- PICK UP key (above the desk) (`keys`)

#### 2.3.1. Cell

- USE `keys` on Victor

### 2.4. Hallway

- TALK TO Victor
  - "Can you lift me up to that window, please?"
    **NOTE:** When you want to go outside again, just talk to Victor again

### 2.5. Window Sill

- TALK TO gargoyle
- USE (right) window

### 2.6. Sorcerer Room

- EXIT through the window

### 2.7. Window Sill

- USE bricks

### 2.8. Bed-Chamber

- PICK UP tree (`leaves`)
- PICK UP feather (on the bed at the right side) (`feather`)
- EXIT through the window and back to the

### 2.9. Candle Room

- USE `candles` on hot water pipe (`melty candles`)
  **NOTE:** You now have to hurry or the candles will get hard again.

### 2.10. Bed-Chamber

- USE `melty candles` on floor

### 2.11. Window Sill

- USE (right) window **2 times**

### 2.12. Sorcerer Room

- LOOK AT book
  1. Tears of a magical being
  2. Essence of nature
  3. Proof of friendship
  4. Mix it well and place is inside a cicle of 6 candles.

### 2.13. Window Sill

- TALK TO gargoyle
- USE `feather` on gargoyle
- USE `bowl` on gargoyle

### 2.14. Hallway

- USE `leaves` on potato press
- USE `bowl` on potato press
- USE potato press
- PICK UP bowl (`bowl`)

### 2.15. Candle Room

- USE `burger` on Cedric (`cheese`)
- USE `cheese` on rat
- TALK TO rat
  - "Can you take a photo of me?" (`photo of cedric`)
- TALK TO Victor
  - "Would you pose for a photo with me, Victor?" (`cedric and victor`)
- USE `cedric and victor` on bowl

### 2.16. Bed Chamber

- USE `bowl` on circle of candles
- USE knurled handle
- EXIT north

### 2.17. Marble Room

- OPEN cabinet
- PICK UP handle (`handle`)

### 2.18. Candle Room

- USE `handle` on cold water pipe
- USE handle

### 2.19. Marble Room

- USE `bowl` on king
