# Cosmos Quest IV: The Ayatolian March - Walkthrough

|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|

Download the free game from [itch.io](https://cosmosquest.itch.io/cosmos-quest-iv)

## 1. Xara Briga Orbital Station

### 1.1. Prison Cell

- LOOK AT cabinet
  - PICK UP handle (`Handle`) (8/8)
  - PICK UP mirror (`Mirror`) (5/13)
  - EXIT
- LOOK AT trash bin (5/18)
  - PICK UP Gazar's Poem (`Torn poem`) (8/26)
  - LOOK AT `Torn poem` (5/31)
  - **NOTE:** Order the pages so a poem forms:
    - Page 2: two hearts be wandering in
    - Page 1: at as one. seven nights
    - Page 3: separated for their love
    - Page 4: they will die, they have to fight...
    - **NOTE:** The numbers `2174`
  - PICK UP wire (`Wire`) (5/36)
  - EXIT
- USE `Handle` on `Wire` (`Handle with wire`) (10/46)
- USE `Handle with wire` on stick (at the laundry opening) (`Stick`) (9/55)
- USE `Stick` on `Mirror` (`Stick and mirror`)
- USE `Handle with wire` on `Stick and mirror` (`Stick and mirror with wire`) (20/75)
- USE `Stick and mirror with wire` on panel (10/85)
  - ENTER code from the poem `2174`
  - USE the Enter-Key (20/105)
- USE opening (of the laundry basket)

### 1.2. Laundry

#### 1.2.1. Elevator Room

- LOOK AT droid (3/108)
- LOOK AT elevator (2/110)
- PICK UP magnetic disk (`Magnetic disk`) (5/115)
- EXIT right

##### 1.2.1.1. Control Panel Room

- USE `Magnetic disk` on cabinet (on the wall next to the window) (5/120)
- USE control panel
- PICK UP magnetic disk (`Magnetic disk`, `Laser sword`, `Diary card`, `Oxygen tank`) (20/140)
- EXIT left

#### 1.2.2. Elevator Room

- USE `Laser sword` on droid (10/150)
- PICK UP droid's hand (`Droid's hand`) (5/155)
- USE `Droid's hand` on panel (at the elevator) (7/162)
- ENTER elevator

## 2. Coffin

- USE `Oxygen tank` on oxygen socket (above your hips) (1/163)

## 3. Asteroid

### 3.1. Junkyard

- USE box (`Card reader`) (5/168)
- EXIT left

### 3.2. Avies Plantation

- PICK UP pump (`Pump`) (6/174)
- USE trap cage (5/179)
- EXIT right **2 times**

### 3.3. Ramp

- PICK UP container (below the ship components) (`Empty container`) (5/184)
- EXIT right (behind the exit tube)

### 3.4. Shuttle

- ENTER shuttle (`Key`, `Translator`) (6/190) (10/200)
- LOOK AT `Key` (4/204)
- USE `Key` on fuel lid (10/214)
- USE `Pump` on fuel tank (8/222)
- USE `Empty canister` on pump (`Container with fuel`) (5/227)
- EXIT left **3 times**

### 3.5. Avies Plantation

- USE `Container with fuel` on fuel tank (`Empty container`) (5/232)
- USE starter (12/244) (10/254)
- EXIT right **2 times** (14/268)

### 3.6. Ramp

- EXIT up the ramp

### 3.7. Entrance Hall

- ENTER shuttle (at the bottom) (`Magnetic card`) (8/276)
- LOOK AT plaque (under the memorial bust) (4/280)
- USE `Laser sword` on plaque (16/296)
- PICK UP precious metal (`Precious metal`) (8/304)
- USE button

### 3.8. Engine Room

- TALK TO Engineer **6 times** (`Anti-radiation gum`) (8/312) (6/318)
- EXIT left

### 3.9. Hallway

- USE `Anti-radiation gum` on door panel (25/343)
- EXIT left

### 3.10. Storage Room

- TALK TO trader **3 times** (22/365)
- EXIT right

### 3.11. Hallway

- Stand on the far right side at the handrail
- WAIT until the robot reappears and tries to close the door
- ENTER door

### 3.12. Communication Room

- PICK UP electronic key (from a small cabinet under the fan) (`Electronic key`) (10/375)
- TALK TO Laroth
- USE `Precious metal` on Laroth (12/387)
- EXIT left, right

### 3.13. Engine Room

- USE `Electronic key` on electronic lock (18/405)
- USE LSS panel (in the right cabinet) (10/415)
- USE `Card reader` on charger (12/427)
- USE `Card reader` on `Diary card` (`Card reader with card`) (8/435)
- LOOK AT `Card reader with card`
  - PAGE DOWN until the end of the text (16/451)
  - **NOTE:** `Niskia`, `Cloud with blue and violet spots`, Coordinate `Q8 (1891,7760)`, `Bar on Hain-Boa`
  - EXIT (6/457)
- EXIT right

### 3.14. Entrance Hall

- EXIT right

### 3.15. Shuttle Bay

- ENTER (left) shuttle (28/485)

### 4.3. Space Ship

#### 4.3.1. Landing Site

- EXIT left

#### 4.3.2. Western Barrier

- LOOK AT barrier (the bottom one, which is flickering) (15/550)
- EXIT right **2 times**

#### 4.3.3. Space Ship Entrance

- TALK TO guard
- EXIT right

#### 4.3.4. Barrier Control

- PICK UP keycard (from the slot reader at the door) (`Keycard`) (10/560)
- EXIT left

#### 4.3.5. Space Ship Entrance

- ENTER Space Ship

##### 4.3.5.1. Main Hall

- PICK UP z accelerator (on the right side above the engine and casting wheel) (`Z accelerator`) (6/566)
- PICK UP x accelerator (at the same spot) (`X accelerator`) (6/572)
- USE `Keycard` on keycard slot (right next to the cabinets) (8/580)
- LOOK AT stuff (in the now open cabinet) (`Connector`) (16/596)
- LOOK AT `Connector` (4/600)
- EXIT southeast and to the left to enter your shuttle

### 4.4. Village

#### 4.4.1. Shuttle Bay

  - **NOTE:** `knowledge is nothing`

- LOOK AT `Map` (14/652)

- PICK UP y accelerator (`Y accelerator`) (6/668)
- USE `Time controller` on droid (3/671)
- USE `Z accelerator` on droid (3/674)
- USE `X accelerator` on droid (3/677)
- USE `Y accelerator` on droid (3/680)
- ENTER shuttle
