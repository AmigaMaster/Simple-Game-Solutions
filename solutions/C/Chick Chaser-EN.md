# Chick Chaser - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 08.03.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/chick-chaser)

## 1. College Room

- USE door
- LOOK AT door (Locker Code: `396`) (4/4)
- USE drawers (of your desk) (`Scissors`) (3/7)
- USE bed side (on the left side of the bed) (2/9)
- PICK UP mag (`Mahatma's Mag`) (2/11)
- USE `Mahatma's Mag` with bathroom (2/13)
- USE door
- EXIT north

## 2. Map

- **ATTENTION:** You can die if you walk onto the street when the car is driving on it.
- WALK TO fountain

## 3. Fountain

- TALK TO Mahatma (2/15)
- EXIT south

## 4. Geology Faculty

### 4.1. Outside

- LOOK AT windows (beside the ladder) (1/16)
- ENTER door

### 4.2. Entrance Hallway

- EXIT upstairs

### 4.3. First Floor Hallway

- LOOK AT Theresa (2/18)
- TALK TO Theresa
- TALK TO geek of the week
- USE noticeboard (`Thumbtack`) (2/20)
- LOOK AT examination room (1/21)
- TALK TO Theresa
- EXIT downstairs, west **2 times**

## 5. Sports Facility

### 5.1. Entrance Area

- LOOK AT sign (2/23)
- EXIT south

## 6. Library

### 6.1. Entrance Area

- EXIT north

### 6.2. Office

- TALK TO Con the Watchdog
- EXIT north, south

## 7. Fountain

- TALK TO Mahatma

## 8. Geology Faculty

### 8.1. Entrance Hallway

- LOOK AT top 10 book (on the left notice board) (2/25)

## 9. Library

### 9.1. Office

- TALK TO Con the Watchdog
  - "I have a specific request for a book." (2/27)
- TALK TO Carmen (until she enters the other room and comes back here)
- EXIT north

### 9.2. Entrance Area

- USE `Scissors` with hand ladder (4/31)
- LOOK AT top shelf (above the ladder) (2/33)

### 9.3. Office

- TALK TO Carmen
  - "Could you get me 'A sweet love tale..." (2/35)
- USE health magazine (on the desk) (`Pool Pass`) (3/38)

### 9.4. Entrance Area

- USE broken hand ladder (`Short Piece of Wood`) (2/40)
- PICK UP poetry book (`A Romulus Reader`) (2/42)
- LOOK AT `A Romulus Reader` (1/43)

## 10. Geology Faculty

### 10.1. Outside

- USE ladder (2/45)

### 10.2. Window Sill

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE `Thumbtack` with `Short Piece of Wood` (`Makeshift Harpoon`) (3/48)
- USE `Makeshift Harpoon` with exam papers (in the left window) (`Exam Paper`) (6/54)
- USE ladder, ENTER building, EXIT upstairs

### 10.3. First Floor Hallway

- USE `Exam Paper` with Theresa (4/58)
- EXIT downstairs

### 10.4. Entrance Hallway

- WALK past the dean's office **multiple times**
  - **NOTE:** The word `moron`
- USE alarm button (next to the door) (2/60)
  - ENTER `morons` (6/66)
- PICK UP trophies (`Sporting Trophy`) (4/70)

## 11. Sports Facility

### 11.1. Entrance Area

- USE `Pool Pass` on Rodney (2/72)
- EXIT west

### 11.2. Lockers Hallway

- USE lockers
  - "396" (2/74)
- USE lockers (`Magnet`) (2/76)
- EXIT east **2 times**

### 11.3. Rooftop

- PICK UP flowers (`Flower`) (2/78)
- USE `Scissors` with `Makeshift Harpoon` (`Short Piece of Wood`, `Thumbtack`) (3/81)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE `Thumbtack` with brainless pile of muscles (6/87)
- **QUICK:**
  - LOOK AT plane (1/88)
- LOOK AT Shawnee (2/90)
- USE `Sporting Trophy` with Shawnee (4/94)
- LOOK AT collateral damage
- PICK UP collateral damage (`Muscles Unplugged`) (2/96)

## 12. Fountain

- Mahatma is not here
- EXIT south

## 13. Map

- WALK to the pub in the northeast but pay attention to the traffic. When you are hit by a car, you will die.

## 14. Pub

### 14.1. Street

- LOOK AT drain (2/98)
- USE `Magnet` with drain (`Coin`) (2/100)
- TALK TO bouncer
- EXIT east

## 15. Sporting Oval

- USE sports bag (`Invitation`) (2/102)
- EXIT east

## 16. Geology Faculty

### 16.1. First Floor Hallway

- USE `Muscles Unplugged` with geek of the week (4/106)
- USE despicable form of life (`Brace`) (2/108)

## 17. Sports Facility

### 17.1. Lockers Hallway

- USE `Coin` with snack machine (`Chocolates`) (3/111)
- USE ladies dressing room
- USE `Brace` with wall (next to the lockers) (6/117)
- LOOK AT peekholes (3/120)

## 18. Pub

### 18.1. Street

- USE `Invitation` with bouncer (1/121)
- ENTER pub

### 18.2. Party

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- LOOK AT girl of dubious morals (on the left on the catwalk) (2/123)
- TALK TO coach Gibbs
- TALK TO Rodderick (`Bottle Opener`) (2/125)
  - "Nah, one is enough."
- USE door

### 18.3. Street

- TALK TO sick one (2/127)

## 19. Geology Faculty

### 19.1. Entrance Hallway

- USE Dean's office
- USE phone
  - "Call the Dean's Office." (2/129)
- USE Dean's office (2/131)

#### 19.1.1. Dean's Office

- USE chair (3/134)
- USE statue (`Key`) (3/137)
- USE cabinet (under the books)
  - USE folders (2/139)
  - USE `Key` with box (2/141)
    - USE lotsa cash
    - Leave the money (3/144)
  - EXIT
- USE cabinet
- USE windows (2/146)

### 19.2. Entrance Hallway

- USE phone
  - "Call the bus station" (4/150)

## 20. College Room

- PICK UP bus ticket (from the desk)
  - "Yeah..." (4/154)

## 21. Thathurst Country

### 21.1. Outside

- LOOK AT mailbox (1/155)
- USE `Bottle Opener` with vent (on the right) (4/159)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE vent to get inside the house (4/163)
