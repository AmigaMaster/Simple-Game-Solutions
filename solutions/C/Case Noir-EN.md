## 1. Central Police Station

### 1.1. Street

- ENTER main doors

### 1.2. Entrance Hall

- TALK TO Franky
- ENTER Chief's office (Cutscene)
- TALK To McCloud
- ENTER door

### 1.3. Hallway

- USE `Key` on locker 11 (`Gun`, `ID Card`)
- EXIT south **2 times**

### 1.4. Street

- USE Ford Deluxe

## 2. Gourd Lake Park

- TALK TO Coroner
- USE legs of the corpse (`Missing clothing`)
- USE footsteps (in the sand left of the corpses feet) (`Shoe prints`)
- USE orange thing in the sand on the left side (`Ornate ashtray`)
- USE head of the corpse (`Victim's injuries`)
- USE right hand of the corpse (`Finger injury`)
- USE handbag (`Victim's bag`)
- TALK TO McCloud
- USE `Ornate ashtray` on McCloud

## 3. Enchant Club

### 3.1. Street

- ENTER door

### 3.2. Bar

- TALK TO barman
- EXIT east

### 3.3. Back Room

- USE `ID Card` on Hobak
- TALK TO Hobak (`Victim's husband`, `License plate No.`)
  - "Lie"
- TALK TO Hobak
  - "Truth"
- EXIT west

### 3.4. Bar

- TALK TO McCloud
- USE `Victim's husband` on McCloud

## 4. Simmons Residence

### 4.1. Street

- USE `License plate No.` on telephone
- USE `Missing clothing` on telephone
- USE `Victim's injuries` on telephone
- USE door
- USE `Gun` on door
- USE door

### 4.2. Kitchen

- USE note (on the kitchen countertop)
- EXIT east

### 4.3. Living Room

- LOOK AT (right-click) boots
- LOOK AT photo
- EXIT east

### 4.4. Bedroom

- USE make-up (on the small desk on the right side)
- LOOK AT box (on the small desk on the right side)

### 4.5. Kitchen

- TALK TO McCloud
- EXIT south

### 4.6. Street

- TALK TO nosy neighbour
- TALK TO McCloud
  - "Yes"
