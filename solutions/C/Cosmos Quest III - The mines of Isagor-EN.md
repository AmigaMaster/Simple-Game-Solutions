# Cosmos Quest III: The Mines of Isagor - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 05.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [itch.io](https://cosmosquest.itch.io/cosmos-quest-iii)

## 1. Shuttle

### 1.1. Passenger Cabin

- USE uniform
- EXIT left

### 1.2. Cockpit

- LOOK AT status screen
- **ATTENTION:** When you arrive at your destination without preparation, you will die.
- LOOK AT navigation system
- EXIT right

### 1.3. Passenger Cabin

- USE uniform (`Messenger`) (3/3)
- LOOK AT `Messenger` (5/8)
  - **NOTE:** Partial coordinates `4.75|3.20`

### 1.4. Cockpit

- USE navigation system
  - The screen is a coordinate system where the center is `0|0`
  - Search for `4.75|3.20` where 4.75 ist to the RIGHT and 3.20 UP and click on the small white star (10/18)
- USE speed controller (5/23)
- WAIT until you arrive - you can check the distance in the status screen (7/30)

## 2. Isagor

### 2.1. Landing Site

- PICK UP crowbar (from the barrel) (`Crowbar`) (4/34)
- EXIT west

### 2.2. Mountain Crossing

- EXIT north

### 2.3. Fingerprint Scanner

- LOOK AT device (on the rock in the middle) (5/39)
  - LOOK AT fingerprint scanner (4/43)
  - EXIT
- EXIT west

### 2.4. Mountain Crossing

- EXIT east

### 2.5. Crystal Tree

- PICK UP crystal tree fruit (`Opium fruit`) (6/49)
- EXIT southeast

### 2.6. Body Remains

- USE body remains (`Frozen finger`) (8/57)
- EXIT northwest, west, south

### 2.7. Landing Site

- USE `Frozen finger` on engine nozzle (`Warmed finger`) (7/64)
- EXIT west, north

### 2.8. Fingerprint Scanner

- LOOK AT device
  - USE `Warmed finger` on fingerprint scanner (10/74)
- EXIT south

### 2.9. Crystal Tree

- EXIT west

### 2.10. Metal Door

- ENTER door

#### 2.10.1. Bunker

##### 2.10.1.1. Entrance

- USE `Crowbar` on cabinet (`Keycard`, `Battery`) (10/84)
- LOOK AT cabinet
  - **NOTE:** The symbols
- USE `Keycard` on keycard slot (on the back wall) (4/88)
- EXIT downstairs

##### 2.10.1.2. Hallway

- EXIT east

##### 2.10.1.3. Elevator Room

- USE AC droid
  - ENTER the symbols from the cabinet
  - When we number the buttons from left to right and top to bottom from 1 to 12, press: `4 2 7 6 5 12` (7/95)
- WALK TO slide door
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE elevator's panel

##### 2.10.1.4. Tunnel

- **ATTENTION:** DO NOT MOVE and wait until you sneeze
- PICK UP plastic canteen (`Plastic canteen`) (5/100)
- PICK UP remote control (in the net) (`Remote control`) (7/107)
- ENTER tunnel

##### 2.10.1.5. Elevator Room

- EXIT west

##### 2.10.1.6. Hallway

- EXIT upstairs

##### 2.10.1.7. Entrance

- EXIT west

### 2.11. Metal Door

- USE `Plastic canteen` on water (`Plastic canteen with water`) (10/117)
- USE `Anti-allergic pill` on `Plastic canteen with water` (`Plastic canteen with anti-allergic solution`) (15/132)
- ENTER door and make your way to the tunnel

#### 2.11.1. Bunker

##### 2.11.1.1. Tunnel

- **ATTENTION:** DO NOT MOVE and wait until you sneeze
- WAIT for the man to disappear
- USE `Plastic canteen with anti-allergic solution` (`Plastic canteen`) (10/142)
- USE `Battery` on `Remote control` (8/150)
- USE `Remote control` on remote receiver (in the lower right corner of the screen) (12/162)

### 2.12. Mines

#### 2.12.1. Crane Room

- PICK UP ore (`Ore`) (3/165)
- USE `Ore` on panel (20/185)
- USE hole (in the barrels on the right)
- USE `Plastic canteen` on hole (`Canteen with oil`) (8/193)
- PICK UP weight ball (on the wall in the background) (`Weight ball`) (10/203)
- EXIT west

#### 2.12.2. Prison Cell

- USE `Mobile transporter` on Nia (20/223)
- TALK TO Nia (10/233)
  - **NOTE:** Rotated `C` and mirrored `L`
- EXIT northeast (into the room you hid before)

##### 2.12.2.1. Numpad

- We need a rotated `C` and mirrored `L`
- USE second row, first button (15/248)

#### 2.12.3. Prison Cell

- EXIT west

#### 2.12.4. Elevator Path

- ENTER elevator

#### 2.12.5. Light Collector Room

- USE `Crowbar` on `Weight ball` (`Crowbar with weight ball`) (20/268)
- USE `Crowbar with weight ball` on steel rope (right above you) (25/293)
- EXIT west

#### 2.12.6. Landing Platform

- USE `Canteen with oil` on feed-tank opening (10/303)
- USE spacecraft

## 3. Shuttle

- WAIT a few seconds

## 4. Zigobi Orbital Restaurant

### 4.1. Shuttle Bay

- WALK down the ramp
- EXIT east

### 4.2. Entrance

- TALK TO Zigi
- TALK TO Ada female
- LOOK AT servant droid (2/305)
- TALK TO servant droid (2/307)
- EXIT east

### 4.3. Passage

- EXIT southeast

### 4.4. Control Room

- LOOK AT panel
  - **NOTE:** All lights are green
  - EXIT
- PICK UP kitchen knives (`Kitchen knife`) (5/312)
- USE `Kitchen knife` on string (left from where the knife was) (`String`) (10/322)
- EXIT west und return here
- TALK TO Zigi (Chef)
- LOOK AT panel
  - One of the lights should be flashing red now
  - **NOTE:** If there is no flashing light, keep walking around in the restaurant and return here to check again.
  - USE button next to the flashing light (5/327)
  - EXIT
- EXIT west **2 times**

### 4.5. Entrance

- PICK UP Zinki coin (`Zinki coin`) (8/335)
- USE `String` on `Zinki coin` (`Zinki coin with string`) (12/347)
- USE `Zinki coin with string` on vending machine (`Translator`)
- TALK TO Ada female **4 times** (5/352)
- EXIT east

### 4.6. Passage

- TALK TO Barabat
- EXIT east

### 4.7. Control Room

- TALK TO Zigi (Chef)
  - **NOTE:** If he is not here, exit the restaurant, walk around some time and return to the control room until he appears
- USE `Advertising brochure` on Zigi (Chef) (`Membership card`) (10/362)

### 4.8. Passage

- USE `Membership card` on Barabat

### 4.9. Gambling Room

- USE `Kitchen knife` on `Opium fruit` (`Sliced opium fruit`) (6/368)
- USE `Sliced opium fruit` on opium fruits (10/378)
- USE paper folder (next to Harg-Xoval) (`Documents`) (10/388)
- USE Harg-Xoval (`Authorization card`) (15/403)
- EXIT west all the way

### 4.10. Shuttle Bay

- USE Hatch (17/420)
