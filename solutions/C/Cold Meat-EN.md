# Cold Meat - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 30.03.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/1210-cold-meat)

## 1. Fast Food Fortress Entrance

- PICK UP handbag (`Handbag`)
- EXIT west

## 2. Playground

- LOOK AT slide
- USE `Handbag` on edge of death (`Nail Scissors`, `Mints`, `Safety Pin`)
- EXIT east

## 3. Fast Food Fortress Entrance

- ENTER FFF (the hotspot is called "car")

### 3.1. FFF Right Entrance Area

- TALK TO Godnur
- TALK TO Foltnir
- TALK TO Jamal (`Filled Coke Cup`, `Marinade`, `Hot Sauce`)
- USE tie (on the counter)
- EXIT west

### 3.2. FFF Left Entrance Area

- TALK TO Rutnor
- PICK UP tray (from the holder on the left) (`Tray`)
- EXIT upstairs

### 3.3. FFF Upper Level

- TALK TO reporter
- EXIT south, east, south, west

## 4. Playground

- USE `Marinade` with sand (`Sandy Marinade`)

## 5. Fast Food Fortress Entrance

### 5.1. FFF Right Entrance Area

    - Lost his beard
    - was wearing woman’s clothes
    - skillful and strong

    - there was always a lot going on
  - Nektom:
    - Was the strongest
    - terrifying warrior
    - got things on his head quite often

    - smelt strange
