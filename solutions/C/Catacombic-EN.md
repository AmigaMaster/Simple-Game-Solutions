# Catacombic - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 06.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/catacombic)

## 1. Camp

### 1.1. Prison

- USE `Key` on door
- PICK UP lamp (next to the door) (`Empty Oil Lamp`)
- USE `Empty Oil Lamp` on barrels (`Full Oil Lamp`)
- EXIT north

## 2. Camp

- USE door to ENTER right house

### 2.1. Sleeping Quarters

- PICK UP teapot (on the crates at the bottom left) (`Teapot with cold water`)
- USE bag (between the beds) (`Small Key`)
- USE `Teapot with cold water` on stove
- PICK UP teapot (`Teapot with warm water`)
- USE `Small Key` on small box (next to the right window) (`Dagger`, `Book`)
- EXIT south

## 3. Camp

- EXIT east

## 4. Bridge

- EXIT west

## 5. Camp

- PICK UP wooden plank (on the ground in front of the left house) (`Plank`)

## 6. Bridge

- USE Plank on bridge
- WALK east up to a mine entrance
- USE `Dagger` on mine entrance
- USE `Teapot with warm water` on mine entrance
- ENTER mine

### 6.1. Mine Entrance

- USE `Full Oil Lamp` on cart
- USE cart

### 6.2. Mine

- USE pile of rubble (on the right) (`Pebbles`)
- USE ladder
- USE door (on the left)
  - Place the pebbles as shown on the left
  - The numbers next to the grid are the number of pebbles found in that row or column
  - There are three versions of the riddle:

### 6.3. Crystal Room

- WALK to the lower right
- USE left light
- WALK to upper right
- USE light
- WALK to lower left
- USE right light
- WALK to the next island on the right
- USE light
- WALK right
- USE light
- EXIT northeast into the tunnel

### 6.4. Lair of Evil

- LOOK AT `Book`
  - **NOTE:** The last four lines
- USE `Book` on one of the men
- "SHUNU..."
- "Tirrama..."
- "Shupi..."
- "ZI DINGER..."
- USE `Full Oil Lamp` on tree
