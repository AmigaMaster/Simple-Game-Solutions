## 1. Achievements

- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Dedicated or Insane
  >- Playing time exceeds 3 hours.
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Cometh the Hour
  >- Total playtime exceeding one hour.
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Speedster
  >- Complete the game in under half an hour.

## 2. Western Moon

- LOOK AT Camper Van
  - PICK UP Duct Tape (`Duct Tape`)
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Explorer (1/4)
  >- Walk northwest
  >- Visit all four corners of the moon.
- USE Roddenberry Bush (`Roddenberry`)
- LOOK AT **all** Roddenberry bushes
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Vegetation Appreciation (1-6/10)
  >- Look at 10 Roddenberry Bushes
- LOOK AT Stargate
- LOOK AT Stargate Controls
  - EXIT
- WALK east
- LOOK AT Ticket Machine
- LOOK AT Monolith
- LOOK AT Train
- LOOK AT Mountain Range
- WALK southwest
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Potholer (1/3)
  >- WALK into the oval hole between and just above the Totem pole and the Giant Monkey Head
  >- Disappear into three holes in the ground.
- LOOK AT Giant Monkey Head
- LOOK AT Totem Pole
- USE wastepaper bin (the blue mug) (`Ticket (Punched)`)
- WALK southeast
- LOOK AT Wall
- LOOK AT Brick (in the wall)
- PICK UP food (`Greens`, `Pudding`)
- USE Shipwreck (`Sealant Spray`, `Hand Phaser`)
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Overly Aggressive
  >- Try to use the phaser or enhanced phaser on 10 or more objects
- USE `Sealant Spray` with `Ticket(Punched)` (`Ticket (Like New!)`)
- WALK east
- LOOK AT Ice
  - **NOTE:** The ice cube in the middle of the ice
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- WALK exactly north our south of the ice cube and WALK onto the ice
  - You slide over the ice and push the ice cube from the ice
- USE `Hand Phaser` on ice cube (`Small Flying Saucer`)
- LOOK AT Hotel
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Explorer (2/4)
  >- Walk southwest
  >- Visit all four corners of the moon.
- WALK north
- USE `Ticket (Like New!)` with Monolith
- USE train
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Commuter
  >- Travel on the train at least 10 times.

## 4. Western Moon

- WALK west
- USE Camper van
  - USE `Enhancement Lens` with `Hand Phaser` (`Enhanced Phaser`)
  - USE `Enhanced Phaser` with toolbox (`Hand Phaser`)
  - USE toolbox (`Crowbar`, `Jump Leads`)
  - EXIT

## 5. Eastern Moon

- WALK southeast
- USE `Crowbar` with crate (south of the Battle Site)
- USE crate (`Hoverboard`)
- **ATTENTION:** If you want to get the Pedestrian achievement, you must not use the hoverboard
  - USE `Hoverboard`
  - FLY west (across the mountains)

## 6. Western Moon

- USE Stargate Controls
  - USE `Crowbar` on panel
  - PICK UP dull crystal (`Dull Crystal`)
  - USE `Jump Leads` with `Dull Crystal` (`Crystal & Jump Leads`)
  - EXIT
- USE `Crystal & Jump Leads` with `Small Flying Saucer` (`Active Flying Saucer`)
- USE `Active Flying Saucer` on `Duct Tape` (`Taped Flying Saucer`)
- USE `Taped Flying Saucer` with `Bjork Hand` (`Flying Hand`)
- WALK southwest
- USE Hotel Entrance

## 10. Western Moon

- USE Stargate Controls
  - USE `Crystal` with Open Panel
  - LOOK AT `Stargate Manual`
    - Page 79
    - You see a Stargate with 4 Apple Logos, 4 Commodore Logos, 4 Windows Logos and 4 Atari Logos
    - EXIT manual
  - USE left and right mouse button to change the logos
  - USE activation button, the panels change to the last dialed code
  - LOOK AT `Stargate Manual`
    - Page 99
    - Look which symbols you need to swap
      - 1  <-> 10
      - 2  <-> 14
      - 3  <-> 7
      - 4  <-> 6
      - 5  <-> 11
      - 8  <-> 9
      - 12 <-> 16
      - 13 <-> 15
      - The final code changes in each playthrough
      - USE activation button
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Finisher
  >- Complete the game
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Pedestrian
  >- Complete the game without using the hoverboard.
