# 2080 Wall City Lockdown - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 03.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/2731)

## 1. Alley

### 1.1. Apartment

- EXIT east
- USE control panel (next to the window)
- USE toolbox (`Screwdriver`)
- USE `Screwdriver` on control panel
- USE control panel
- USE `Smartphone` on control panel
- EXIT east

## 2. Act I: Empty Pockets

### 2.1. Alley

- TALK TO Ethan
- USE box (bottom right) (`Bottle Cap`)
- LOOK AT `Bottle Cap`
- EXIT alley

### 2.2. Workshop

- PICK UP toy car (`Broken Toy Car`)
- LOOK AT `Broken Toy Car`
- TALK TO Tim
- EXIT west

### 2.3. Bazaar

- TALK TO Amy (`IceBall Chewing Gum`)
- USE bowl (in the lower right) (`Spring`)
- TALK TO Morgan (`Batteries`)
- USE `IceBall Chewing Gum` (`Chewing Gum`)
- USE `Chewing Gum` on `Bottle Cap` (`Bottle Cap with Gum`)
- USE `Bottle Cap with Gum` on `Broken Toy Car` (`Repaired Toy Car`)
- EXIT north

### 2.4. Underpass

- TALK TO old beggar (Lucius)
- USE `Repaired Toy Car` on old beggar
- LOOK AT small bottle (on the table in the background)
- USE small bottle (`Sedatives`)
- LOOK AT can (on the left table)
- USE can (`Can of Condensed Milk`)
- TALK TO old beggar
  - Talk about a deal
- TALK TO old beggar **again**
  - **NOTE:** Order of techniques: aggressive, aggressive, neutral, aggressive, sensitive
- EXIT east

### 2.5. Workshop

- TALK TO Tim
  - "I have some thoughts to share."
  - "I will never surrender."
  - "I will move mountains..."
  - "An emotionless approach..."
  - "I will get you one booster..."
  - "I understand that you really need it." (`Radiolon X-3000 Battery`)

## 3. Act II: Freedom Fighters

### 3.1. Alley

- USE `Radiolon X-3000 Battery` on Ethan (`Money`)
- TALK TO barman
  - "I have money..."
  - Always select the first answer
- USE `Money` on money slot (of the vending machine on the right)

### 3.2. Bazaar

- TALK TO Amy
  - "Can I buy the last issue of Glamorous Past?" (`Bills`, `Coins`, `Magazine`, `Leaflet`)
- LOOK AT `Leaflet`

### 3.3. Alley

- USE `Magazine` on barman (`Duct Tape`)
- USE `Coins` on money slot (`Spitzz Cola`)

### 3.4. Train Station

- TALK TO Ray
- LOOK AT door (of the railcar)
- EXIT east (on the right side of the bikes)

### 3.5. Gossip Corner

- TALK TO Ron
- TALK TO Boy
- EXIT east

### 3.6. Bazaar

- TALK TO Morgan
  - "Are you selling that cute robot?" (`Toy Robot`)

### 3.7. Gossip Corner

- USE `Toy Robot` on Boy
- USE toy gun (`Toy Gun`)
- USE bricks (from the pile on the left) (`Brick`)

### 3.8. Parking

- TALK TO Don
- USE Hanger (on the semi truck on the right) (`Hanger`)
- EXIT west

### 3.9. Train Station

- USE `Brick` on door
- USE `Hanger` on door
- EXIT west

### 3.10. Dock

- TALK TO Wombat
- EXIT east

### 3.11. Parking

- TALK TO Don
  - "Do you have your Velvet Crew badge?"
- LOOK AT cabinet and shirt (in the background, left of Don)
- USE `Toy Gun` on tin can (on top of the semi-truck) (`Velvet Crew Badge`)

### 3.12. Dock

- USE `Velvet Crew Badge` on Wombat
- GO TO end of wharf (on the north east)
- USE box
- GO BACK (to the north east)

### 3.13. Train Station

- TALK TO tough woman (Fiona)
- USE `Can of Condensed Milk` on Fiona (`Cheese`)

### 3.14. Dock

- GO TO end of wharf
- USE `Cheese` on box (`Small Key`)
- TALK TO Wombat
  - "I have the little key."

### 3.15. Backyard

- USE `Sedatives` on bowl
- USE `Small Key` on cabinet (`Envelope`, `Token`)

### 3.16. Dock

- TALK TO Wombat
  - "I have the envelope."

### 3.17. Train Station

- TALK TO Ray
  - "The tide is high..."

### 3.18. V Street

- TALK TO suspicious guy **until he lets you to the boss**

### 3.19. Lookout

- TALK TO Gillian
- EXIT east

### 3.20. V Street

- TALK TO Matty
  - "I need you help..."

### 3.21. Building

- USE `Leaflet` on logo (above the doors)
- EXIT east

### 3.22. Underpass

- USE `Leaflet` on old beggar
- TALK TO old beggar

### 3.23. Building

- USE `Token` on coffee dispenser (`Coffee`)

### 3.24. Underpass

- USE `Coffee` on old beggar (`Lucius Card`)

### 3.25. Building

- USE `Lucius Card` on card reader (next to the doors)

#### 3.25.1. Inside Building

- USE `Lucius Card` on card slot
- USE screen
- USE down button
- EXIT west

### 3.26. Building

- LOOK AT oil leak (left of the coffee machine)
- USE `Duct Tape` on oil leak
- USE `Lucius Card` on card reader

#### 3.26.1. Inside Building

- USE down button
- EXIT west

### 3.27. Building

- USE `Spitzz Cola` on lid
- USE lid

### 3.29. Workshop

## 4. Act III: Armaggeddon't

### 4.1. Refinery

- TALK TO Woman in Uniform
- EXIT north

### 4.2. Bazaar

- TALK TO Morgan (`Signal Receiver`)

### 4.3. Building

- USE `Screwdriver` on maintenance hatch (of the coffee machine) (`Circuit Board`)

### 4.4. V Street

- TALK TO Matty (`Explosive C4G`)

### 4.5. Workshop

- USE wires (under the car) (`Wires`)

### 4.6. Refinery

- TALK TO Dayneh
  - "I have the signal receiver."
  - "I have the circuit board."
  - "I have the C4"
- USE `Wires` on Dayneh
- TALK TO Dayneh
  - "Do you have all the parts..." (`Small Explosive Charges`)
- USE `Small Explosive Charges` on Ventilation Shaft (on the right)
- USE ventilation shaft

### 4.7. Tunnel Exit

- USE pile of gravel (`Pebbles`)
- USE funnel (at the power rooters in the lower left) (`Funnel`)
- EXIT west

### 4.8. Street across Park

- TALK TO nervous guy (`Disruptor`)
- LOOK AT clue cup
- USE blue cup (`Blue Cup`)
- USE can (in the hedge in the center) (`Empty Can`)
- LOOK AT lasers
- EXIT east

### 4.9. Tunnel Exit

- USE `Disruptor` on robot (`Drill`)

### 4.10. Street across Park

- USE `Drill` on electric box (next to the lasers)
- USE `Funnel` on electric box
- USE `Blue Cup` on electric box
- EXIT northwest

### 4.11. Building Entrance

- USE `Pebbles` on `Empty Can` (`Can with pebbles`)
- USE `Duct Tape` on `Can with pebbles` (`Sealed full can`)
- USE `Sealed full can` on bushes (on the right beside the checkpoint)
- USE plastic tubes (`Short Tube`)
- EXIT west

### 4.12. Construction Site

- USE `Short Tube` on guard dog (in the center)
- USE boards (left of where the dog was)
- EXIT west

### 4.13. Observatory

- TALK TO scientist
- EXIT east

### 4.14. Street across Park

  - "Do you have any spare clothes?"
- EXIT southwest

### 4.15. Airport
