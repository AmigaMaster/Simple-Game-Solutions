## 1. Game

- USE door (to the tower)
  - "Headbutt door."
- USE kite
  - "Untie..."
  - "Bite..." (`Kite`)
- LOOK AT `Kite` (`Note`)
- LOOK AT `Note`
- USE headstone
  - "Take..."
  - "Headbutt..."
- PICK UP feather (`Feather`)
- TALK TO vampire
- USE hat
  - "Cast..."
  - "Leporidae"
  - "Actus"
  - "Apparoir"
  - "Hottr"
- USE `Feather` on vampire
- TALK TO vampire (until he leaves)
- PICK UP rose (`Rose`)
- PICK UP record (`"Happy Leg Jamboree"`)
- USE piggy (at the tower on the lamp)
  - "Take..." **3 times**
- PICK UP coin (`Coin`)
- USE `Rose` on headstone
- TALK TO ghost
- USE `Coin` on ghost
- USE cart
  - "Take..."
  - "Headbutt..."
- PICK UP record (`"You Can Bank on it"`)
- USE gramophone
  - "Stop..."
  - "Remove..." (`"Heartbreak Toast"`)
- USE `"You Can Bank on it"` on gramophone
- USE gramophone
  - "Wind..."
- TALK TO note (under the headstone) (`Receipt`)
- USE `Receipt` on ghost
- WALK to the right side of the balloon
- USE balloon
  - "Talk..."
  - "Please..."
  - "RAAAAAAA..." (it moves to the left over the headstone)
- USE headstone
  - "Headbutt..."
- WALK to the left side of the balloon
- USE balloon
  - "Talk..."
  - "RAAAAAAA..." **4 times** (it moves to the right over the hat)
- USE hat
  - "Cast..."
- PICK UP balloon (`Key`, `Balloon`)
- USE `Key` on trapdoor
- USE trapdoor (`"June Tripping"`, `"Stuck in 3 Ruts"`)
- USE gramophone
  - "Stop..."
  - "Remove..." (`"You Can Bank on it"`)
- USE `"Stuck in 3 Ruts"` on gramophone
- USE gramophone
  - "Wind..."
  - "Try winding..."
  - "Try forcing..." (`Handle`)
- USE `Handle` on box
- TALK TO Jack
  - TALK about everything
  - "Impersonation?"
  - "Lady Asphalt"
  - "Worst impersonation ever!"
  - "You have witch hair!" (`Hair`)
- USE `Hair` on `Balloon` (`Balloon & Hair`)
- USE `Balloon & Hair` on witch
