# What Makes You Tick - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 29.03.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/What_Makes_You_Tick)

## 1. Bridge

- USE handle (from the well)
- PICK UP handle (`Handle`)
- OPEN lantern
- USE `Lighter` with open lantern
- PICK UP shiny object (in the box right of the lantern) (`Muddy Thing`)
- EXIT northeast

## 2. Mill

- PICK UP flag (`Piece of Cloth`)
- PICK UP stick (`Long Stick`)
- EXIT southwest

## 3. Wheat Field

- LIFT stone (next to the barrel on the ground)
- PICK UP worm (`Wiggly Worm`)
- PICK UP cocktail shaker (on the barrel) (`Cocktail-Shaker`)
- PICK UP leather glove (on the bench) (`Single Glove`)
- PICK UP wheat from field of wheat (`Handful of Wheat`)
- TALK TO reading stranger
- EXIT east **2 times**

## 4. Bridge

- TALK TO stranger (`Can o' Dr Pepper`)

## 5. Wheat Field

- EXIT north

## 6. Sea

- TALK TO sailor
- PICK UP book (below the crate in the bottom) (`Captn Matt's Guide to Life`)
- PICK UP bottles (`Corked Bottle of Alcohol`)
- USE `Captn Matt's Guide to Life`
- OPEN `Can o' Dr Pepper` (`Open Dr. Pepper`)
- USE `Open Dr. Pepper` (`Empty Can`)
- USE `Empty Can` with hermit crab
- PICK UP old glove (`Glove`)
- USE `Glove` with `Single Glove` (`Pair of Gloves`)
- LOOK AT leaflet (`Leaflet`, `Hook`)
- EXIT southwest

## 7. Wheat Field

- USE `Pair of Gloves` with barbed wire fence (`Barbed Wire`)
- EXIT east

## 8. Mill

- USE `Barbed Wire` with iron gate
- EXIT north

## 9. Sun Clock

- PICK UP piece of string (in the small tree) (`String`)
- EXIT northeast

## 10. Sea

- USE `String` with `Long Stick` (`Stick with String`)
- USE `Hook` with `Stick with String` (`Stick with Hook on String`)
- BITE `Corked Bottle of Alcohol` (`Full Bottle of Alcohol`, `Cork`)
- USE `Cork` with `Stick with Hook on String` (`Fishing-Rod`)
- USE `Wiggly Worm` with `Fishing-Rod` (`Fishing-Rod with Bait`)
- USE `Fishing-Rod with Bait` with sea (`Fresh Fish`)
- USE `Fresh Fish` with old dog
- USE `Captn Matt's Guide to Life` with old dog
- PICK UP mushroom (`Seta de Muerte (not deadly)`)

## 11. Bridge

- USE `Full Bottle of Alcohol` with `Cocktail-Shaker` (`Half a Bottle of Alcohol`, `Cocktail-Shaker (Alcohol)`)
- USE `Handful of Wheat` with `Cocktail-Shaker (Alcohol)` (`Cocktail-Shaker (Alcohol & Wheat)`)
- USE `Seta de Muerte (not deadly)` with `Cocktail-Shaker (Alcohol & Wheat)` (`Cocktail-Shaker (Alcohol, Wheat & Mushroom)`)
- USE `Cocktail-Shaker (Alcohol, Wheat & Mushroom)` (`Sedative-Seeds`)
- USE `Sedative-Seeds` with crow **3 times**

## 12. Sun Clock

- PICK UP key from sleeping crow (`Key Part`)

## 13. Wheat Field

- TALK TO Nora
- PICK UP key? (`Tiny Key Part`)
- USE `Tiny Key Part` with `Key Part` (`Larger Part of a Key`)

## 14. Mill

- USE `Larger Part of a Key` with door

## 15. Sea

- TALK TO Nora
- TALK TO sailor

## 16. Wheat Field

- TALK TO old man

## 17. Mill

- PULL door-mat
- PICK UP key? (`Small Key Part`)
- USE `Small Key Part` with `Larger Part of a Key` (`Large Key`)
- USE `Large Key` with door
- ENTER strange house

### 17.1. Study

- USE `Piece of Cloth` with `Half a Bottle of Alcohol` (`Wet Cloth`, `Almost empty Bottle of Alcohol`)
- USE `Wet Cloth` with `Muddy Thing` (`'Death'-Plate`)
- USE `'Death'-Plate` with ovalshaped holes (at the golden bird figure)
- LOOK AT interesting blue book (on the left)
- PICK UP interesting blue book (`The Inside Matters`)
- OPEN `The Inside Matters` (`'Adventurer'-Plate`)
- USE `'Adventurer'-Plate` with with ovalshaped holes
- EXIT downstairs

#### 17.1.1. Hidden Room

- USE binoculars
- LOOK AT note (on the board on your right)
- LOOK AT larger note (on the same board)
- LOOK AT map
- LOOK AT photograph
- TOUCH photograph
- USE `Handle` with hole
- USE handle
- EXIT east

#### 17.1.2. Laboratory

- TALK TO Dr. Coppelius
- LOOK AT old photograph (on the desk)
- EXIT west

#### 17.1.3. Hidden Room

- TALK TO crow **2 times**
- OPEN cupboard
- TAKE things from cupboard

#### 17.1.4. Laboratory

- USE `Lighter` with `Leaflet` (`Burning Leaflet`)
- USE `Burning Leaflet` with tank? (in the cylindershaped tank)
- USE `Wet Cloth` with tank?
- USE `Almost empty Bottle of Alcohol` with tank?
- USE cylindershaped pod

### 17.2. Study

- PICK UP plates (`'Death'-Plate`, `'Adventurer'-Plate`)
- USE `'Adventurer'-Plate` with ovalshaped holes
- USE `'Death'-Plate` with ovalshaped holes
- EXIT downstairs
