## 1. Corridor

### 1.1. Bedroom

- USE light switch
- PICK UP transistor radio (from the crate on the left) (`Transistor Radio`)
- OPEN `Transistor Radio` (`Batteries`)
- USE `Batteries` on `Flashlight`
- USE `Flashlight`

## 2. Corridor

- OPEN kitchen door

### 2.1. Kitchen

- OPEN drawers (beside the oven) (`Fuse Wire`)

## 3. Corridor

### 3.1. Laundry Room

- OPEN fuse box
- USE `Fuse Wire` on switches and fuses
- CLICK ON flashlight off button
