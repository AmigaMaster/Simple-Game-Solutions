# Jimmy The Squatter - Episode 1 - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 31.12.2025 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

## 1. Station

- LOOK AT telephone (`Coin`)
- TALK TO stranger
  - "Hi, Jimmy is the name..."
- GIVE `Coin` to stranger

## 2. Street

- EXIT north

### 2.1. Old House Entrance

- EXIT west

### 2.2. Left Side of House

- PICK UP dirty rag (`Dirty Rag`)
- USE `Dirty Rag` on dirty window
- LOOK AT dirty window
- EXIT west

### 2.3. Backdoor

- EXIT west

### 2.4. Right Side of House

- OPEN (left) rubbish bin (`Coathanger`)
- LOOK AT vines
- EXIT east

### 2.5. Backdoor

- EXIT south

### 2.6. Backgarden

- PICK UP garden shears (`Old Rusty Shears`)
- USE `Dirty Rag` on garden shed window
- LOOK AT garden shed window
- EXIT west

### 2.7. Driveway

- PULL `Coathanger` (`Hook`)
- USE `Hook` on car door
- OPEN car door
- PICK UP WD40 (`WD40`)
- USE `WD40` on `Old Rusty Shears` (`Old Garden Shears`)
- EXIT east

### 2.8. Backgarden

- EXIT east

### 2.9. Backdoor

- EXIT west

### 2.10. Right Side of House

- USE `Old Garden Shears` on vines
- USE wooden lattice
