# Jimmy the Troublemaker - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 01.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/jimmy-the-troublemaker)

## 1. Grampa's Attic

- USE desk
- USE lamp
- USE desk **2 times** (`Key`)
- USE `Key` on chest
- USE chest (`Magic book`)
- USE `Magic Book` on yourself (without hotspot text)

## 2. Barn

- PICK UP shovel (`Shovel`)
- USE `Shovel` on ground (from the bottom right corner of the barn door, move down in a straight line to find this hotspot)
- USE hole in ground
- EXIT south

## 3. Barn Entrance

- PICK UP toadstool (`Toadstool`)
- EXIT west

## 4. Witch's House

- USE door
- ENTER house

### 4.1. Livingroom

- EXIT upstairs

### 4.2. Bedroom

- TALK TO witch **3 times**
- EXIT north

### 4.3. Livingroom

- EXIT east

## 5. Witch's House

- EXIT south

## 6. Barn Entrance

- EXIT east

## 7. Before Bridge

- EXIT east

## 8. Prison

### 8.1. Cell

- PICK UP jar (`Jar`)
- USE `Jar` on yourself
- PICK UP skull (`Skull`)
- TALK TO door
- USE `Skull` on stupid guard

## 9. Prison

- PICK UP knife (`Knife`)
- PICK UP bone (`Bone`)
- USE `Knife` on `Bone` (`Sharp Bone`)
- USE `Sharp Bone` on padlock (`Keyring`)
- PICK UP bottle (`Bottle`)
- USE `Bottle` on wine barrel (`Bottle with vine`)
- USE `Bottle with vine` on middle barrel
- USE loose stone (a little bit below and right of blue glass on the right shelf)
- USE `Keyring` on trapdoor

## 10. Eastern Cave

- EXIT southwest

## 11. Western Cave

- LOOK AT hole
- EXIT east

## 12. Eastern Cave

- USE ladder

## 13. Prison

- PICK UP board (`Board`)

## 14. Western Cave

- USE `Board` on hole
- EXIT northeast

## 15. Tree with Door

- EXIT south

## 16. Forest Crossing

- EXIT west

## 17. Cabin

- LOOK AT door
- LOOK AT hole (next to the door)
- PICK UP grass (it moves - between tree and house) (`Grass`)
- EXIT east, northeast

## 18. Cemetery

- WALK north
- EXIT northeast

### 18.1. Gnome Cemetery

- PICK UP leaves (next to the big tree) (`Leaves`)
- USE bush (on the right side) (`Nut`)
- EXIT south **2 times**

## 19. Forest Crossing

- USE `Shovel` on ground (a little bit left from the middle stone)
- USE `Leaves` on hole
- USE `Grass` on hole in ground
- USE `Leaves` on hole in ground
- USE `Nut` on hole in ground
- WALK behind tree
- USE hole in ground (`Angry gnome`)

## 20. Cabin

- USE `Angry gnome` on hole

### 20.1. Inside Cabin

- WALK under bed (`Key`)
- USE `Key` on cabinet
- PICK UP gold coins (`Goldcoins`)
- EXIT through hole

## 21. Cabin

- EXIT east

## 22. Forest Crossing

- EXIT southeast

## 23. Gnome Village

- EXIT west **3 times**

## 24. Western Forest Crossing

- EXIT west

## 25. Forest Path

## 26. Western Forest Crossing

## 27. House

- **QUICK:**
  - EXIT west - you need to exit before the bee exits

## 28. Village

- **QUICK:**
  - HIDE behind the house with the red roof
  - **NOTE:** The password `Misery`
  - **NOTE:** If you were too slow, WALK to the forest crossing and then north to the tree with the door, then follow again
- USE (left) door

### 28.1. Shop

- TALK TO shopkeeper
- EXIT shop

## 29. Village

- EXIT north

### 29.1. Broken House

## 42. Forest Entrance

## 43. Forest

- EXIT east **2 times**, south, east

### 43.1. Campsite

- TALK TO Charlie
- EXIT west **3 times**

### 43.2. Medora Village

- WALK north
- USE (left) door

### 43.3. Charlie's House

#### 43.3.1. Livingroom

- USE cabinet
- EXIT northwest

#### 43.3.2. Bedroom

- USE jewelry box **2 times** (`Small Key`)
- USE nightstand **2 times** (`Cassette`)
- USE `Small Key` on diary
- LOOK AT open diary
  - **NOTE:** combination `732`
- EXIT west

#### 43.3.3. Livingroom

- USE desk
  - ENTER combination from the diary `732` (`Taperecorder`)
- EXIT east

### 43.4. Medora Village

- USE door (from the right house in the background)

#### 43.4.1. House

- PICK UP tape (from the lower right corner of the poster) (`Piece of tape`)
- USE `Piece of tape` on `Cassette` (`Recordable cassette`)
- USE `Recordable cassette` on `Tape recorder`
- EXIT east

### 43.5. Medora Village

- EXIT south **2 times**, east

### 43.6. Troll Bridge

- USE `Taperecorder` on ugly troll
- EXIT west

### 43.7. Small House

- USE small house
- EXIT west

### 43.8. Gate

- USE `Gold ring` on hollow (under Bob)
- WALK north (so you are behind the tree)
- EXIT north (behind the fir tree), west

### 43.9. Grave

- USE `Shovel` on ground (in front of the tombstone)
- USE `Taperecorder` on Wizard's tomb
- USE `Shovel` on ground
- USE hole (`Wizard book`)

## 44. Witch's House

## 45. Forest Entrance

## 46. Forest

- EXIT east **2 times**, south, west, south, east

### 46.1. Troll Bridge

- USE `Spell` on ugly troll
- EXIT east

### 46.2. Cave Entrance

- ENTER cave

#### 46.2.1. Cave Entrance Area

- EXIT northwest

#### 46.2.2. Cave Path to the North

- EXIT north

#### 46.2.3. Big Cave

- EXIT northeast (you need to follow the path on the left side until at the back wall)

#### 46.2.4. Cave with Tracks

- EXIT east

#### 46.2.5. Cave with Ladder

- USE ladder

### 46.3. Castle

#### 46.3.1. Torture Chamber

- EXIT west

#### 46.3.2. Basement

- PICK UP flower pot (`Flower pot`)
- PICK UP key (`Castle key`)
- EXIT upstairs

#### 46.3.3. Staircase

- WALK TO painting

##### 46.3.3.1. Beach

- PICK UP flag (from the sand castle) (`Small flag`)
- USE `Small flag` on clam
- PICK UP clam (`Pearl`)
- PICK UP exotic drink (`Exotic drink`)
- TALK TO Professor Brown
- EXIT east, south

##### 46.3.3.2. Fairytaleish House

- ENTER house

###### 46.3.3.2.1. Brown's House

- TALK TO Professor Brown
- PICK UP batteries (`Batteries`)
- USE drawer **2 times** (`Vacuumcleaner`)
- PICK UP hollow stick (at the fireplace) (`Hollow stick`)
- TALK TO Professor Brown
- EXIT north

##### 46.3.3.3. Fairytaleish House

- EXIT east

##### 46.3.3.4. Totem Crossing

- EXIT south

##### 46.3.3.5. Village

- WALK west
  - WALK towards the ghost in small steps - in the first round, you can try to walk a bigger way until the black tree
- EXIT west

##### 46.3.3.6. Totempole

- PICK UP tooth (`Tooth`)
- EXIT east **2 times**

##### 46.3.3.7. Totem Crossing

- USE `Tooth` on `Exotic drink` (`Sticky tooth`)
- USE `Sticky tooth` on totempole (`Pipe`)
- LOOK AT `Pipe`
- EXIT east

##### 46.3.3.8. Lake

- EXIT east

##### 46.3.3.9. Quiz Lady

- TALK TO Quizlady
  - "Hey! I want to try the quiz"
  - "Light brown"
  - "Red"
  - "3"
  - "11"
  - Choose any answer (`Seeds`)
- EXIT east

##### 46.3.3.10. Tree

- USE `Hollow stick` on golden fruit **3 times**
- PICK UP golden apple
- USE `Batteries` on `Vacuumcleaner`
- USE `Vacuumcleaner` on golden apple (`Golden apple`)
- EXIT west **4 times**

##### 46.3.3.11. Fairytaleish House

###### 46.3.3.11.1. Brown's House

- USE `Golden apple` on bucket
- USE `Pipe` on bucket
- USE `Pearl` on bucket

##### 46.3.3.12. Fairytaleish House

- TRY to enter the house
- WAIT some seconds
- ENTER house

###### 46.3.3.12.1. Brown's House

- USE painting

#### 46.3.4. Staircase

- EXIT upstairs

#### 46.3.5. Bedroom

- USE `Castle key` on chest
- USE chest (`Magic mirror`)
- USE nightstand **2 times**
- USE jar (on the other nightstand) **2 times**
- ENTER wardrobe
