## 1. Walkthrough

- OPEN garbage bin
- PICK UP garbage bag (`Garbage Bag`)
- OPEN `Garbage Bag`
- PICK UP business card (`Business Card`)
- PICK UP die (`Die`)
- LOOK AT rubbish (`Broken Screw`)
- LOOK AT `Business Card`
  - **NOTE:** `9571 7613`
- TALK TO window (in the background) **2 times**
- USE `Die` with `Broken Screw` (`Do-It-Yourself Corkscrew`)
- USE `Do-It-Yourself Corkscrew` with wood knot (in the fence)
- PULL corkscrew in wood knot (`Do-It-Yourself Corkscrew`)
- LOOK AT peephole
- TALK TO window
  - "Garry. Your name is Garry!"
  - "Call 9571 7613"
