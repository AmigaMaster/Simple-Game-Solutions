## 1. Cave

- TALK TO Luigi
- USE door to the truck (`Bandages`)
- TALK TO Regina
- USE entrance of the lower right tent

### 1.1. Tent

- USE `Bandages` on Anna
- TALK TO Anna
- PICK UP wire **2 times** (`Lightbulb`, `Wire`)
- USE `Wire` on `Lightbulb` (`Lightbulb with wire`)
- EXIT tent

## 2. Cave

- USE `Lightbulb with wire` on Luigi
