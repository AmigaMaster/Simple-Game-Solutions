# Bog's Adventure In Easy3D - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------
|   1.0   | 21.02.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/bogs-adventure-in-easy3d)

## 1. House

- EXIT east

## 2. Outside

- WALK southeast
- PICK UP flower (`Flower`)
- WALK to Clod
- USE `Flower` on Clod (`Thorny Stick`)
- LOOK AT `Thorny Stick` (`Blood`)
- TALK TO Clod
- WALK to cottage
- USE door
- USE cottage

### 2.1. Cottage

- PICK UP stone (`Stone`)
- PICK UP spoon (`Spoon`)
- **NOTE:**
  - The letters on the walls: (G)reen (T), (B)lue (H), (R)ed (I)
- TALK TO chest
  - ENTER `bright`
- USE chest (`Magic Book`)
- LOOK AT `Magic Book`
- EXIT south

## 3. Outside

- LOOK AT note (in front of the cottage)
- WALK west
- USE stone in the river to cross
- FOLLOW the path
- PICK UP cup (`Magic Blue Cup`)
- WALK back to Clod
- USE `Magic Blue Cup` on water (`Cup with Water`)
- WALK south where you found the flower
- USE tower

### 3.1. Tower

- CLIMB UP

#### 3.1.1. Tower Top

- USE `Spoon` on clock
- EXIT tower

## 4. Outside

### 4.1. Cottage

- TALK TO wizard
- USE `Thorny Stick` on wizard (`Flame`)
- EXIT cottage

## 5. Outside

- WALK around the past to the right and then to the left to the rocks
- LOOK AT rocks
- TALK TO rocks
- USE `Blood` on `Cup with Water` (`Cup with Water and Blood`)
- USE `Stone` on `Cup with Water and Blood` (`Cup with Water, Blood and Stone`)
- USE `Flame` on `Cup with Water, Blood and Stone` (`Cup with something bubbling`)
- USE `Cup with something bubbling` on rocks
