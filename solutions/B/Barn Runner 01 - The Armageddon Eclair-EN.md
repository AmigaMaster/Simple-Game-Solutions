# Barn Runner 01 - The Armageddon Eclair - Walkthrough

| Version | Datum      | Autor                                     |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 18.05.2024 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the free game from the [Homepage](https://www.barnrunner.com/barn1.html)

## 1. Part One

### 1.1. Food Court

- WALK southwest to the green shop

#### 1.1.1. World of Byproducts

- TAKE orange cube from the tray (`Beef flavoured Jello`)
- EXIT view

### 1.2. Food Court

- WALK northwest to the orange shop

#### 1.2.1. Stuff on a Stick

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- REPAIR (use multi-tool ICON) robot
- TAKE free cheese sample from the tray (`Piece of cheese`, `Toothpick`)
- TALK TO girl
  - "I'm looking for a missing robot."
  - "Mind if I look at your robot's files?"
  - "Can I speak to your assistant manager? I'm a cop."

#### 1.2.2. Manager's Office

- TALK TO manager
  - "Had any problems with your robot lately?"
  - "You didn't answer my question."
  - "I need to look at your robot's monthly download files." (`Blue disk`)
- USE `Blue disk` with `Data link`
  - **NOTE:** Code `8008135`
- USE buttons next to the door on the left side

#### 1.2.3. Stuff on a Stick

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- TAKE cheese sample
- EXIT south

### 1.3. Food Court

- WALK southeast to the white shop

#### 1.3.1. Refried Things

- USE red cord (`Red power cord`)
- EXIT south

### 1.4. Food Court

- WALK east to the pink shop

#### 1.4.1. I can't believe, I can't taste it!

- USE `Beef flavoured Jello` with `Toothpick` (`Greasy toothpick`, `Greasy stick`)
- USE `Greasy toothpick` with broken lock

#### 1.4.2. Crime Scene

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE your game!
- TAKE green floppy disk (`Green disk`)
- **QUICK:** SHOOT attacking robot
- TALK TO robot
- SHOOT robot
- TAKE robot head (`Robot head`)
- USE `Multi tool` with blue cord (`Blue power cord`)
- USE `Red power cord` with `Blue power cord` (`Long power cord`)
- USE `Energy clip` with `Energy pistol`
- EXIT east

### 1.5. Food Court

- ENTER blue room to the north

#### 1.5.1. Men's Room

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- SAVE your game
  >- ENTER open stall
  >- Choose as you want
  >- LOAD the game and retry with different options

#### 1.5.2. I can't believe, I can't taste it!

- USE red keypad

#### 1.5.3. Storage Room

- USE `Long power cord` with socket sticking out of the lower left side of the blue machine
- USE `Robot head` with little gray doors of the purple machine
- TALK TO robot
  - "I can shoot you again, if you like."
  - "Travel Plans." (`Travel plans`)
  - "Thanks, I have everything I need now."
- **NOTE:** Password `bantha`

## 2. Part Two: Recipe For Disaster

### 2.1. Landing Zone

- EXIT east

### 2.2. Eastern Forest 1

- EXIT east

### 2.3. Eastern Forest 2

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- MULTITOOL bush (the one between two trees)
  >- EXIT north through the bushes
  >- MULTITOOL gate in the upper right screen
  >- ENTER gate
  >- LOOK AT each tombstone
  >- EXIT northwest
  >- TALK TO man
- MULTITOOL green vine (`Vine`)
- EXIT east

### 2.4. Eastern Forest 3

- MULTITOOL branch (`Stick`)
- EXIT southeast

### 2.5. Creek

- USE `Stick` with bridge
  - **NOTE:** You need to have the `Vine` to do this.
- EXIT east

### 2.6. Dirt Road 1

- EXIT east

### 2.7. Dirt Road 2

- EXIT north to the house

### 2.8. Ruined House

- EXIT north to the well

#### 2.8.1. Out House

- TAKE axe (`Axe`)
- EXIT south

### 2.9. Ruined House

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- USE right flowerbox (`Brown ski mask`)
  >- TRAVEL back to the first easter egg house
  >- TALK TO man
  >   - Ask about the mask
  >- USE `Brown ski mask` with man (`Whiskey`)
- EXIT south

### 2.10. Dirt Road 2

- EXIT east

### 2.11. Dirt Road 3

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE your game!
- EXIT east
- While you are walking, change to the SHOOT icon

### 2.12. Road Block

- **QUICK:**
  - SHOOT (First shoot icon = Spatha) robot **2 times**
  - SHOOT2 (the second shoot icon = Kopesh) robot **1 time**
  - SHOOT the robot **1 time**

### 2.13. Break Down

- USE `Spare power pack` with `Spatha energy pistol`
- USE `Multitool` with man
- USE `Axe` with fallen tree (`Axe handle`)

### 2.14. Parking Lot

- TAKE crow bar from the red truck (`Crowbar`)
- ENTER restaurant

#### 2.14.1. Restaurant

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- USE cabinets (`Catsup`)
- TALK TO robot
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE your game!
- USE `Axe handle` or `Crowbar`, so the item is in your hand
- USE door

#### 2.14.2. Kitchen

- USE `Your weapon of choice` with robot
- **NOTE:** Do not shoot the robot
