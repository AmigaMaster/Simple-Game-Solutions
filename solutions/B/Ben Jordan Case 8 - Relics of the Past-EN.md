- LOOK AT statue's chest (above the door)
- LOOK AT **all three** statues
  - **NOTE:** From left to right: Amette, Denis, Guibert
- LOOK AT **all three** statue bases
- LOOK AT **all four** bricks (in the left wall)
  - **NOTE:** The initials from the bricks (from top to bottom):
    - `ADG`, `GDA`, `AGD`, `DAG`
- USE the following statue bases
  1. left, middle, right (69/100)
  2. right, middle, left (70/100)
  3. left, right, middle (71/100)
  4. middle, left, right (72/100)

  - Place the tiles so the picture is a heart with a cross on top (79/100)
  - The tile with "S. C." in the middle
  - The cross at the top middle
