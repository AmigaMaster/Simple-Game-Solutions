## 1. Cheat Code

The arcade sections are extremely difficult and you most possibly need to enable the cheat.

- In the menu screen, click the small round spindle in front of the turbine blades on the hood of the car.
- SPACE to start the Screen with the follwing cheat codes:
  - **NOTE:** If you roll the health or fuel bars over too many times, the game may crash.
  - **NOTE:** Previously saved games will NOT have the cheats active. You must start a new game.
  - BACKSPACE: skips the current arcade mission. This may cause errors with the movies, forcing you to press the SPACEBAR to skip them if they lock up.
  - ENTER: Add fuel and health
  - PAGE UP: In-Game Save
  - PAGE DOWN: In-Game Load

## 2. Action Sequence 1

Avoid the enemies the following way:

- Left through the cars
- Immediately right into the cars
- Make your way through the right side
- Exit left at the lowest possibly point, avoiding the enemies
- Left through the enemies
- Again left
- Immediately to the right side
- When the big ship appears, to the right top
- When the first big ship end, immediately push down und all the way to the left

## 5. Action Sequence 4

Eay to the secret exit and secret ending:

- Middle
- Keep right and collect the energy, fly to the top screen part
- Hard left to the screen border
- At the section keep left
- In the big cave, directly fly to the left side
- At the section take the right path and fly to the top screen section
- Collect the ammo ibn the middle section and directly fly to the right cave wall

## 6. Secret Action Sequence 5

Avoid the duck hunters by flying big circles around them

Their positions and your route after exiting the cave:

- Fly to the right screen border
- RIGHT:
  - Fly slowly to the left side, avoiding his bullets. You have enough time.
- MIDDLE and halve right side:
  - Circle around the first but stay below the second one
  - When the first one left the screen, fly to the left and upper corner
- FAR LEFT:
  - Corner him around to the right side
- MIDDLE:
  - Corner him around the left side
- FAR RIGHT:
  - Stay below as long as you can, then fly up through the bullets
- Short break
- LEFT AND RIGHT:
  - Try to corner both of them to the far right side
  - As soon as you are save, fly to the right
- RIGHT and MIDDLE:
  - Corner left
  - Then corner right
- Keep far right and shoot the house for an extra bonus sequence at the end
- Directly fly left
- **The last 25 seconds are ridiculously difficult**
- Under any circumstances avoid the tower bullets!
- You need to collect the fuel container on the left side between all towers
- Keep in the middle of the screen avoiding all bullets
- Then keep at the right side but keep an eye on the bullets from the far left

## 7. Secret Ending

Without an external cheat app, it is very uncommon to see this ending. You need to collect all seven secret points without using the internal cheat function.

Gain secret points:

- +1 for Passing both parts of your flight evaluation.
- +1 for Hitting All 20 Target Drones.
- +1 for Finding the Secret Level.
- +1 for Shooting the Shack in the Secret Level.
- +3 for **???**

Lose secret points:

- -1 for Hitting more than for 5 Laser Gates in the Timed Trial.
- -1 for Activating the Gamma-class WarDyne's aggression mode.
- -1 for Destroying the Kilo-class Target Drone Carrier.
