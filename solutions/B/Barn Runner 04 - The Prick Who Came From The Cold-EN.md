# Barn Runner 4 - The Prick Who Came From The Cold - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 25.05.2024 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the free game from the [Homepage](https://www.barnrunner.com/barn4.html)

## 1. Landing Zone

- EXIT east

## 2. Dump

- TAKE No Dumpin' sign (`Pole`)
- >EASTER EGG:
  >- TALK TO yellow robot **9 times**
- EXIT east

## 3. Shack

- TAKE piece of wood from the ground (`Fire wood`)

## 4. Landing Zone

- EXIT west

## 5. Fence

- >EASTER EGG (Part 1):
  >- LOOK AT gun tower in the background
  >- LOOK AT fence post
- WALK east through the shadows of the trees and then EXIT NORTH

### 5.1. Spider Web

- USE `Fire wood` with `DataLink` (`Broken glass`, `Broken DataLink`)
- USE `Broken glass` with web

## 6. Fence

- USE `Fire wood` with `Pole` (`Sticky pole`)
- USE `Sticky pole` with red ornament in the nest in the tree (`Red ornament`)
- EXIT north

## 7. Dump

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- USE `Sticky pole` with Santa's hat (`Santa Claus hat`)

## 8. Shack

- USE `Multi tool` with door knob

### 8.1. Inside Shack

- >EASTER EGGS:
  >- LOOK AT stew pot **13 times**
  >- LOOK AT right picture **4 times**
  >- LOOK AT left picture **3 times**
  >- LOOK AT rifle **3 times**
  >- TAKE rifle **7 times** (`Automatic pistol`)
  >- USE `Santa Claus hat` with old man (`Package`)
  >- USE `Multi tool` with `Package` (`Teddy bear`)
  >- USE `Broken DataLink` with old man
- >EASTER EGG (Part 2):
  >- TALK TO old man - he will tell you about the towers and fence
  >- TALK TO MAN - you can tell him a joke, if you talked to the robot earlier
- TALK TO old man
- USE `Red ornament` with old man (`Coat hanger`)

## 9. Landing Zone

- USE `Coat hanger` with car window
