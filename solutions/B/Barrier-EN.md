## 1. Walkthrough

- KICK tent
- KICK tree
- JUMP tree
- KICK crane **four times**
- TOUCH crane
  - **QUICK:** JUMP crane
- WALK to bush on the right, then face to the left (looking at Mei)
- TOUCH flower, then **QUICK:**
  - KICK flower
- TALK TO Mei
- TOUCH guard **4 times**
