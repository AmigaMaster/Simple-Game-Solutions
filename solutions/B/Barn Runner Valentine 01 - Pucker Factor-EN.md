## 5. Action Sequence 2: Storms

- >ACHIEVEMENT: Eye of the needle
  >- You must not get damage due to radiation and must arrive with yellow fuel level. This is quite hard to achieve.
  >- From the beginning keep northwest until the first green radiation cloud
  >- With an interspace of about you AutoDyne width, fly north beside the first and second radiation cloud
  >- Pass the right cloud northeast as close as possible (about half interspace of your AtoDyne)
  >- Keep flying east / southeast / northeast, always passing the clouds with about half of your AutoDyne width.
  >- At the target you need to fly into the red "land here" box.
  >- Good luck!

- Begin with the green tiles which belong into the first row
- The top left tile is completely green

- Second row has heads in the left tile and the orange crane in the right tile
- Third row has the grey container on the left side and the black container on the right side
- Fourth row with red container with big stripes on the left and red container with small stripes on the right side
