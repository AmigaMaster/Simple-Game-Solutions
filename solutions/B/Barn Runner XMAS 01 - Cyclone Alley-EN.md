## 1. Cheat Code

The second arcade sections is very difficult and you most possibly need to enable the cheat.

- In the menu screen, click the small, leftmost tailpipe between both big thrusters.
- Start the game and use the following cheat codes:
  - **NOTE:** If you roll the health or fuel bars over too many times, the game may crash.
  - **NOTE:** Previously saved games will NOT have the cheats active. You must start a new game.
  - BACKSPACE: skips the current arcade mission. This may cause errors with the movies, forcing you to press the SPACEBAR to skip them if they lock up.
  - ENTER: Add fuel and health
  - PAGE UP: In-Game Save
  - PAGE DOWN: In-Game Load

## 2. Action Sequence 1

- Simply try to avoid the tornados, you should have plenty of energy#
- It does not matter how low your energy is when you reach the end of level 1

## 3. Action Sequence 2

- Enter and fly left
- At the next junction you need to keep right
- Fly all the way through the big cave, follow all the way to the next junction
- Keep right and again right and fly to the upper poart of the screen
- Through the brigher part an hard to the right, you need to fly down to get through the corner
- immediately fly to the upper part and hard to the left, again pushing down to get through the left corner
- immediately partial hard right. Again you need to partially fly down the get the right corner into the cave
- Follow the way to the exit
- >EASTER EGG:
  >- As soon as you exit, fly to the top left part of the screen and immediately fly to the bottom right to avoid the drone
- Immediately fly the the half left top of the sreen
- As soon as the next Drone appears, wait for it to end and directly keep pushing down and right to avoid the next drone on the left side
