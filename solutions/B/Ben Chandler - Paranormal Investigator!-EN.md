# Ben Chandler: Paranormal Investigator! - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 15.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/ben-chandler-paranormal-investigator)

## 1. Ben's House

### 1.1. House

- **NOTE:** Do not pick up the accordion yet
- USE (left) door to exit

## 2. Akubra Bar

- EXIT west

## 3. Sheep Farm

- ENTER house entrance

### 3.1. House Entrance

- TALK TO Minge Patterson
- PICK UP shears (`Shears`)
- EXIT west

### 3.2. Bawn

- USE `Shears` on Ram
- EXIT east **2 times**

## 4. Sheep Farm

- EXIT west

## 5. Forest Path

- EXIT northwest
- TALK TO Grand Wizard
- EXIT east

## 6. Sheep Farm

- TALK TO little boy

## 7. Akubra Bar

- ENTER Bar

### 7.1. Bar

- TALK TO bar patron
  - "Any advice for shearing a sheep?"
- TALK TO Rosie
  - "I'd like a drink, please."
  - "What's the most complicated and time-consuming..."
  - You now need to head bck to your hut

## 8. Ben's House

### 8.1. House

- PICK UP accordion (`Accordion`)

## 9. Akubra Bar

### 9.1. Bar

- USE `Accordion`
- PICK UP small bottle (from the table) (`Sheep Tranquilizers`)

## 10. Ben's House

### 10.1. House

- USE accordion hook

## 11. Sheep Farm

### 11.1. Bawn

- USE `Sheep Tranquilizers` on Ram
- USE `Shears` on Ram (`Wool`)

### 11.2. House Entrance

- USE `Wool` on Minge Patterson (`Money`)

## 12. Akubra Bar

### 12.1. Bar

- TALK TO Rosie
  - "I'd like a drink, please."
  - "Have you got any wine?"
- USE `Money` on Rosie (`Wine`)

## 13. Sheep Farm

- USE `Wine` on little boy (`Des Bonbons Aux Fraises`)

## 14. Forest Path

- USE `Des Bonbons Aux Fraises` on Grand Wizard
- EXIT northwest
