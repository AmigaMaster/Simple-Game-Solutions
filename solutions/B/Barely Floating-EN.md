## 1. Lower Deck

### 1.1. Bar Hallway

#### 1.1.1. Your Cabin

- OPEN suitcase
- USE `Cane` with suitcase
- USE suitcase (`Green pills`, `Red pills`)
- USE door
  - Talk about every dialog line
  - Ask about window (while talking move the cursor onto the window)
  - "I'm bored"
  - "Tell a story"
- USE door

### 1.2. Bar Hallway

- EXIT right

### 1.3. Information Desk Hallway

- TALK TO Information Officer (Igon) (`Blue Pills`)
- EXIT left

### 1.4. Bar Hallway

- ENTER bar

#### 1.4.1. Bar

- TALK TO bartender
- PICK UP extension cord (on the speaker)
- WAIT until the bartender look at the shelves, then
  - PICK UP extension cord (`Extension Cords`)
- EXIT bar

### 1.5. Information Desk Hallway

- TALK TO Igon (`Rope`)
- USE `Cane` with power plug (on the machine)
- USE `Extension Cord` with power plug
  - USE Extension cord with power outlet (on the left from the desk)
- USE machine
- TALK TO little girl
- USE machine
- Walk to the left and return to the bar. Walk to the left and talk to Dave. Tell him about the kids and offer him to get them out. Tell him about the bingo plan and he’ll help if he can.

### 1.6. Bar Hallway

#### 1.6.1. Bar

- TALK TO bartender
  - Talk about everything
  - Then order a drink
- USE `5$ banknote` with bartender

### 1.7. Bar Hallway

- USE cabin door
- USE `Rope` with statue
  - USE rope wit cabin door

### 1.8. Information Desk Hallway

- TALK TO Igon
  - Ask about plant (`Leaf`)
- EXIT upstairs

### 11.4. Bar Hallway

#### 11.4.1. Bar

- TALK TO triplets
- PICK UP bright lamp (on the table)
- TALK TO brother Tahwid
  - Living: In harmony, acting through inaction
  - God: Many gods
  - Death: Return to original state after death
  - Likes: Black & white movies (=`Picture left of jukebox`)
  - Dislikes: Alcohol (=`Bottles on the bar`)
- TALK TO brother Bahwid (Buddhism)
  - Living: Avoid extremes, take the middle way
  - God: No god
  - Death: Reincarnation
  - Likes: Fish (=`Fish on the wall above bar`)
  - Dislikes: Captivity (=`Chain around jukebox`)
- TALK TO brother Mahwid
  - Living: Serves Allah
  - God: 1 god
  - Death: Depending on the way you lived
  - Likes: Sun (=`Torch in front of boys`)
  - Dislikes: Monsters (=`Octopus behind you`)
- USE karaoke equipment
  - You need to let the boys agree to each other by providing a "Like" or "Dislike" answer. Example 1:
    - Mahwid asks Tahwid if he believes in more than one god.
    - Mahwid believes in one god, so Tahwid answer must be a "Dislike"
    - You need to sing about Tahwid's dislike `Bottles on the bar`
  - Example 2:
    - Bahwid asks Mahwid about reincarnation
    - Bahwid believes in reincarnation, ao Mahwid answer must be a "like"
    - Sing about the `Picture left of jukebox`
  - Repeat this until the brothers agree to each other
- PICK UP bright lamp (`Bright lamp`)
