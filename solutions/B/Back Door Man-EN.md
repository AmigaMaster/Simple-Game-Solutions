## 1. Bar

- Choose your story

## 2. Young Woman Client

### 2.1. Home

- Choose any name

### 2.2. Restaurant

- When you play for the money, always select the first or second answer ($200)
- Otherwise always select the third answer, then you can choose to do it and get the money or quit

## 3. The Older Lady

### 3.1. Hallway

- USE (right) door

#### 3.1.1. Apartment

- TALK TO woman
- USE red handbag
  - "No ambulance"
- WALK left to the bathroom
- USE cabinet
  - USE blue bottle
- TALK TO woman
  - "Are you okay?..."
  - "I believe I satisfied my end of the deal..." ($200)

## 4. Male Client

### 4.1. Street

- USE door

### 4.2. Living Room

- **QUICK:**
  - USE left door

### 4.3. Bedroom

- TALK TO man
  - "What is it you do for a living?"
  - "Do you like living in Coral Gables?"
  - "Okay, enough chat..."
- TALK TO man
  - "$400 extra"
  - "Refuse"
  - "$300 extra"
- USE closet
- USE left door

### 4.4. Living Room

- PICK UP red vase
- PICK UP statuette from the worktop (on the far right)
- PICK UP sculpture (from the table) ($650)
- USE front door
