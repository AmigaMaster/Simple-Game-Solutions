## 1. Lobby Elevator

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- LOOK AT `DataLink`
- TALK TO Coat Check Bot
  - "Let me ask you about something else…"
  - "Can you lower that screen and let me in?"
  - "Maybe we can find another solution?"
- USE `Note` with open drawer
- USE `Pistol` with open drawer
- EXIT east

## 2. Restaurant Area

### 2.1. Restaurant

- >EASTER EGG:
  >- USE lectern
  >- TALK TO Pierre
  >   - Ask about the other people
- TALK TO Pierre (`Menu`)
- TALK TO Aurora
- TALK TO Mayor DuChamp
- TALK TO Boss Huffmann
- TALK TO Notenki
- TALK TO Ginger
- EXIT South to the bar

#### 2.1.1. Bar

- TALK TO Bartenderbot
  - "Let me ask you about something else…"
  - "What do you have that’s good for hangovers?" (`Coffee mug`)
- ENTER men toilets to the right

##### 2.1.1.1. Men's Room

- TALK TO Stu

## 3. Elevator Lobby

- USE `Electronic lock release` with door lock

### 3.1. Coat Check Closet

- TAKE gold plated DataLink
- USE `Broken DataLink` with Melanie (`gold-plated DataLink`)
- USE `gold-plated DataLink` with Busbot
- TALK TO Busbot (`Platinumware`)
- USE `Menu` with Melanie

## 4. Restaurant Area

### 4.1. Restaurant

#### 4.1.1. Bar

- TALK TO Bartenderbot
  - "Let me ask you about something else…"
  - "Got anything to eat?" (`Packwich`)

## 5. Elevator Lobby

### 5.1. Coat Check Closet

- USE `Packwich` with Melanie
- USE `electronic lock release` with Busbot

## 6. Restaurant Area

### 6.1. Restaurant

#### 6.1.1. Office Elevator

- USE `electronic lock release` with door lock to the storage room

##### 6.1.1.1. Storage Room

- USE `Multi tool` with Hangarbot
- USE `Multi tool` with Maidbot
- TAKE badge (`Badge`)
- LOOK AT security monitoring station
- USE security monitoring station
- USE `electronic lock release` with security monitoring station

#### 6.1.2. Office Elevator

- USE `electronic lock release` with door lock to the manager's office

##### 6.1.2.1. Aurora's Office

- USE control unit (on the wall) **3 times**
- TAKE can of whipped cream (`Grip Whip`)
- USE `Badge` with Aurora (`Spatha Pistol`)
- TAKE wedge of cheese (`Cheddar`)

## 7. Restaurant Area

### 7.1. Restaurant

#### 7.1.1. Bar

- USE `Spatha Pistol` with Bartenderbot

##### 7.1.1.1. Men's Room

- TAKE rubber gloves (`Kitchen gloves`)

#### 7.1.2. Bar

- ENTER kitchen

##### 7.1.2.1. Kitchen

- >EASTER EGGS:
  >- TALK TO foam
- USE tape player
- USE `Memory stick` with Chefbot
- USE cabinet nearest to the Chefbot
- USE `Grip Whip` with Chefbot (`Squee-Key-Clean`)

### 7.2. Restaurant

- TALK TO Ginger
  - "Let me ask you about something else…"
  - "Do you have any demo tapes?"

#### 7.2.1. Bar

- TALK TO Bartenderbot
  - "Let me ask you about something else…"
  - "I want to get a drink…"
  - Choose one of the drinks (`Cocktail`)

### 7.3. Restaurant

- USE `Cocktail` with Ginger (`Cassette tape`)

#### 7.3.1. Bar

- ENTER kitchen

##### 7.3.1.1. Kitchen

- USE `Cassette tape` with tape player
- USE foam
- USE `Multi tool` with drain handle
- USE `Squee-Key-Clean` with foam
- USE faucet knobs (`Moo cow cheese`)
  - **NOTE:** You need to have the `Kitchen gloves` to get the cheese
- TALK TO Stu

### 7.4. Restaurant

- USE `Moo cow cheese` with Chief
  - Choose any answer
