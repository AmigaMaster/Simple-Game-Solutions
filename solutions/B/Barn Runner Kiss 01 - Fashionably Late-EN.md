## Game

There are 16 dolls to unlock. It depends on the decisions you make during the game. To get all dolls, stay to the following order:

1. A normal day at work.
2. A day off spent sleeping in.
3. A date with her girlfriend.
4. A formal event with the Mayor.
5. A fancy party with the important people of the city.
6. A light lunch with a notorious crime boss.
7. A day spent at home with her girlfriend.
8. A good workout at the gym.
9. A happy reunion with her visiting big sister.
10. A normal day at work.
11. An end to this anxiety-fueled dream.
12. An entire day spent at the gym!!!!
13. An early morning meeting with a co-worker.
14. A kidnapping followed by a play date.
