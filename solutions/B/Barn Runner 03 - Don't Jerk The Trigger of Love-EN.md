## 1. Infos

- If you succeed in all tests, you miss many dialogues
- You need to fail on purpose
- You can cycle through LOOK, TALK and USE to interact with the room behind yourself
- After you fail a range, TALK TO Debbie
- Failing range two and three enables extra dialogue options

## 2. Game

### 2.1. Range 1

- Leave the range without any shooting
- TALK TO Debbie
- Shoot one target
- Miss the other two targets
- Hit all targets

### 2.2. Range 2

- Leave the range without any shooting
- Miss all shots and wait for the end, then EXIT range
- TALK TO Debbie
- Hit all targets

### 2.3. Range 3

- Leave the range without any shooting
- TALK TO Debbie
- Shoot all targets, including the green one
- Shoot only the green target
