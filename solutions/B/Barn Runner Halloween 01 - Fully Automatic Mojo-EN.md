# Barn Runner - Halloween 01 - Fully Automatic Mojo - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 01.06.2024 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the free game from the [Homepage](https://www.barnrunner.com/spooky1.html)

## Traffic

- Move to the right lane until you pass the green car
- Immediately move to the center lane

## Sewer 1

- Change to secondary weapon and shoot each CHUD three times
- Change to primary weapon and shoot the remaining CHUD's

## Escape with your Car

- Try to avoid all CHUD's
- LEFT
- RIGHT
- LEFT
- RIGHT
- LEFT
- RIGHT

## Car Accident

- Change weapon and concentrate on the left CHUD and shoot it first
- There are only two CHUD's to shoot

## Inside Car

- Cup holder
- Coin tray
- Glove Box
- Dashboard
- Arm rest
- Under passenger seat
- Under driver's seat
- Behind driver's seat
- Jacket

## Sniper

- Just kill all seven CHUD's

## Water Vapor Recovery Pipes

- Simply wait before all obstacles until they are safe to cross. They all have simple patterns.

## Head over Heels

- Concentrate on the CHUD which is far most to the top left of the screen
- Do not forget to reload
- One of the three CHUD's will stop walking at the crossway, this is your least important target

## Actuators

- Open all valves from right to left
- Click on the little red thing left to the first valve to transfer the missing two pressure bars to the
- Switch on the manual extend on the right side

## Escape from CHUD Crowd

- Immediately run south, over the crossway
- Follow the street further south until the bottom screen border
- At the last way south, keep a little bit to the right side of the street to avoid two CHUD's
- Walk east through the three corners
- On the way south, keep as far left as possible to avoid one CHUD
- Follow north, then west to te center
- North
- West, keep at the lower part of the street
- North to the car
