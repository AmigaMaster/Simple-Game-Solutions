## 1. Annie's House

- PICK UP bowl (`Bowl`) (1/82)
- QUESTION Annie Roberts
  - "Husband" (2/82)
  - "The Lost Galleon" (`Police Report`) (3/82)
  - "Herself"
  - "Dunesberg"
- LOOK AT `Police Report` (4/82)
- EXIT east
