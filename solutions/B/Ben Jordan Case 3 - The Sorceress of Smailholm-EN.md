## 12. Signpost Crossing

- LOOK AT red text on the tree (67/144)
  - **Riddle:** Decrypt the message
    - Open the file 2.gif in the handbook folder
    - You can now decrypt 6 letters: A, O, T, C, J, Z
    - LOOK AT `Code Letter` to fully decrypt the message:
      ```
      WW Dead
      Mary will die
      innocently BJ
      Where we want
      ```

## 16. Quick Solution

### 16.1. Archway Street

- Simply WAIT and do nothing

### 16.2. Small Room

- USE rope **4 times** (`Rope`) (80/144)
- USE hole (in the wall) (83/144)
- USE `Rope` on hook (85/144)
- WAIT until the squeaks, then
  - **QUICK:** USE rope (88/144)
- PICK UP rope with hook (`Rope with hook`) (90/144)
- TALK TO McGee
- USE `Rope with hook` on McGee (`Brass Key`) (92/144)
- USE `Brass Key` on door (94/144)

### 16.3. Forest Crossing

- **SAVE YOUR GAME HERE**
- **QUICK:** USE `Rope with hook` on Zortherus (105/144)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 17. Full Solution

### 17.1. Smailholm

#### 17.1.1. Archway Street

- EXIT west

### 17.2. Town Path

- USE `Crowbar` on McGee (84/144)
- USE trousers (`Key on a Ring`, `Tranquilizer Gun`) (90/144)

### 17.3. Smailholm

#### 17.3.1. Archway Street

##### 17.3.1.1. Jail

- USE `Key on a Ring` on cell door (95/144)

#### 17.3.2. Hotel Inn

##### 17.3.2.1. Main Room

- **SAVE YOUR GAME HERE**
- EXIT hotel inn

##### 17.3.2.2. Percy's Room

- "Tell the truth" (99/144)
- "Accuse the McBees" (103/144)
- "Say nobody killed them" (107/144)
- EXIT hotel and town

### 17.4. Forest Crossing

- LOOK AT red text on the tree (110/144)
  - **Riddle:** Decode the text
    - Use the `Code Letter` and the `2.gif` in the handbook
    - Decoded it is:
      ```
      Eliminate
         PQJ
             Z
      ```
    - `Z` is an initial of a character

### 17.5. Smailholm

#### 17.5.1. Hotel Inn

##### 17.5.1.1. Main Room

- USE first door on the upper level

##### 17.5.1.2. Percy's Room

- "Tell about Witch Code"

- PICK UP shovel (`Shovel`) (118/144)
- EXIT hotel inn

#### 17.5.2. Cemetery

##### 17.5.2.1. Grave

- USE coffin (124/144)

#### 17.5.3. Ceremony
