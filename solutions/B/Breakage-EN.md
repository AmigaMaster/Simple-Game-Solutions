# Breakage - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 20.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/breakage-v1.5.0.4)

## 1. Dungeon Entrance

### 1.1. Corridor

#### 1.1.1. Prison

- PICK UP can (`Can`)
- TALK TO blob
  - "How do I get out of this room?"
  - "Are you sitting..."
- PICK UP key (`Key`)
- USE `Key` on door
- EXIT cell

#### 1.1.2. Corridor

- PICK UP scrap of paper (`Scrap of paper` (1))
- EXIT east

## 2. Dungeon Entrance

- EXIT south

## 3. Bridge

- PICK UP ? (the creature on the water)
- EXIT north through the gate

## 4. Forest

- TALK TO tree
- PICK UP acorn (`Acorn`)
- TALK TO green creature
- EXIT west

## 5. Fish Shop

- TALK TO large fish shop
- EXIT east

## 6. Forest

- TALK TO green creature
  - "It isn't tiring, all that flipping?"
  - "The repetitive movement doesn't bother your?"
  - "Feeling the joints move again and again..."
  - "It's agonizing isn't it?"
- PICK UP coin (`Coin`)

## 7. Fish Shop

- TALK TO large fish shop
  - "Can I buy something?"
  - "I'll buy the silly thing." (`Small boat`)
- USE `Small boat` on a small stream
- EXIT south (`Coins`)

## 8. Fountain

- EXIT east

## 9. Fish Shop

- TALK TO large fish shop
  - "Can I buy something?"
  - "I want the more expensive thing." (`Bowl`)
  - "I want the costly thing" (`Drill`)
- EXIT east

## 10. Forest

- EXIT north through the portal

## 11. Bridge

- EXIT south

## 12. Dungeon Entrance

- USE `Drill` on barrels
- USE `Bowl` on ugly patch (`Bowl with goo`)

## 13. Fountain

- USE `Bowl with goo` on box (on the left side) (`Bowl`)
- PICK UP bolt cutter (`Bolt cutter`)

## 14. Forest

- EXIT east

## 15. Crash Site

- PICK UP belts (`Belt`)
- PICK UP smoking box (`Box`)
- PICK UP bendy thingy

## 16. Fountain

- PICK UP stamp? (`Stamp`)

## 17. Dungeon Entrance

- USE `Stamp` on a marking stone
- ENTER dungeon

#### 17.0.3. Corridor

- PICK UP shiny stone (`Ruby`)

## 18. Dungeon Entrance

- USE `Ruby` on some kind of device

### 18.1. Corridor

- USE `Bolt cutter` on ball (`Ball`)

## 19. Dungeon Entrance

- USE `Bowl` on ugly patch (`Bowl with goo`)

## 20. Crash Site

- USE `Ball` on statue
- EXIT east

### 20.1. Passageway

- USE (upper) lever (`A stick`)
- USE `Bowl with goo` on box
- PICK UP candle (`Candle`)
- USE (lower) lever (`A stick`)
- USE `Candle` on chandelier
- USE `Bolt cutter` on rope
- PICK UP matchbox (`Matches`)
- USE `Matches` on candles
  - **NOTE:** The shadows on the holes
- USE `Acorn` on squirrel
- USE rope
- WALK down
- USE the sticks from your inventory and from the wall and put them into the following holes:
  - 2, 7, 10, 12
- EXIT east

### 20.2. Control Room

- PICK UP scrap of paper (on the keyboard) (`Scrap of paper` (2))
- USE button (right next to the cover)
- PICK UP straw (`Straw`)
- EXIT east

## 21. Mountains

- PICK UP scrap of paper (on the ground) (`Scrap of paper` (3))
- EXIT north

## 22. Witch House

- PICK UP vegetables (`Potato`, `Carrot`, `Cabbage`)
- EXIT west

## 23. Mountains

- EXIT east

## 24. Crossroad

- USE `Bolt cutter` on hammer (on the vending machine)
- PICK UP hammer (`Club`)
- EXIT east

## 25. Castle

- Ground:
  - ENTER left doorway
- Second Level:
  - ENTER middle doorway
- Fourth Level:
  - ENTER third doorway
- Furnace:
  - USE `Matches` on furnace
  - ENTER doorway
- Fourth Level:
  - USE lever (on the left side)
  - PICK UP cog (`Cog`)
  - ENTER second doorway
- Third Level Window:
  - USE `Cog` on cogs
  - ENTER window
- Fourth Level:
  - USE crane
  - ENTER fourth doorway
- Fifth Level:
  - USE `Bolt cutter` on chain
  - ENTER rightmost doorway
- Third Level:
  - ENTER main door (`Scrap of paper` (4))
- EXIT south

## 26. Crossroad

- EXIT north

## 27. Spaceship

### 27.1. Entrance

- PICK UP small cloud (`Cloud`)
- TALK TO shy alien (behind the cloud)
- USE console (The cloud in the middle)
- ENTER door

### 27.2. Back Room

- PICK UP small cloud (`Cloud`)
- PICK UP key (from the right alien) (`A Small Key`)
- USE button (in the center cloud console)

### 27.3. Cork Room

- PICK UP cork (from the alien's ear) (`Cork`)
- ENTER (left) door

### 27.4. Portal Room

- USE `Cabbage` on thingy (the machine on the right)
- ENTER portal

### 27.5. Top of Spaceship

- PICK UP cork (on the left edge) (`Cork`)
- USE `Club` on (red) ball (`Coin`)
- ENTER (lower) portal

### 27.6. Fisherman

- PICK UP (white) pants (`White Pants`)
- TALK TO older alien
- ENTER portal

### 27.7. Top of Spaceship

- ENTER upper portal

### 27.8. Portal Room

- USE `Cork` on another alien **2 times**
- PICK UP cloud (`Cloud`)
- ENTER door

### 27.9. Cork Room

- ENTER (right) door

### 27.10. Entrance

- USE `White Pants` on shy alien
- EXIT south

## 28. Crossroad

- LOOK AT glass plate (of the vending machine)
- USE `A Small Key` on glass plate
- PICK UP scrap of paper (`Scrap of paper` (5))
- EXIT west **2 times**

## 29. Mountains

### 29.1. Control Room

- USE `Bowl` on bottom of machine
- USE red button
- USE yellow button
- PICK UP bowl with orange (`Bowl with Orange`)
- USE `Bowl with Orange` on `White Pants` (`Orange Pants`)
- EXIT east **2 times**, north

## 30. Spaceship

### 30.1. Entrance

- USE `Orange Pants` on shy alien (`Large Cloud`)

## 42. Witch House

- PICK UP wood (from the right bucket) **2 times** (`Log`, `Log`, `Box`)
- TALK TO old woman (`Key` (1))
- LOOK AT `Box`
- PICK UP door mat (`Key` (2))
- PICK UP coverup?
- USE `Box` on long key
- PICK UP frosting (`Frosting`)
- WALK TO the front of the cauldron
- TALK TO yellow creature **2 times** (on the wire)
- PICK UP scissors (`Scissors`)
- USE `Scissors` on pumpkin tapestry (`Cloth with Pumpkin`)
- USE `Scissors` on rope (right of the chimney)
- WALK TO the right door
- TALK TO yellow creature **7 times**
- PICK UP key (`Key` (3))
- TALK TO black hole
- PICK UP cymbal (`Cymbal`)
- USE `Frosting` on `Cymbal` (`Cymbal with Frosting`)
- USE `Cymbal with Frosting` on `Cloth with Pumpkin` (`Cymbal with Pumpkin`)
- USE `Cymbal with Pumpkin` on cauldron
- USE `Book` on old woman
- USE `Cymbal with Pumpkin` on cauldron
- PICK UP key (`Key` (4))
- USE **all four** `Key` on box
- USE box (hotspot on the key inside the box)
- PICK UP small ball (`Small ball`)
- USE door to exit

## 43. Lake

- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE to see both endings
  - To end the game, EXIT east
- USE `Drill` on `Can` (`Can with hole`)
- USE `Duct tape` on `Can with hole` (`Taped with hole`)
- USE `Straw` on `Taped with hole` (`Taped with straw`)
- USE `Small ball` on `Taped with straw` (`Taped, straw and ball`)
- UE door (to the shop)
- TALK TO Arty F.
  - "Do you buy anything?"
- USE `Taped, straw and ball` on Arty F. (`Big Whoop`)
- USE water
