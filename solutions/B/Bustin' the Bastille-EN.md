# Bustin' the Bastille - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 01.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/2174-bustin-the-bastille)

## 1. Street

### 1.1. House

- USE cheese
- TALK TO LĂŠopold
- EXIT east

## 2. Street

- LOOK AT door (of your house)
- PICK UP rail (`Rail`)
- USE knife
- ENTER your house

### 2.1. House

- TALK TO LĂŠopold
  - "I heard a voice."

## 3. Street

- EXIT east

## 4. Bastille

- LOOK AT flag on the Bastille
- TALK TO pacifist
  - "Dou you recognize the banner on the tower?" (`White Flag`)
- USE Parisian flag
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
  - **NOTE:** If you shoot the cannon at the position, where the gunner is warning you **3 times**, you see an extra scene
- TALK TO citizen cannon loader
  - MOVE the cannon all the way up
  - USE cannon to shoot
- TALK TO citizen cannon loader
  - MOVE the cannon all the way down
  - USE cannon to shoot
  - **QUICK:**
    - PICK UP Parisian flag (`Parisian Flag`)
- EXIT north

## 5. Street

### 5.1. Home

- USE `White Flag` on `Parisian Flag` (`Patchwork`)
- USE `Patchwork` on LĂŠopold (`Colorful Thing`)
- USE `Colorful Thing` on `Rail` (`Thingamajig`)

## 6. Bastille

- USE `Thingamajig` on crowd of citizens
  - With the cursor keys move to the right using the safe spots and EXIT east

### 6.1. Corridor

- PICK UP sabre (`Sabre`)
- USE Swiss guard (`Powder Pouch`, `Keys`)
- PICK UP flag of the free (`Colorful Thing`)
- USE `Keys` on (middle) cell door

#### 6.1.1. Middle Cell

- TALK TO greenish citizen
- PICK UP banknote (`Banknote`)
- TALK TO lazy citizen?
- EXIT cell

### 6.2. Corridor

- USE `Powder Pouch` on `Banknote` (`Ball of Powder`)
- USE `Ball of Powder` on (right) cell door
- USE `Sabre` on brazier
- USE `Colorful Ting` on brazier (`Sabre wrapped with Flag`)
- USE `Sabre wrapped with Flag` on (right) cell door

#### 6.2.1. Right Cell Door

- USE turntable (the jug with vinegar must be visible)
- USE halberd
- USE pointy thing (on the ground)
- USE metallic sarcophagus?
