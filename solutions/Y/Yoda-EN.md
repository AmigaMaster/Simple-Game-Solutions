## 1. Cave

- PICK UP small stones (below the big one) (`Small Stones`)
- PICK UP rope (`Rope`)
- PICK UP nail (from the wall where the rope hung) (`Nail`)
- PICK UP stick (`Stick`)
- USE `Rope` on `Stick` (`Rope on a Stick`)
- USE `Nail` on `Rope on a Stick` (`Fishing Rod`)
- USE `Fishing Rod` on water (`Fish`)
- PICK UP pot (`Pot`)
- USE `Pot` on water (`Pot with Water`)
- USE `Pot with Water` on fireplace
- USE `Small Stones` on fireplace
- USE `Fish` on pot
- USE pot (`Cooked Fish`)
- USE `Cooked Fish` on yourself
- USE big stones
- EXIT east
