# Pig in a poke - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 08.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [Archive.org](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/2516-pig-in-a-poke)

## 1. Junk Yard

- PICK UP apple (27.08)
- PICK UP spare rib bone (on the blue greyish barrel) (`Spare Rib`)
- PICK UP sweets (on the oven) (`Sweets`)

## 2. Park

- PICK UP wallet
- USE `Spare Rib` on wallet
- PICK UP wallet (`Wallet`) (£11.50)
- USE park bin (28.10)

## 3. Attic

- PICK UP bubblegum (the small red spot on the right) (28.11)
- USE stash chest
- USE hoagie (in the spider web)

## 4. Burger Van

- TALK TO burger chef Pedro (29.01) (£3.50)
- PICK UP French fries (29.04)

## 5. Pier

- PICK UP fly (flying around, hard to catch) (29.05)
- PICK UP shrimp (on the pier) (29.07)

## 6. Junk Yard

- PICK UP trash grabber (on the right pile, at the bottom of the pile) (`Trash Grabber`) (£2.50)
- PICK UP (purple) bicycle pump (next to the owner) (`Bicycle Pump`) (2.00)
- PICK UP whip (right next to the owner's head) (`Whip`) (£0.00)

## 7. Pier

- USE `Bicycle Pump` on kid (`Key`) (29.09)

## 8. Burger Van

- USE `Trash Grabber` on banana (in the lamp post) (29.11)

## 9. Park

- USE `Trash Grabber` on park bin
- PICK UP sap (tree on the left) (29.12)
- PICK UP dandelion (right of the bin) (29.14)

## 10. Attic

- USE `Whip` on web
- PICK UP hoagie (29.18)
- USE `Key` on stash chest (29.22)
- USE `Sweets` on Fred (yourself) (29.29)
