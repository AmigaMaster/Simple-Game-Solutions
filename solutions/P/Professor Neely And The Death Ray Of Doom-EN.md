# Professor Neely And The Death Ray Of Doom - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 19.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/professor-neely-and-the-death-ray-of-doom)

## 1. Street

- USE mailbox lid
- TAKE mail (`Mail`)
- LOOK AT `Mail` (`Junk Mail`, `Fake Credit Card`)
- EXIT north into the path left of the house

## 2. Backyard

- TAKE can of nails (`Tin Can`, `Nails`)
- TAKE magnet (`Magnet`)
- EXIT east

## 3. Street

- ENTER side porch and EXIT around the left corner

## 4. Backyard Porch

- USE `Magnet` on old key (`House Key`)
- EXIT around the corner

## 5. Street

- To enter the house, use one of the following possibilities:
  1. USE `House Key` on front door
  2. USE `Fake Credit Card` on front door

## 6. House

### 6.1. Entrance Hall

#### 6.1.1. Kitchen

- TAKE ball of twine (`Twine`)
- TAKE box of baking soda (`Baking Soda`)
- TAKE bottle of vinegar (`Vinegar`)
- TAKE old mop (`Mop`)
- USE `Magnet` on `Mop` (`Magnet Poll`)
- There are two possible ways to open the door:
  1. Violence
     - USE `Baking Soda` on novelty sized water bottle
     - USE `Vinegar` on novelty sized water bottle
  2. Unlock
     - USE `Magnet Poll` on key (`Old Key`)
     - USE `Old key` on set of bars
- TALK TO Egore
- Task: Kill 10 Bugs
  - **NOTE:** You need to run through the entire house, the bugs can be everywhere. If you squished all 10 return to the kitchen.
- TALK TO Egore (`Bathroom Key`)
- EXIT east

### 6.2. Entrance Hall

- WALK upstairs into the room on the northwest

#### 6.2.1. Bedroom

- USE dubious bump in the wall (on the left wall beside the bed)
- TAKE Jacob's ladder (`Jacob's Loader`) (on the right side of the bed)
- USE `Jacob's Loader` on air duct
- USE dresser door
- TAKE lab coat (`Lab Coat`)
- EXIT west

### 6.3. Entrance Hall

- EXIT through the lower east door

### 6.4. Basement

- TAKE Jacob's loader (`Electrode`)
- TAKE pointy shard (`Shard`)
- USE security panel
- USE `Electrode` on exposed wires (at the top of the stairs)
- EXIT northwest

### 6.5. Entrance Hall

- EXIT northwest

### 6.6. Hallway

- USE `Bathroom Key` on (left) door
- ENTER (left) door

#### 6.6.1. Bathroom

- TAKE shower curtain (`Shower Curtain`, `Shower Rod`)
- EXIT west

### 6.7. Hallway

- USE switch
- USE `Shower Rod` on malfunctioning door
- ENTER (right) door

#### 6.7.1. Study

- LOOK AT planner (on the desk)
  - FLIP to the 31st
  - EXIT view
- TAKE diary (from the bookshelf) (`Embarassing Diary`)
- LOOK AT `Embarassing Diary`
  - "Read about the 31st?" (`Lab Pass Code`)
- USE painting
- TAKE shopping list (`Shopping List`)
- LOOK AT `Shopping List`
- USE painting (to close it)
- USE `Shard` on painting (`Picture`)
- EXIT study

### 6.8. Entrance Hall

- LOOK AT Dr. Spliner portrait (above the entrance to the hallway)
- USE `Twine` on `Nails` (`Grappling Hook`)
- USE `Grappling Hook` on Dr. Spliner portrait
- TAKE tear (`Picture`)

#### 6.8.1. Kitchen

- USE `Shopping List` on refrigerator controls
- TAKE deathray plans (`Deathray Plans`)

### 6.9. Basement

- USE `Picture` on `Lab Coat` (`Disguise`)
- USE security panel
- EXIT northeast

#### 6.9.1. Laboratory

- USE `Embarassing Diary` on Dr. Spliner
- USE `Deathray Plans` on insidious machine part
