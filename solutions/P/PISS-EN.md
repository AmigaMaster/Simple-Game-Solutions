# PISS - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 14.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/pissfinal)

## 1. Street

### 1.1. House

- PICK UP bottle (`Bottle`)
- PICK UP jar (`Jar`)
- USE bottles (`Bottle Cap`)
- EXIT northwest

## 2. Street

- TALK TO Emba
- TALK TO Gurt
- TALK TO Nax
- EXIT east

## 3. Plaza

- USE rune (the left one on the ground in the middle) (`Feather`)
- USE shrub (`Leaf`)
- USE corpse (in front the the shrub)
- USE beetle (on the bottle)
- USE post
  - "Flick the banner"
- PICK UP beetle (`Beetle`)
- TALK TO Yktor (Coppers 1/3)
- TALK TO Aoa
- EXIT west **2 times**

## 4. Marketplace

- TALK TO Banse
  - "I'm here to pick up a talisman for Yktor" (`Talisman`)
- TALK TO Krathor
- TALK TO Rhea
- EXIT east **2 times**

## 5. Plaza

- USE `Talisman` on Yktor (Coppers 1/4)
- USE `Bottle` on Aoa (Coppers 2/6)

## 6. Marketplace

- TALK TO Banse (`Ring`)
- USE `Beetle` on Banse (Coppers 2/8)
- EXIT north

## 7. Graveyard

- PICK UP stick (`Stick`)
- USE bag (in front of the left tombstone)
  - Read the note
  - Examine leaves
  - Leave the bag alone
- USE `Jar` on light (on Olte) (Coppers 2/10)
- TALK TO Olte
- TALK TO Zile (`Mortuary Ticket`) (Coppers -5/5)
- TALK TO TwoHeads
  - After some conversations, choose "The skin in brown."
- ENTER Mortuary

### 7.1. Mortuary

- TALK TO Dhel
- USE (right) corpse (Cutscene)

## 8. Marketplace

- TALK TO Rhea
  - "I went and looked into that bag." (Coppers 3/8)

## 9. Street

- USE `Ring` on Nax
- TALK TO Gurt
  - "You could leave a message in blood on a card" (Coppers 2/10)
- USE `Feather` on Gurt (Coppers 1/1)
- USE `Bottle Cap` on Gurt (Coppers 1/12)

## 10. Plaza

- TALK TO Yktor
  - "I've enough coppers for the pass." (`Temple District Pass`) (Coppers -10/2)
- EXIT north (Coppers 3/5)

## 11. Temple District

- PICK UP greenstone (`Greenstone`)
- TALK TO Cally (Coppers -1/4)
- TALK TO Ulle (`Note`)
- TALK TO Astis (`Holy Water`) (Coppers 2/6)
- ENTER temple

### 11.1. Temple

- TALK TO Khale (`Resurrection Charm`) (Coppers -5/1)
- TALK TO Quorm

## 12. Street

- USE `Greenstone` on Emba (Coppers 1/2)

## 13. Marketplace

- USE `Holy water` on Banse (Coppers 2/4)
- USE `Note` on Krathor (Coppers 2/6)
- USE `Stick` on glowing energy (in the tree) (`Wand`)
- USE `Wand` on Banse (2/8)

## 14. Plaza

- TALK TO Aoa
  - "You must know some of the mysteries..."

## 15. Graveyard

- TALK TO Olte
  - "I found out some info on the Mortuary." (Coppers 3/11)

## 16. Street

### 16.1. House

- USE `Leaf` on kettle

## 17. Afterlife

### 17.1. Floating Door

- USE light (the small one directly above the door)

### 17.2. Cavern

- USE casket
  - "Inspect the top of the casket."
    - **NOTE:** `Vulture, Reaper, Three Eyes`
- TALK TO Tawr
  - "What are those symbols on top of the casket?"
  - "What's that casket?"
- USE Moira (yourself)

### 17.3. Floating Door

- USE light (the smaller one in the middle of the room)

### 17.4. Fhorn

- TALK TO Fhiel
  - "I am having trouble deciphering some hieroglyphs."
  - "A Vulture"
  - "The grim reaper"
  - "Three eyes"

### 17.5. Floating Door

- USE (rightmost, small) light

### 17.6. Outskirts

- TALK TO Bonn
- TALK TO Merne
  - "That Bonn seems to fancy you."
- TALK TO Bonn
  - "The tailor seems to like you too."
- LOOK AT crystal (hanging from the tree)
- USE crystal

### 17.7. Cavern

- USE casket
  - "Look beneath the casket."
- TALK TO Tawr
  - "What are you going to do with the book?"

### 17.8. Floating Door

- USE light (the big light above the door)

### 17.9. Inside Temple

- TALK TO Phul
  - "Would you be willing to translate..."
  - "Any idea how to talk to spirits?"

### 17.10. Outskirts

- PICK UP crystal (`Crystal`)

### 17.11. Cavern

- USE `Crystal` (`Ring`)
- TALK TO Tawr
  - "You should take the book to the temple of Urille."

### 17.12. Temple

- TALK TO Phul
  - "Has Tawr brought Calisir's writings here?" (`Transcription`)

### 17.13. Outskirts

- USE `Ring` on Bonn
- USE `Transcription` on Bonn

### 17.14. Temple

- USE `Transcription` on Phul

### 17.15. Floating Door

- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE door

### 17.16. River

- USE `Resurrection Charm`

### 17.17. Floating Door

- CHOOSE as you like

## 18. Street

### 18.1. House

- USE table
- EXIT house

## 19. Plaza

- TALK TO Yktor

## 20. Temple District

- ENTER temple

## 21. Plateau

- PICK UP vase (`Vase`)
- USE rose
- PICK UP (left) rose (`Rose`)
- PICK UP rod (`Rod`)
- USE `Rose` on rose (in the vase)
- USE `Vase` on rose (on the block)
- PICK UP rose (from the vase on the block) (`Rose`)
- USE `Rose` on rose (in the right vase)
- USE `Rod` on rose (on the right) (`Rose`)
- USE `Rose` on rose (in the vase)
- PICK UP cloth (on the lounge) (`Cloth`)
- USE `Cloth` on sticky (a bowl under the lounge) (`iCloth`)
- USE `iCloth` on lounge
- PICK UP rose (from the lunge) (`Rose`)
- USE `Rose` on rose (in the vase)
