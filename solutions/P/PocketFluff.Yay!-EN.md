# PocketFluff.Yay!  - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 04.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

## 1. Notes

- **ATTENTION:** There is an arcade sequence which is pretty hard as the game crashes very often and the controls are bad. I provide a savegame `PocketFluff.Yay!-Savegame.7z` inside this folder of this walkthrough.

## 2. Your Room

- USE radio (no batteries)
- EXIT south

## 3. Garden

- EXIT east

## 4. Huge Tree

- USE dirt?? (the hotspot with two "?", on the lower left of the tree) (`Batteries`)
- EXIT south, west

## 5. Your Room

- USE `Batteries` on radio

## 6. Huge Tree

- TALK TO Polina

## 7. Garden

- USE dead bird
- PICK UP letter (`Letter`)
- LOOK AT `Letter`

## 8. Your Room

- PICK UP power cord (from the floor on the left. **NOTE:** There is no hotspot name!) (`Plug & Cord`)
- USE `Plug & Cord` on outlet
- USE Galactic Brain Eaters
  - **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE. The game crashes very often during this part, even when played inside a Windows XP virtual machine.
  - You need 200 points in order to beat the highscore
  - Your target is to collect the small white bombs and then kill the alien with the bombs using the `SPACE` key
  - The easiest way I found is to collect one or two bombs (save each time you get one because the game crashes very often when using a ladder!) and then wait at a ladder for the alien and lay a bomb. **NOTE:** You can collet a bomb after you placed it. When you successfully kill the alien, yot get another free bomb.
  - PRESS `s` to start the game
  - PICK UP the bomb on the lowest level and **SAVE YOUR GAME**
  - WALK UP the left ladders and collect the second bomb **SAVE YOUR GAME**
  - WALK UP the left ladder to the 4th level and wait until the alien arrives, then place a bomb at the ladder. Each time you kill the alien **SAVE YOUR GAME**
- PICK UP little thingy (from the arcade's coin slot) (`Lint`)
- PICK UP cup?? (from the shelf, left and above the radio) (`Water`)

## 9. Garden

- USE `Lint` on dead bird
- USE dead bird
  - Play the game
  - Number the pictures from 1 to 8 from left to right and from top to bottom
  - USE them in order `8, 6, 1, 5, 3, 2, 4, 7` (`Seed`)
- PICK UP toadstools (`Toadstool`)

## 10. Huge Tree

- USE `Seed` on Polina (`Yellow Seed`, `Brown Seed`, `Orange Seed`, `Red Seed`, `Small Seed`, `Blue Seed`)
- USE `Small Seed` on (right) dirt
  - **ATTENTION:** Start with the right side as you may become stuck when starting with the left side and you need to restore an old savegame.
- USE `Brown Seed` on (left) dirt
- PICK UP compost heap (`Compost`)
- USE `Compost` on Polina
  - **NOTE:** You need to walk to the upper part of the screen and to the left and Polina
- USE `Water` on Polina
- USE `Toadstool` on Polina
