# Necro Quest 1 Deluxe - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 04.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/necroquest-1-deluxe)

## 1. Street

- ENTER house (right-click)

### 1.1. Necromancer House

#### 1.1.1. Stairs

- ENTER first floor

#### 1.1.2. First Floor

##### 1.1.2.1. Reception

- USE desk
- ENTER office

##### 1.1.2.2. Office

- USE desk (`Keys`)
- USE PC

##### 1.1.2.3. Reception

- USE `Keys` on desk
- USE desk (`Basement Key`)
- EXIT east
- down to the basement and use the key to unlock the door. Then enter the basement.

#### 1.1.3. Stairs

- USE `Basement Key` on basement

#### 1.1.4. Basement

##### 1.1.4.1. Pentagram Room

- PICK UP Necrostaff (it does not work)
- >EASTER EGG:
  >- USE pentagram

#### 1.1.5. First Floor

##### 1.1.5.1. Office

- USE PC

##### 1.1.5.2. Reception

- USE desk (`Pen`)

## 2. Street

- ENTER Orks Warshop

### 2.1. Orks Warshop

- LOOK AT cool poster (top right wall) (`Cool Posters`)
- TALK TO Jarukhai (`Pistol`, `Gun Repair Kit`, `Crowbar`)

### 2.2. Necromancer House

#### 2.2.1. First Floor

##### 2.2.1.1. Office

- USE PC (`Necrostaff Spell`)

#### 2.2.2. Stairs

- USE `Keys` on attic
- ENTER attic

#### 2.2.3. Attic

- USE poster (the one with Succubus)
  - **NOTE:** `Tzuko`

#### 2.2.4. Basement

##### 2.2.4.1. Pentagram Room

- USE `Necrostaff Spell` on Necrostaff

#### 2.2.5. First Floor

##### 2.2.5.1. Office

- USE PC (`Succubus Binding Spell`)

## 3. Street

- PICK UP flyer (the blue one on the right) (`HRDL Poster`)

### 3.1. Necromancer House

#### 3.1.1. Basement

##### 3.1.1.1. Pentagram Room

- USE `HRDL Poster` on torch
- USE `Basement Key` on door to the stairs
- USE `Succubus Binding Spell` on Succubus
  - "Obey me Tzuko!"
- USE pentagram
- USE `Basement Key` on door to the stairs

#### 3.1.2. First Floor

##### 3.1.2.1. Reception

- USE button
- USE machine gun

## 4. Street

### 4.1. Orks Warshop

- TALK TO Jarukhai

### 4.2. Necromancer House

#### 4.2.1. Attic

- USE `Crowbar` on big box
- USE MG in big box (`Maxim-08/15`)

#### 4.2.2. First Floor

##### 4.2.2.1. Reception

- USE `Gun Repair Kit` on machine gun (`MG6x12`)
he warshop again. Give the machine gun to the shopkeeper and he’ll have the ammo for the MG delivered at your house.

### 4.3. Orks Warshop

- USE `MG6x12` on Jarukhai

### 4.4. Necromancer House

#### 4.4.1. First Floor

##### 4.4.1.1. Office

- EXIT to the street

## 5. Street

- USE `Pistol` on Necromancer
