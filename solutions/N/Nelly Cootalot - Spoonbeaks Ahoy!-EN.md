# Nelly Cootalot: Spoonbeaks Ahoy! - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 21.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/Nelly_Cootalot_Spoonbeaks_Ahoy_18)

## 1. Barnacle & Harbour

### 1.1. Beach

- ENTER the Barnacle

### 1.2. Barnacle

- PICK UP map (from bar) (`Map`)
- TALK TO Barman Friday
- TALK TO Captain Rehab
- USE `Map`

## 2. Market Street

### 2.1. Street

- ENTER Bjorn & Olafssen

### 2.2. Bjorn & Olafssen

- TALK TO Bjorn & Olafssen
- EXIT east

### 2.3. Street

- ENTER Store of Babylon

### 2.4. The Store of Babylon

- TALK TO Mme Leatherette
- TALK TO Baron
- When Baron is reading his own book:
  - PICK UP ink well from table (`Quill`, `Ink`)
- USE bargain bin (on the right side) (`Book`)
- EXIT west
- USE `Map`

## 3. Toll Booth

- TALK TO Blind Jimmy Thimble
- LOOK AT box
- PICK UP box (`Heavy Rock`)

## 4. Market Street

### 4.1. Bjorn & Olafssen

- USE `Book` on Coot

### 4.2. The Store of Babylon

- TALK TO Baron
  - "I hear you have the Saul Island gate pass..."
  - "Where is your tower..."

### 4.3. Street

- TALK TO Baroness

## 5. Widebeard Tower

### 5.1. Hill

- LOOK AT window (left from the tower door)
  - LOOK AT safe
  - EXIT by using the arrow in the lower right corner

## 7. Market Street

### 7.1. Bjorn & Olafssen

- LOOK AT tattoo stand
  - USE skull and crossbones tattoo (top right) (`Tattoo`)

## 8. Widebeard Tower

### 8.1. Hill

- LOOK AT lone grave (on left)
- USE `Spade` on lone grave (`Phrase Book`)
- LOOK AT `Phrase Book`

## 9. Barnacle & Harbour

### 9.1. Beach

- LOOK AT graffiti
  - You can swap two letters by clicking in them
  - Try to decode the message
  - ```
    I SWEAR BY GRAVES 'NEATH FOAM O'WAVES,
    OH I AM A PIRATE SO TRUE,
    AND I SWEAR ON ME SHIPS AND THE CAT-O-NINE WHIPS,
    I'LL DO WHAT A PIRATE MUST DO,
    AND I SAY BY THE SKY AND ME ONLY GOOD EYE,
    I SEEK OUT ME TREASURE SO FAIR,
    AND I'LL SAY TO BREEZE AND THE SE'EN HIGH SEAS,
    THAT I WON'T TRIM MY BODILY HAIR!
    ```
  - CLICK on "Read"

### 9.2. Barnacle

- TALK TO Captain Rehab
  - "I know the Pirates' Oath!"
- USE `Tattoo` on Barman Friday
- USE `Tattoo` on Captain Rehab
  - "I have the Pirate Guild Tattoo!" (`Cat Burglar`)

## 10. Widebeard Tower

### 10.1. Hill

- LOOK AT window (left from the tower door)
  - USE `Ink` on fishtank (`Pass`)

## 11. Toll Booth

- TALK TO Blind Jimmy Thimble
  - "I have the gate pass!"
- EXIT north

## 12. Leper Colony

### 12.1. Entrance

- EXIT west

## 13. Saul Mine

- TALK TO El Mono
- ENTER mine (does not work)
- EXIT east

## 14. Leper Colony

### 14.1. Entrance

- TALK TO window (on the left side of the door) **2 times**
- TALK TO Leprechaun (`Flag Blueprint`, `Daalder`, `Daalder`)

## 15. Barnacle & Harbour

### 15.1. Barnacle

- USE `Daalder` on donation box
- EXIT east

#### 15.1.1. Rogues' Gallery

- PICK UP postcards (`Postcard`)
- PICK UP moustache (from skeleton) (`Moustache`)

## 16. Market Street

### 16.1. Bjorn & Olafssen

- USE `Postcard` on Bjorn & Olafssen
- USE `Quill` on `Postcard` (`Signed Postcard`)
- USE `Signed Postcard` on Bjorn & Olafssen (`iHook`)
- EXIT

### 16.2. Street

- USE `Daalder` on Stibble Bellwise
  - "Let's do it!"
  - PICK UP hook (`Hook`)
  - USE `Hook` on ducks (you will fail)
- TALK TO Stibble Bellwise
  - "The game is rigged!" (`Daalder`)
- PLAY again but this time:
  - USE `iHook` on `Hook` (`Magnetic Hook`)
  - USE `Magnetic Hook` on ducks (on the hook of their head)
    - You need to catch seven ducks, then click beside the ducks to finish (`Rainbow Scarf`)

## 17. Widebeard Tower

### 17.1. Hill

- EXIT upstairs

### 17.2. Top of Tower

- TALK TO Hortense
- USE `Cat Burglar` on Hortense (does not work)

## 18. Barnacle & Harbour

### 18.1. Barnacle

- TALK TO Barman Friday
  - "Captain Rehab offered to buy me a drink."
  - "Cat Guts." (`Cat Guts`)
- USE `Cat Guts` on `Cat Burglar` (`Fierce Cat Burglar`)

## 19. Widebeard Tower

### 19.1. Top of Tower

- USE `Fierce Cat Burglar` on Hortense (`Four Leaf Clover`)
- EXIT downstairs

### 19.2. Hill

- PICK UP envelope (from ground) (`Letter to the DLA`)
- LOOK AT `Letter to the DLA`

### 19.3. Top of Tower

- TALK TO Hortense
  - "Has Angelo arrived yet?"
  - "What's Angelo like?"
- USE `Moustache`
- TALK TO Hortense
- USE `Heavy Rock` on pipe (below the dignified ladies) **2 times**
- TALK TO pipe (`Voucher`)

## 21. Leper Colony

### 21.1. Entrance

- USE `Rainbow Scarf` on `Underthings` (`Underthings & Scarf`)

- USE `Flag` on Leprechaun

## 22. Saul Mine

- PICK UP orange (`Orange`)
- LOOK AT pipe (from the furnace)
- USE `Heavy Rock` on pipe
- USE `Orange` on pipe
- ENTER mine

### 22.1. Mine

- PICK UP loose stones (on the left side)
  - "I can help you."
  - "I could give you information..."
  - "I'll tell you about pirates."
  - "Pirates have deep secrets..."
  - "Secret Codes..."
- PICK UP loose stones (`Stones`)
- USE crank handle
- USE `Stones` on bell
- **QUICK:**
  - USE crank handle
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE control panel
  - This riddle is quite confusing. You need to position the magnet right above the Baron.
  - The vertical lever chooses four different movements
  - You start the movements with the lower right button
  - From the starting position, USE the following lever position, numbered from top (1) to bottom (4) position. After each setting PUSH the bottom right button:
    - `2, 3, 1, 1, 1, 3, 2, 1`
  - USE red button
