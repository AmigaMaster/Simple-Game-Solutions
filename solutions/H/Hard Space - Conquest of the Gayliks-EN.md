## 1. Notes

- This walkthrough tries to cover all three difficulty levels
- You can reach Rank B on easy but the way for Rank A on the harder levels is missing
- On the easiest difficulty level, you cannot get more than the silver rating

## 2. I.S.S. Penetrator

### 2.1. Bridge

- USE (right-click) `WristCom`
  - Communicator
    - "Captain's Log"
  - EXIT with the red cross button
- QUIP TO Lt. Scatman
  - "Were you just playing StarTris?"
  - "I want that android!"
  - "The Transputter's broken AGAIN?"
- WALK to heart shaped door
- INTERACT WITH lift controls

### 2.2. Transputter Room

- SLUG circuitry panel (Achievement: Slugfest 1/4)
  - You need to move the 3x3 circuit fields to the correct positions
  - Note the x y and z tiles in the grid
  - For each axis you must set up the grid so that the power only reaches one of the three targets
  - But you musst keep the grid integrity
  - When we number the 3x3 fields from top to bottom and left to right:
  - X-Axis:
    - 9 up **2 times**
    - 6 left
    - 9 left
    - 9 up
    - USE blue button (CHANGE AXIS) to select the X-axis
    - USE yellow button (RUN DIAGNOSTIC) (x = 7)
  - Y-Axis:
    - **NOTE:** If you play on easiest difficulty setting, the puzzle wil reset to the beginning. In this case, place the tiles as described in X-Axis, then continue with the following:
    - 9 left, up
    - CHANGE AXIS to select the y axis
    - RUN DIAGNOSTIC (y=5)
  - Z-Axis
    - **NOTE:** Again, when selecting the easiest difficulty setting, you have to repeat all steps from x AND z axis and continue with:
    - 6 left **2 times**
    - 5 right, up
    - 6 down
    - CHANGE AXIS to select the z axis
    - RUN DIAGNOSTIC (z=6)
  - Return the grid to the initial layout to connect all the axis by reversing the moves:
    - 9 up
    - 3 down, left
    - 4 right **2 times**
    - 5 down, right
    - 6 down
    - 8 right
    - 5 right
    - 3 down **2 times**
  - USE green button (commit changes)
- INTERACT WITH transporter controls (`Order Spunk`)

## 3. Fallux V

### 3.1. Transputter Point

- EXIT southeast

### 3.2. Collapsed Bridge

- **NOTE:** You find this location in the east
- USE `WristCom`
  - Communicator
    - "Call Ramsey" (`Order Ramsey`)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Rash
  >- Shoot the statue before analyzing it
- USE `Order Spunk` on manly statue
- USE `Sputa Stunner` on manly statue
- USE `WristCom`
  - Communicator
    - "Captain's Log"
- EXIT east

### 3.3. Village

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- WALK east
  - "State your position"
  - "Question the captors"
  - "Ask about ruined village"
  - "Ask about judgment"
  - "Demand a chance to defend yourself"

### 3.4. The Arena of Truth

#### 3.4.1. Easy and hard difficulty level

- **QUICK:**
  - QUIP Depantroclus
    - "Insult religion"
    - "Question concept of order"
    - "Challenge logic"
    - "Challenge him directly"
  - INTERACT WITH your pants (`Your Pants!`)
  - USE `Your Pants!` (`Sputa Stunner`, `ISC Badge`, `Order Spunk`, `Order Ramsey`)
  - INTERACT WITH dirk (on the floor) (`Dirk`)
  - USE `Dirk` with impressive codpiece (`Nice Codpiece`)
  - INTERACT WITH loose stones (`Stones`)
  - USE `Stones` on `Nice Codpiece`
  - USE `Nice Codpiece` on **both** gongs
  - USE `Sputa Stunner` on creature
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Pantsed!
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Lothario
  >- USE `WristCom`
  >   - Communicator
  >   - "Captain's Log"

#### 3.4.2. Hardest Difficulty Level

- **QUICK:**
  - INTERACT WITH dirk (on the floor) (`Dirk`)
  - USE `Dirk` with impressive codpiece (`Nice Codpiece`)
  - INTERACT WITH loose stones (`Stones`)
  - INTERACT WITH spear shaft (in the middle of the arena) (`Shaft`)
  - USE `Stones` on `Nice Codpiece`
  - USE `Dirk` on `Shaft` (`Rearslayer?`)
  - WALK near the growlikin
  - >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Moxie!
    >- USE `Rearslayer?` on growlikin
- INTERACT WITH your pants (`Your Pants!`)
- USE `Your Pants!` (`Sputa Stunner`, `ISC Badge`, `Order Spunk`, `Order Ramsey`)

#### 3.4.3. All Difficulty Levels

- USE `WristCom`
  - Communicator
    - "Captain's Log"
- SLUG Dr. Ramsey (Achievement: Slugfest 2/4)
- INTERACT WITH Laya Foxana
  - You can freely talk to her
  - At last, "Could you take us to your Temple now?"
- Return to this location
- SLUG growlikin (Achievement: Slugfest 3/4)

### 3.5. Mysterious temple

- **NOTE:** Located in the northern lush jungle

#### 3.5.1. Path to Temple

- EXIT up the path

#### 3.5.2. Temple

- USE `Order Spunk` on strange crystal (in the hands of the statue)
- USE `Order Spunk` on ancient records (on the left pillar beside the strange device)
  - **NOTE:** The colors mentioned in the text:
    - Purple, Green, White, Red, Yellow
- INTERACT WITH strange device
  - Color the planets according to Spunk's translation. From inside to outside:
    1. yellow
    2. red
    3. white
    4. green
    5. purple
- For middle and hard difficulty level:
  - USE `Order Spunk` on ancient records (on the right pillar)
    - **NOTE:** `Planet` of Fallux, race that multiplied `people`, massive `comet`, turned to our devices `machine`, weak `orbit`
  - USE (right) strange device
    - Change the order of the symbols:
      1. Planet (A circle with lines above and beneath)
      2. People (2 tall and 1 small people)
      3. Comet (Circle in the lower left with three lines)
      4. Machine (two people with a circle in the middle)
      5. Orbit (circle with 3 orbiting circles and a circle in the lower left)
  - >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Versatile
- USE `WristCom`
  - Communicator
    - "Captain's Log"
- EXIT south **2 times**

### 3.6. Ruin

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- ENTER opening
  - There are now different paths:

#### 3.6.1. Laya Foxana

- WALK TO the left
- INTERACT WITH Computer Cycle A
  - You need to get the column on the right (purple) to the maximum value by changing the values of the others
    - SELECT any of the P, D, M, E bars and keep pressing on the down button until nothing changes anymore
    - Repeat this speedily und see the purple power raising
- Continue with the "you go first" chapter at the part with the upload terminal

#### 3.6.2. Ensign Spears

- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Secursity
- Continue with the "you go first" chapter
  - **NOTE:** You will get fired with this decision when you play on the easiest difficulty level

#### 3.6.3. You go First

- You can get one of the two achievements:
- QUIP with Gaylik Go
  - >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Slugfest 4/4
    >- Slug Gaylik
  - >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Tactician
    >- "Use tact"
    >- "Why to destroy all..."
    >- "Harmony? Man would still kill..."
    >- "Question Gaylik logic"
    >- "Attack purpose"
- WALK to the left
- INTERACT WITH cables running into the upload terminal
- INTERACT WITH upload terminal
  - USE red evil looking chip (the red one at the lower right)
  - When we number the panels from left to right an top to bottom turn the following ones:
    1. panel 4 **3 times**
    2. panel 8 **2 times**
    3. panel 5
    4. panel 9 **2 times**
    5. panel 10 **2 times**
    6. panel 13 **2 times**
    7. panel 14 **2 times**
    8. panel 15
    9. panel 16 **2 times** (it's now connected)
    10. panel 12 to interrupt the connection
- There are different choices again:
  1. Tell him to shut down
     - >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Rank B
  2. Tell him to stay active
     - >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Rank C
  3. Tell Laya it's her decision
     - **NOTE:** This will lower your rank
- "Talk about feelings"
- "Kiss her!"
- >**ACHIEVEMENT:** Amicable

## 6. Ways to Die

- Teleport on the planet when the transputter is not calibrated correctly
- Shoot the statue before analyzing it
- Order defensive fire during your arrest in the town
- Have Ensign Spears killed at the bridge and order defensive fire during your arrest
- Get killed by the beast in the arena
- Let Ensign Spears enters the ruins first
- Fire your Sputta Stunner at the Gaylik
- After you’ve told the Gaylik that you're his worst nightmare, answer “Yes!” to his question
- Let Laya enters the ruins first and fire your Sputta Stunner at the Gaylik when he's ignoring you
- During the final sequence, let the countdown reach 0
