# Hitchhiker's Guide To The Galaxy Remake - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 22.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/the-hitchhikers-guide-to-the-galaxy-remake)

## 1. Earth

### 1.1. Dent's House

#### 1.1.1. Inside House

- USE lamp (1/1)
- PICK UP gown (from chair) (`Your Gown`) (3/4)
- Right-click to open your inventory
  - WEAR `Your Gown` (1/5)
  - SEARCH `Your Gown`
  - CONTENTS `Your Gown`
  - CONSUME `Buffered analgesic` (4/9)
  - EXIT inventory
- PICK UP screwdriver (under the bed) (`Screwdriver`) (1/10)
- PICK UP toothbrush (`Toothbrush`) (1/11)
- EXIT southeast

#### 1.1.2. Porch

- PICK UP junk mail (`Junkmail`) (2/13)
- EXIT south

#### 1.1.3. Outside

- LIE IN mud (2/15)
- EXAMINE mud
- Wait
- TALK TO Ford
  - "What about my home?" (5/19)
- EXIT north

### 1.2. Country Lane

- ENTER pub

#### 1.2.1. Pub

- DRINK beer **3 times** (2+2+2/25)
- BROWSE shelf (behind the bar)
  - BUY sandwich (`Sandwich`) (3/28)
- EXIT southwest

### 1.3. Country Lane

- SELECT `Sandwich` and give it to the dog (5/33)
- EXIT west

### 1.4. Dent's House

#### 1.4.1. Outside

- EXIT south

#### 1.4.2. Porch

- PICK UP device (`THUMB`) (2/35)
- EXAMINE `THUMB`
- STICK OUT `THUMB`
  - CLICK green button (2/37)

## 2. Darkness

- SMELL darkness (you will notice a shadow)
- LOOK AT shadow (`Peanuts`) (2/39)

## 3. Vogon Spaceship

### 3.1. Hold

- EXAMINE `Peanuts`
- EAT `Peanuts` (2/41)
- USE `Your Gown` on hook (at the end of the chain hanging from the ceiling)
- PICK UP towel (`Towel`)
- PICK UP satchel (`Satchel`)
- USE `Satchel` on panel (left of the mattress)
- USE `Junkmail` on top of the satchel
- USE `Towel` on grate (in the middle of the room)
- PRESS BUTTON of dispenser (on the right) (22/63)
- PICK UP gown (`Your Gown`)
- PICK UP towel (`Towel`)
- PICK UP satchel (`Satchel`)
- SELECT `Towel`
  - Click on the `Satchel` and then while still having the `Towel` selected, click on the left side to put the `Towel` into the satchel
- PRESS switch (on the left glass case)
  - **NOTE:** The word from the poem `Third word from second verse of Captain's current favorite poem` (5/68)
- WAIT for the guard

### 3.2. Captain's Cabin

- "Enjoy the poetry." (5/73)
- "Unfortunately yes."
- "I really mean it."
  - **NOTE:** The word you require to open the case. The complete verse is:
    ```txt
    Bleem miserable venchit! Bleem forever mestinglish asunder frapt.
    Fripping l/Iyshus wimbgunts, awhilst moongrovenly kormzibs.
    Gashee morphousite, though expungiest quoopisk!
    Gerond withoutitude form into formless blait, why not then? Moose.
    ```

### 3.3. Hold

- TYPE keyboard (at the glass case on the left)
  - ENTER the word from the poem `venchit` (5/78)
- PICK UP plotter (`Atomic Plotter`) (5/83)

### 3.4. Airlock

- STICK OUT `THUMB`
  - CLICK green button (6/89)

## 5. Heart of Gold

### 5.1. Entry Bay 2

- OPEN to corridor (the door in the back)

### 5.2. Bridge

- PUT `Hitchhiker's Guide` in `Satchel`
- PICK UP handbag (`Handbag`) (1/92)
- PUT `Toothbrush` in `Handbag`
- PICK UP molecular hyperwave pincer (`Molecular Pincer`) (1/93)
- PUT `Molecular Pincer` in `Your Gown`
- EXIT east

### 5.3. Upper Corridor

- ENTER galley

### 5.4. Galley

- PICK UP carton (`Carton`, `Strange Gun`) (1/94)
- ORDER pad (to order a tea substitute) (2/96)
- PUT `Screwdriver` in `Carton`
- PUT `THUMB` in `Carton`
- PICK UP substitute (`Adv. Tea Substitute`) (1/97)
- PUT `Adv. Tea Substitute` in `Carton`
- OPEN to corridor door

### 5.5. Upper Corridor

- EXIT south

### 5.6. Lower Corridor

- ENTER engine room **5 times**

### 5.7. Engine Room

- CLICK light switch (on the left) **3 times** (6/103)
- PICK UP ionic rasp (on the floor to the right) (`Ionic Diffusion Rasp`) (5/108)
- PUT `Ionic Diffusion Rasp` in `Carton`
- PICK UP hypersonic pliers (`Pliers`) (5/113)
- PUT `Pliers` in `Carton`
- PICK UP generator (`Improbability Drive`) (4/117)
- EXIT west, northwest **2 times**

### 5.8. Bridge

- SELECT `Atomic Plotter` and put it in the console
- OPERATE atomic plotter
  - SELECT `Improbability Drive` and put it on the small receptacle (on the lower right part of the atomic plotter)
  - SELECT `Adv. Tea Substitute` (you need to remove it from the `Carton` first) and put it onto the long dangly bit (from the plotter)
  - USE switch (on the right)

## 19. War Chamber

### 19.1. Strategic Room

- PICK UP awl (`Ultra-plasmic Awl`) (1/218)
- WAIT until you are transported

### 19.2. Space Room

- INTERACT with one object (the hotspot names don't match) and **NOTE** the object you interacted with (for example the hotspot called "window") - the hotspot name vanishes
  - Now INTERACT with the object you had interacted before (in this example, interact with the window on the left side) - the hotspot names swap
- Do the same for the following objects:
  1. Window
  2. Puddle
  3. Flowerpot
  4. Book
  5. Bookcase
- ENTER door (at the ceiling)
- PICK UP particle (on the TV under the flowerpot) (`Black Particle`) (15/233)
