# Frank the Farmhand: Part 2 - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 05.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/frank-the-farmhand-part-2)

## 1. Military Camp

### 1.1. Prison Cell

- TALK TO prisoner
- TALK TO guard
- USE window

### 1.2. Courtyard

- ENTER (right) tent

### 1.3. Tent

- PICK UP hedge-cutters (`Hedge-Cutters`)
- EXIT west

### 1.4. Courtyard

- USE `Hedge-Cutters` with fence
- EXIT east

## 2. Village

- In the south of the island

### 2.1. Village Square

- TALK TO carpenter (Mountain Pass)
- PICK UP chisel and mallet (`Chisel and Mallet`)
- EXIT east

### 2.2. Beach

- PICK UP thing in the sand (in the lower left part, looks like the bottom half of a glass or bottle) (`Jar`)
- TALK TO girl
- TALK TO boy
- EXIT west

### 2.3. Village Square

- EXIT northwest

## 3. Mountain Pass

- Between the two mountains in the northwest

### 3.1. Pass

- EXIT southeast

## 4. Archaeological Site

- PICK UP termites (from the tree on the right) (`Termites`)
- TALK TO archaeologist (Archie)
- PICK UP note (from the table) (`Recipe`)
- LOOK AT `Recipe`
- PICK UP charcoal (from the table, a tiny hotspot) (`Charcoal`)
- LOOK AT pile of dirt
- USE pile of dirt (`Magnifying Glass`)
- LOOK AT top of hill (the green hill right next to the mountains)
- EXIT east

## 5. Hill

### 5.1. Outside

- PICK UP long leafed plants (`Cannabis`)
- PICK UP wunderbaum (from the wall) (`Wunderbaum`)
- ENTER hut **2 times** (`Plate of Food`)

### 5.2. Hut

- When he's not throwing:
  - TALK TO Clifford
- EXIT south, west

## 6. Mountain Pass

### 6.1. Pass

- USE `Wunderbaum` with cave entrance
- ENTER cave entrance

#### 6.1.1. Cave

- USE `Plate of Food` with `Jar`
- USE `Jar` with ground (under the green sulphur)
- USE `Plate of Food` with ground
- PICK UP pink creature (`Pink Creature`)
- PICK UP sulphur (`Powdered Sulphur`)
- EXIT north

### 6.2. Pass

- EXIT northwest

## 7. Village

### 7.1. Beach

- GIVE `Pink Creature` to girl (`Book`)

## 8. Hill

### 8.1. Outside

- EXIT behind hut (left from the hut)

### 8.2. Behind Hut

- PICK UP iron rod (on the ground in front of the tree) (`Iron Rod`)
- LOOK AT coconut-man
- EXIT northwest, ENTER hut

### 8.3. Hut

- GIVE `Book` to Clifford
- TALK TO Clifford
  - "Can I have that dart?" (`Dart`)
  - "What's up with that smiling coconut-man..."
- PICK UP firewood (`Log`)
- USE `Cannabis` with `Termites`
- USE `Termites` with `Log` (`Sawdust`)

### 8.4. Behind Hut

- USE `Charcoal` with coconut-man
- **QUICK:** WALK into the hut

### 8.5. Hut

- **QUICK:**
  - PICK UP bottle of oil (from the fireplace)

## 9. Archaeological Site

- USE bowl (on the table) (`Bomb`)

## 10. Mountain Pass

### 10.1. Pass

- PICK UP tape (on the mountain wall left of the cave entrance) (`Piece of Duct Tape`)
- USE `Bomb` with debris
- USE `Magnifying Glass` with bomb
- EXIT northwest

## 11. Village

### 11.1. Beach

- USE `Dart` with boy
- EXIT west and return here
- PICK UP inflatable snake (`Inflatable Snake`)
- USE `Inflatable Snake` with `Piece of Duct Tape`

## 12. Military Camp

### 12.1. Courtyard

- USE window

## 13. Prison Cell

- USE `Inflatable Snake` with Jim
- USE `Chisel and Mallet` with door
- OPEN door

## 14. Airplane

- EXIT west

## 15. Secret Door

- EXIT west

## 16. Airplane

- PUSH green button
- USE `Iron Rod` with coil (`Magnetized Rod`)

## 17. Village

### 17.1. Village Square

- USE pulley
- USE `Magnetized Rod` with rope
- USE pulley **2 times**
- PICK UP iron rod (`Key`, `Magnetized Rod`)

## 18. Mountain Pass

### 18.1. Pass

- ENTER cave entrance and EXIT (to prevent a bug where you cannot walk to the Archaeological Site anymore and end up here in the pass)

## 19. Archaeological Site

- PICK UP fossilized bone (the smaller bone in the dig site on the left of the skeleton, the lowest of the three bones) (`Fossilized Dino-Bone`)

## 20. Secret Door

- USE `Fossilized Dino-Bone` with guard dog
- USE `Key` with keyhole

### 20.1. th Level

#### 20.1.1. Southwestern Corridor

##### 20.1.1.1. Torture Chamber

- "Please don't hurt us!"
- "Please don't hurt Ben at least!"
- When Max operates the console:
  - USE valve
- "Just go ahead and do it! Kill me!"
- "I bet you don't dare to juggle with that chainsaw..."
- USE Max (`Security Card`, `Security Card`)
- PICK UP chainsaw (`Spark Plug`)
- OPEN door
- EXIT north

#### 20.1.2. Southwestern Corridor

- EXIT north

#### 20.1.3. Western Corridor

- USE elevator
  - SELECT 2

### 20.2. nd Level

#### 20.2.1. Western Corridor

- EXIT south, east

#### 20.2.2. Southern Corridor

- LOOK AT (right) grid (it's loose)
- USE (right) grid
- PICK UP card (`Security Card`)

#### 20.2.3. Western Corridor

- USE elevator

### 20.3. th Level

- PICK UP brochure (on the left corner on the console) (`Brochure`)
- LOOK AT `Brochure` (`Chewing Gum`)
- USE elevator

### 20.4. nd Level

#### 20.4.1. Western Corridor

- EXIT north

#### 20.4.2. Northern Corridor

- USE `Chewing Gum` with Frank (`Chewed Gum`)
- USE `Chewed Gum` with card reader
- EXIT west and return here - the cleaning bot appears
