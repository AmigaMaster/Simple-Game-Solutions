# Featherweight - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 06.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/featherweight-2009)

## 1. Crashed Vehicle

### 1.1. Tower

- USE backpack (behind Callaghan) (`Wrench`)
- ENTER door

## 2. Crashed Vehicle

- USE shockprod (below the entrance to the tower) (`Cartridge`)
- USE `Wrench` on axle (on the vehicle)
- PICK UP axle (`Axle`)
- USE Tire (Mistyped: Tyre) (`Inner Tube`)
- USE `Inner Tube` on `Axle` (`Axle + Inner Tube`)
- USE `Axle + Inner Tube` on device (at the lamp post)
- **QUICK:**
  - USE vehicle

## 3. If you get caught

### 3.1. Cell

- USE `Axle + Inner Tube` on keypad
  - top
  - bottom
  - WAIT until the light extinguishes
  - top
  - top
  - WAIT until the light extinguishes
  - bottom

### 3.2. Bridge

- WAIT until the sentry looks to the right, then **QUICKLY:**
  - WALK TO rubble
- PICK UP rag (on top of the rubble) (`Rag`)
- WAIT until the sentry looks to the left, then **QUICKLY:**
  - USE `Rag` on sheet of tin (on the ground to the right)
- WAIT until the sentry looks to the left, then **QUICKLY:**
  - WALK TO sign

## 4. Crashed Vehicle

- EXIT east

## 5. Crane

- USE buttons (on the machine on the left side)
  - 1st (Left): Middle (Green light)
  - 2nd: Top (broken light)
  - 3rd: second lowest (green light)
  - 4th: Bottom (green light)
  - 5th: second from the top (broken light) - Ths sentry is put into a lower position
- USE sentry (`Card`)
- EXIT east

## 6. Wall

- USE `Card` on box (at the pole with the screen on the top)
- USE panel (on the door)
  - **NOTE:** The cables from the pole to the wall beside the panel
  - When the top is lit (blue light), **NOTE** the sign on the pole screen and check which part of the triangle is missing
  - USE top button to set the symbol to the missing part
  - **REPEAT** fot the other two cables
  - Solution:
    1. Left baseline
    2. Upper right line
    3. Lower left line
- ENTER doorway

## 7. Sentry with Gun

- WAIT until the sentry looks to the right, then **QUICKLY:**
  - WALK TO crates
- USE backpack (on the right side of the crates) (`Wire`, `Scope`, `Pocket Watch`)
- USE shaped charge (on he left side on the crates) (`Shaped Charge`)
- WAIT until the sentry looks to the left, then **QUICKLY:**
  - WALK TO barrel on the left
- EXIT west

## 8. Wall

- USE bar (in the debris in the middle)
- USE `Wire` on `Pocket Watch` (`Watch + Wire`)
- USE `Cartridge` on `Watch + Wire` (`Detonator`)
- USE `Detonator` on `Shaped Charge` (`Bomb`)
- USE `Bomb` on debris
- USE `Axle + Inner Tube` on bar
- PICK UP bar (`Bar`)
- EXIT west

## 9. Crane

- USE bar on crate
- USE crate (`Bullet`)
- EXIT east **2 times**

## 10. Sentry with Gun

- WAIT until the sentry looks to the right, then **QUICKLY:**
  - WALK TO crates
- WAIT until the sentry looks to the left, then **QUICKLY:**
  - USE `Bullet` on barrel
  - EXIT east

## 11. Forcefield-Device

- USE the three control levers to rotate and align the three rings
  - The markings of all three rings must point to the lower right
- USE `Scope` on light beam
  - Start at the upper right position (one blue light is lit now)
  - Anti-clockwise **3 times**
  - Clockwise **2 times**
  - Anti-clockwise **4 times**
