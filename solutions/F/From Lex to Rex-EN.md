## 1. Sewer Entrance

- USE a round thing (in the grey part on the ground on the left) (`Truffle (3)`)
- USE `Truffle (3)` on hole (`Truffle (2)`)
- USE `Truffle (2)` on sewer (`Truffle`)
- ENTER sewer

### 1.1. Sewers

- PICK UP rake (`One half of the rake`, `The other half`)
- USE `One half of the rake` on remains (`One half of the rake (adhesive)`)
- USE `The other half` on writing (`The other half (hardener)`)
- USE `One half of the rake (adhesive)` on `The other half (hardener)` (`Rake`)
- USE `Rake` on crack (between the second to last and last stone) (`Half rake`)
- PICK UP corner (of the fire poster) (`Music sheet`)
- TALK TO someone reading
- USE `Half rake` on bucket full of clean water
- USE `Music sheet` on gutter
- USE drain

## 2. Castle

#### 2.0.1. Princess Room

- USE `Half rake` on princess
- Go back
- USE window
- USE a very pink bed (`Bitten biscuit`, `Candle`)
- USE `Half rake` on toad (`Toad`)
- USE braid
- USE `Toad` on go back
- Exit
- USE `Bitten biscuit` on latrine

### 2.1. Hallway

- USE (big) vase
- PICK UP petals (`Petals`)
- USE candle
- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Half rake` on candle (`Waxed half rake`)
- USE `Petals` on `Waxed half rake` (`"Bunch of flowers"`)
- USE `"Bunch of flowers"` on (small, empty) vase (hanging on the wall)
- USE door next to the vases

#### 2.1.1. Kitchen

- TALK TO the chef
  - **NOTE:** The hints
    - When there is wind, he won't drink any beer
    - On hunting days (he hunts werewolves), he eats meat
    - He likes wine and meatpie
    - Dessert is always ice cream but he wants three, four or five balls
- LOOK AT the moon
  - On full moon is hunting day (because he hunts werewolves)
- LOOK AT flan
  - When there is wind, the flan wobbles in the wind
- LOOK AT star constellation
  - Count the number of stars to get the number of ice balls
- The solution differs for each playthrough, when you got all information:
  - TALK TO the chef and choose the correct answers ()
  - EXIT kitchen (`Chef's cloth`, `2 bits censor`)
    - **NOTE:** If you fail one part, you cannot exit the kitchen and can try again

### 2.2. Under King's Chamber

- LOOK AT glued paper
  - **NOTE:** `555 - 5368`
- USE candle on glued paper (`Lighted candle`)
- USE `Lighted candle` on **lower** end of rope (`My old clothes`)

### 2.3. King's Chamber

- WALK east
- PICK UP piece of metal (on the ground in the left bottom) (`Metal scrap`)
- PICK UP bottle (from the scales in the right cup) (`Bottle of oil`)
- WALK west
- USE `Metal scrap` on horrific head of a werewolf (on the wall)
- WALK east
- USE sliding furniture
  - TALK TO blue guard
- WALK west
- TALK To blue guard

### 2.4. Kitchen

- USE salad
- USE cheese
- USE breakfast
- USE glass of wine
- USE pork knuckle (`Tough bone`)
- USE roast chicken
- USE metal jar (`Beautiful jug`)
- USE jar
- USE egg flan (on the shelf under the window)
- USE delicious honey (`Sticky and delicious honey`)
- USE cake (`Polished and shiny tray`)

#### 2.4.1. Chimney

- USE `Chef's cloth` on wall (`Ball of wool`)
- USE `Ball of wool` on `Tough bone` (`Improvised rope`)
- USE `Improvised rope` on `Sticky and delicious honey` (`Sticky ball of wool`)
- USE `Sticky ball of wool` on flue
- USE `Beautiful jug` on chef

### 2.5. Rooftop

- USE `Polished and shiny tray` on prince
- LOOK AT cool sticker (on the wall above the grid)
  - **NOTE:** `Point at what shines where everything shines`
- PICK UP arrows (`Arrow`)
- USE `Arrow` on shining star
  - **NOTE:** This is a very small hotspot in the sky which is only clickable when you have looked at the cool sticker AND did not use the `Arrow` on the hearts. If you already have the heart arrow, USE nailed iron nail to the left of the cool sticker to retry the scene
- ENTER secret area

### 2.6. Secret Area

- LOOK AT hanged recipe
  - **NOTE:** Eggs, flour, oil, cream. Mix together at strong tempo.
- PICK UP horrible omelette (from the table) (`Omelette (do not eat)`)
- PICK UP cask (`Cream`, `Bread scrap`)
- USE `Bread scrap` on (blue) cookbook
- LOOK AT musical cookbook
- USE `Cream` on funnel
- USE `Bread scrap` on funnel
- USE `Omelette (do not eat)` on funnel
- USE metronome **2 times** (second speed setting)
- USE piano-cocktail
- EXIT to rooftop

### 2.7. Rooftop

- PICK UP grass (`Rosemary`)
- USE `Arrow` on hearts (`Arrow heart`)
- USE `Arrow heart` on nailed iron nail (`Caramelised arrow`)
- USE `Caramelised arrow` on hearts (`Cotton candy`)
- USE grid

### 2.8. Changing Cubicle

- PICK UP tailer iron (on the bench on the left) (`Tailor iron`)
- USE `Tailor iron` on (right) wall
- USE right cotton **2 times** (the hand in the sewer)
- USE `Truffle` on my hand
- USE left cotton **3 times**
  - "Granted" (it now switches to the royal public worker)
- PICK UP something under a sleeve (in the bottom row of the shelf, in the white jacket) (`Permit A38`)
- USE `Permit A38` on royal public worker (`A14 (not A38)`)
- USE left cotton (the puddle)
- PICK UP truffle (`Truffle`)
- USE left cotton (princess room)
- PICK UP that damned wand (`Wand`)
- USE `Wand` on `A14 (not A38)` (`Green permit`)
- USE `Wand` on `Green permit` (`Red permit`)
- USE `Wand` on `Red permit` (`Purple permit`)
- USE left cotton (royal public worker)
- USE `Purple permit` on royal public worker
- USE left cotton **2 times** (vase)
- PICK UP pouch (`Pouch of seeds`)
- USE right cotton (the gardener Lex)
- USE `Pouch of seeds` on gardener Lex

### 2.9. King's Chamber

- "You were about to give me some seeds."
- PICK UP scroll (from the red bench) (`Fermented herring (Surströmming)`)
- USE `Fermented herring (Surströmming)` on rusted chain (above the red bench) (`Broken chain`)
- USE `Rosemary` on `Bottle of oil` (`Oil with rosemary`)
- USE Lex (hanged) (`Rosemary oil (shaken, not stirred)`)
- USE `Rosemary oil (shaken, not stirred)` on Lex (from portal)
- USE stand with book
- PICK UP portal (`Portal`)
- USE `2 bits censor` on Apocryphal (`Wooden spoon`)
- TALK TO red guard
  - "The fruit is the key!"
- USE `Wooden spoon` on crack (above yourself in the wall)
- USE `Broken chain` on (right) root (`Cookbook`)
- USE `Cookbook` on blue guard (`Censor wand`)
- USE `Censor wand` on `Portal` (`Charged wand`)
- USE `Charged wand` on royal public worker
- USE `Charged wand` on red guard
- USE `Charged wand` on gardener
- USE `Charged wand` on blue guard
- USE `Charged wand` on REX

## 3. Sewers

- USE `Wooden spoon` on swimming "pool" (the sewer's water)
- USE jump!
- USE `Truffle` on LEX
