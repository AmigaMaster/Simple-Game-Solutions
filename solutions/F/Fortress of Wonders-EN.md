# Fortress of Wonders - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 05.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/fortress-of-wonders)

## 1. Forest Path

- EXIT north

## 2. Fortress

### 2.1. Bodan's Study

- USE (left) door

### 2.2. Fortress Map

- ENTER Royal Bedroom

### 2.3. Bedroom

- TALK TO guard
- EXIT west

### 2.4. Balcony

- TALK TO Queen Jessica (`Bedroom Key`)
- USE door

### 2.5. Bedroom

- USE `Bedroom Key` on great door

#### 2.5.1. Bed

- USE (left) pillow
- PICK UP doll (`Doll`)
- LOOK AT floor
- EXIT east, west

### 2.6. Kitchen

- TALK TO cook (`Letter`)
- USE door

### 2.7. Great Halls

- TALK TO Duke
- USE `Letter` on Duke
- EXIT north

### 2.8. Kitchen

- TALK TO cook
- PICK UP meat (`Meat`)

### 2.9. Cellar

- **NOTE:** You can find this location on the map in the corner of the kitchen
- TALK TO ogre
- PICK UP empty flask (it has a small hotspot) (`Flask`)
- EXIT upstairs

### 2.10. Watchtower

- TALK TO guard
- While he is looking to the east:
  - **QUICK:** PICK UP horn (`Horn`)
- USE door

### 2.11. Armoury

- PICK UP knife (from the wooden doll) (`Knife`)
- TALK TO fighter (`Cloth`)

### 2.12. Garden

- PICK UP feather (on the right side of the lamp) (`Feather`)
- LOOK AT island (on the left of the waterfall)
- PICK UP strange plant (`Garlic`)
- EXIT east

### 2.13. Fortress Map

- EXIT south to the forest

## 3. Ariana's Hut

- PICK UP axe (`Axe`)
- EXIT west and walk to the southern forest

## 4. Forest

- TALK TO faerie
- USE `Axe` on branch
- PICK UP ripped branch (`Branch`)
- EXIT northwest

## 5. Ruins

- USE `Axe` on Dark Knight
- USE hole (at the lower part of the building in the middle) (`Treasure Chest`)
- EXIT west or east

## 6. Dragon Lands

- TALK TO Centauress
- EXIT west or east

## 7. Orc Village

- TALK TO Orc Chieftain
- USE `Horn` on Orc Chieftain (`Poison`)
- EXIT east

## 8. Fortress

### 8.1. Stables

- TALK TO hunter
- USE `Treasure Chest` on hunter
- USE cage door (on top of the cage)
- EXIT west

## 9. Dragon Lands

- TALK TO Centauress (`Amulett`)

## 10. Fortress

### 10.1. Stables

- TALK TO black chicken

### 10.2. Fortress Map

- WALK TO Bodan's Study (`List`)

### 10.3. Bodan's Study

- LOOK AT `List`

### 10.4. Library

- TALK TO scholar
- USE door

### 10.5. Great Halls

- TALK TO ranger
- EXIT east

## 11. Forest

- TALK TO faerie
  - Ask about unicorns
  - Ask about her
  - Ask about nature
  - Ask for mandrake root

## 12. Dragon Lands

- EXIT north

### 12.1. Dragon Mountains

- USE `Poison` on `Meat` (`Deadly Meal`)
- USE `Deadly Meal` on Wyvern
- USE `Axe` pn Wyvern (`Tongue`)
- EXIT east

## 13. Forest

- USE `Tongue` on faerie (`Mandrake Root`)

## 14. Graveyard

- PICK UP shovel (`Shovel`)
- TALK TO vampire
- USE `Garlic` on vampire
- USE crypt

### 14.1. Crypt

- USE coffin
- USE `Knife` on `Branch` (`Stake`)
- USE `Stake` on coffin
- USE `Flask` on coffin (`Vampire Blood`)
- EXIT north, east

## 15. Mountains

- USE Dragon's Carcass
- EXIT east

## 16. Fortress

### 16.1. Great Halls

- TALK TO ranger
  - "I found the dragon. He is dead."

### 16.2. Armoury

- TALK TO Guard Master

### 16.3. Library

- TALK TO book lizard
- TALK TO scholar
- TALK TO book lizard again
- USE shelf (`Scroll`)

## 17. Ariana's Hut

- USE `Key` on door

### 17.1. Hut

- TALK TO Gimli
- USE door

## 18. Skull Coast

- USE `Shovel` on Y marks the spot (front of the big rock) (`Unicorn Bone`)
- WALK TO the right
- LOOK AT `Scroll`
  - When questioned, answer "Water" (`Sword`)

## 19. Fortress

### 19.1. Armoury

- USE `Sword` on Guard Master (`Eye`)

### 19.2. Bodan's Study

- (`Potion`)
- EXIT study

### 19.3. Stables

- USE `Potion` on black chicken (`Royal Order`)

### 19.4. Cellar

- USE `Royal Order` on Ogre

### 19.5. Armoury

- TALK TO dwarf

### 19.6. Great Halls

- TALK TO Duke
  - When he does not want to talk to the cook, tell him about his little secret

### 19.7. Kitchen

- TALK TO cook
  - "Duke Flint sent me..."

### 19.8. Bodan's Study

- USE `Doll` on Bodan

### 19.9. Fortress Map

- ENTER Dungeons (middle far left)

### 19.10. Dungeons

- USE `Doll` on executioner
- TALK TO executioner
- USE dungeon door

#### 19.10.1. Lower Dungeons

- TALK TO Wraith
- EXIT upstairs

### 19.11. Dungeons

- TALK TO executioner
- USE torture bed (`Lever`)
- **ATTENTION:** Do not enter the secret passage in the lower dungeons or you will die and have to restore an ols savegame

### 19.12. Bodan's Study

- PICK UP spellbook (from the table) (`Spellbook`)

### 19.13. Dungeons

#### 19.13.1. Lower Dungeons

- USE `Lever` on hole (below the bones)
- ENTER secret passage
