## 1. Bedroom

- PICK UP matches (at the left window) (`Matches`)
- LOOK AT bed
  - PICK UP plank (`Plank`)
  - PICK UP springs (`Spring`)
  - EXIT bed
- USE `Spring` on carpet
- USE `Plank` on spring
- PICK UP spring (`Long Wire`)
- PICK UP shotgun (from the statue) (`Shotgun`)
- USE `Matches` on `Shotgun` (`Powdered Shotgun`)
- USE `Long Wire` on `Powdered Shotgun` (`Plugged Shotgun`)
- USE `Long Wire` on cabinet
- USE cabinet
- LOOK AT cabinet
  - PICK UP dirty socks (`Stinky Socks`)
  - PICK UP scarf (`Cotton Scarf`)
  - PICK UP raincoat (`Raincoat`)
  - LOOK AT raincoat (`Note`)
  - LOOK AT `Note`
    - **NOTE:** `20 right, 50 left, 3ß right`
  - USE secret button (on the right cabinet side)
  - PICK UP jewels (`Jewels`)
  - EXIT view
- USE `Jewels` on `Plugged Shotgun` (`Loaded Shotgun`)
- USE window (to open it)
- USE `Loaded Shotgun` on window
- USE `Plank` on Antonio the cat
- PICK UP Antonio the cat (`Antonio`)
- PICK UP feathers (`Feathers`)
- USE `Stinky Socks` on `Antonio`
- LOOK AT cabinet
  - PICK UP jewels (`Jewels`)
- USE `Matches` on `Shotgun` (`Powdered Shotgun`)
- USE `Long Wire` on `Powdered Shotgun` (`Plugged Shotgun`)
- USE `Jewels` on `Plugged Shotgun` (`Loaded Shotgun`)
- USE `Loaded Shotgun` on candelabra
- PICK UP splinter (`Sharp Splinter`)
- USE `Sharp Splinter` on rug
- USE `Note` on safe
- LOOK AT safe
