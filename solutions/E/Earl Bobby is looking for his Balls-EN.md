## 1. Teeing Ground

- PICK UP sign (`SIgn`)
- PICK UP (red) tee (`Tee`)
- PICK UP tee markers (`Tee markers`)
- PICK UP flowers (from the bushes) (`Red flower`)
- USE golf bag
  - PICK UP tee (`Tees`)
  - PICK UP key
  - EXIT view
- EXIT east

## 2. Pond

- TALK TO greenkeeper
  - "ball"
  - "cigarette"
  - "work"
  - "lake"
  - "hair"
  - "barkeeper"
  - "lawnmower"
  - "bees"
  - "branch"
  - "golf"
  - "bye"
- While he checks his lawnmower:
  - USE `Seven Iron` on greenkeeper
- LOOK AT lawn mower
  - PICK UP cigarettes (`Cigarettes`)
  - PICK UP long cord (`Wire`)
  - EXIT view
- LOOK AT pond
- PICK UP reed (`Reed stalk`)
- PICK UP water lilies (`Water lily`)
- EXIT west

## 3. Teeing Ground

- EXIT south

## 9. Bushes

## 10. Beehive

- USE `Matches` on `Cigarettes` (`Burning cigarette`)
- USE `Burning cigarette` on beehive

## 11. Island

- USE gigantic match
- WALK to the right
- USE butterflies
- USE gigantic match (to push it back to the left and ignite it)
- USE butterflies
- USE gigantic match
- USE butterflies (to get onto the middle island)
- WALK right and step onto the water lilies
- USE gigantic joint
- WALK left onto the water lilies
- USE gigantic joint (to push it back)
- WALK right onto the water lilies
- TALK TO gigantic joint
- USE trunk

## 12. Beehive

- USE `Sugar shaker` on beehive (`Honey`)
- USE `Toilet brush` on beehive (`Sticky toilet brush`)
- EXIT north

## 13. Pond

- USE `Reed stalk` on pond

### 13.1. Underwater

- PICK UP algae (`Algae`)
- USE `Seven Iron` on sleeping Nessie and walk east
  - **REPEAT** these steps until you are at the bomb
- USE flap
- PICK UP plutonium rod (`Plutonium rod`)
- EXIT west

## 14. Pond

- USE `Telescope` on mountain (behind the trees on the right)
- LOOK AT lawn mower
  - USE `Toilet chain` on blade
  - USE button
  - PICK UP blade (`Knife`)
  - PICK UP metal filings (`Metal filings`)
  - EXIT view
- EXIT east

## 15. Hole

- EXIT south

## 16. Tree

- USE `Knife` on branch (`Branch`)
- USE `Honey` on soft ground
- EXIT west **2 times**

## 17. Bushes

- PICK UP forked branch (in the bushes on the right) (`Forked branch`)
- EXIT east, north, north

## 18. Mountain

- USE `Sticky toilet brush` on `Algae` (`Customized toilet brush`)
- USE `Plunger` on `Customized toilet brush` (`Equipment`)
- USE `Equipment` on mountain

## 19. Top of Mountain

- USE `Algae` on `Elastic canvas` (`Balloon`)
- USE `Balloon` on crater
- FLY to the east at the height of the bird
  - RIGHT-CLICK to clap your hands when the bird is close to you
  - FLY further to the east and do the same for all birds
  - USE hook (to land)
- EXIT east

### 19.1. Birds Nest

- USE `Seven Iron` on bird
- USE `Seven Iron` on ball
- EXIT east

## 20. Top of Mountain

- USE balloon
- EXIT west **2 times**, south (on the right side of the pond), west **3 times**

## 21. Clubhouse

- USE `Metal filings` on ball cleaner
- USE `Woody's ball` on ball cleaner (`Weighted down ball`)
- ENTER clubhouse

### 21.1. Bar

- TALK TO Mary Hankingfire
- USE window (to her right)
- TALK TO barkeeper
  - "token" (`Token`)
  - "bye"
- EXIT north, south, east

## 22. Car Park

- LOOK AT Woody's car
- LOOK AT tire
- USE `Knife` on tire (`Piece of gum`)
- USE hood (of fat car)
- LOOK AT engine
  - USE `Token` on screw
  - PICK UP battery (`Car battery`)
  - EXIT view
- USE `Key` on trunk (of your car)
- LOOK AT trunk
  - PICK UP jewels (`Jewels`)
  - EXIT view
- USE `Piece of gum` on `Forked branch` (`Slingshot`)
- USE `Jewels` on `Slingshot`
- LOOK AT trunk
  - PICK UP jewels (`Jewels`)
  - EXIT view
- EXIT west

## 23. Driving Range

- LOOK AT machine
  - PICK UP ball baskets (`Ball basket`)
  - USE `Ball basket` on ball dispenser
  - USE `Token` on coin slot
  - PICK UP basket (`Balls`)
  - PICK UP ball baskets (`Ball basket`)
  - EXIT north
- LOOK AT window (of the clubhouse)
  - USE `Ball basket` on window
  - USE basket
    - USE `Slingshot` on Mary Hankingfire
    - USE `Slingshot` on silver plate
      - **NOTE:** You need to have moved the silver plate in order to hit Mary
    - EXIT the window und window view and enter the clubhouse
