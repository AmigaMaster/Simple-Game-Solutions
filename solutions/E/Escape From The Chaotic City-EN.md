# Escape From The Chaotic City - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 18.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/1290)

## 1. Home

### 1.1. Your Room

- PICK UP bat (`Bat`)
- EXIT right

### 1.2. Entrance Hall

- PICK UP newspaper
- OPEN middle door

## 2. Street

- ATTACK **both** zombies with `Bat`
- TALK TO man at the car
- PICK UP battery (`Battery`)
- EXIT down

## 3. Burth Street

- ATTACK **both** zombies with `Bat`
- EXIT right

## 4. Southern Harrow Street

- OPEN door on the alley to Phil's bar
  - "I'm a girl in trouble!!"

### 4.1. Phil's Bar

- "We can cross the bridge"
- EXIT down on the left side

## 5. Southern Harrow Street

- EXIT right

## 6. Northern Harrow Street

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- ENTER bank

### 6.1. Bank

#### 6.1.1. Entrance Area

- OPEN elevator (on the left)

#### 6.1.2. Second Floor

- PICK UP fuse (next to the briefcase) (`Fuse`)
- OPEN middle doors

##### 6.1.2.1. Office Hallway

- PICK UP tape roll (on the floor on the left) (`Tape Roll`)
- OPEN left door

###### 6.1.2.1.1. Office

- EXIT down

##### 6.1.2.2. Office Hallway

- EXIT right

#### 6.1.3. Second Floor

- OPEN elevator

#### 6.1.4. Entrance Area

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- PICK UP CD (next to the man who killed himself) (`CD`)
- EXIT up

## 7. Eastern Burnside Street

- EXIT right

## 8. Burnside Mall

- PICK UP fuse (in the trolley on the left)
- ATTACK zombie with `Bat`
- EXIT right

## 9. Burnside Gas Station

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- OPEN door

### 9.1. Gas Station

- ATTACK zombie with `Bat`
- PICK UP fuel can (`Empty Fuel Can`)
- OPEN left door

### 9.2. Storage Room

- PICK UP crowbar (`Crowbar`)
- PICK UP garden scissors (`Garden Scissors`)
- ATTACK right box with `Bat`
- PICK UP shotgun shells (`Shotgun Shells`)
- OPEN door

### 9.3. Gas Station

- ATTACK zombie with `Bat`
- EXIT right

## 10. Burnside Gas Station

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- USE `Empty Fuel Can` with pump (on the right) (`Fuel Can`)
- EXIT right

## 11. Hospital

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- OPEN doors to he hospital

### 11.1. Reception

- PICK UP fuse (on the reception desk) (`Fuse`)
- EXIT left

### 11.2. Hallway

- PICK UP hose (`Hose`)
- OPEN the left open back door

#### 11.2.1. Sick Room

- PICK UP key (from the floor) (`Key`)

## 12. Southern Harrow Street

### 12.1. Phil's Bar

- USE `Shotgun Shells` with Phil (`Shotgun`)
- EXIT down on the left, then left

## 13. Southern Harrow Street

- OPEN door to the church

### 13.1. Church

- TALK TO priest
- USE `Tape Roll` on priest

## 14. Southern Harrow Street

- EXIT right

## 15. Northern Harrow Street

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- EXIT up

## 16. Eastern Burnside Street

- EXIT right

## 17. Burnside Mall

- ATTACK zombie with `Bat`
- OPEN doors to the mall

### 17.1. Mall Entrance

- WALK east onto one of the escalators
- WALK west on the upper level to the three wires
- USE `Garden Scissors` with wires (`Cables`)
- WALK down the escalators, then WEST on the lower level
- OPEN door to men's room

#### 17.1.1. Men's Room

- WALK to the back (`Chewing Gum`)
- EXIT down

### 17.2. Mall Entrance

- OPEN exit doors

## 18. Southern Harrow Street

### 18.1. Phil's Bar

- EXIT down, left **2 times**

## 19. Tramway

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- OPEN doors to the tram

### 19.1. Tram

- **QUICK:**
  - PICK UP extinguisher

## 20. Southern Harrow Street

### 20.1. Phil's Bar

- TALK TO Phil
- EXIT down, left, down

## 21. Roth Street

- ATTACK zombie with `Bat`
- USE `Crowbar` with sewer lid

## 22. Sewers under Roth Street

- EXIT up

## 23. Sewers Hub

- ENTER left door
  - Now simply leave the room through the door you came in through **4 times**

## 24. Sewers under Roth Street

- OPEN fuse box
- EXIT right

## 25. Sewers Crosswalk

- WALK southwest to the lit door
- OPEN door

## 26. Subway Station Entrance

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- PICK UP shotgun shells (at the turnstiles on the left) (`Shotgun Shells`)
- EXIT up into the tunnel **2 times**

## 27. Tunnel

- OPEN subway train

### 27.1. Passenger Wagon

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- OPEN door (in the back)

### 27.2. Driving Cab

- USE `Cables` with `Battery` (`Wired Battery`)
- USE `Wired Battery` with cables (in the cabinet)
- OPEN lever

## 28. Clindale Street

- USE `Hose` on hydrant
- EXIT up on the left side of the truck

## 29. Burnside Street

- EXIT left

## 30. Harrow Street

- ATTACK zombies with `Bat`
- USE `Fuel Can` with bus
- EXIT down and enter bar

## 31. Southern Harrow Street

### 31.1. Phil's Bar

- PICK UP bottle of oil (from the floor) (`Oil`)
- PICK UP lighter (on the pool table) (`Lighter`)
- PICK UP cloth (under the pool table) (`Cloth`)
- USE `Cloth` with `Oil` (`Molotov Cocktail`)
- USE `Molotov Cocktail` with Phil

## 32. Bus

- "North"
- "East"
- "North"

## 33. Bridge

- OPEN door to control room

### 33.1. Control Room

- OPEN red lever (on the right)

## 34. Station A

### 34.1. Laser Room

- USE `Chewing Gum` with laser
- OPEN vent (on the right) **3 times**

### 34.2. Hallway

- LOOK AT map (on the wall behind you)
- OPEN right door

#### 34.2.1. Test Room

- PICK UP suit

### 34.3. Hallway

- "Jack"
- "Jackson"
- "Emergency!"
- "The testing guy has woken up"
- "808"

#### 34.3.1. Test Room

- PICK UP keyboard (next to the panel)
- EXIT left

### 34.4. Hallway

- OPEN laboratory door (on the left side)

#### 34.4.1. Laboratory

- PICK UP scalpel (under the table) (`Scalpel`)
- PICK UP diary
  - Read all pages
- EXIT left

### 34.5. Hallway

- USE `Scalpel` with switch box (to the right of the lasers)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- When the lasers are off, **QUICK:**
  - EXIT up
    - "Left"

#### 34.5.1. Butterfly Room

- WALK left
- WALK right
- **QUICK:**
  - WALK right (you need to be completely out of the room)
  - PICK UP buttons (on the control box next to the door) to close the door
- PICK UP id card (from the floor) (`Key`, `ID-Card`)
- LOOK AT `ID-Card`
  - **NOTE:** `3883`
- EXIT right

### 34.6. Hallway

- OPEN door to the back

#### 34.6.1. Picture Room

- LOOK AT bottom right picture
  - "Air"
  - "Earth"
  - "Fire"
  - "Water" (in alphabetically order)
- WALK right
- PICK UP syringe (`Syringe`)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- OPEN back door

##### 34.6.1.1. Office

- USE `Syringe` in yourself
- OPEN cabinet (on the left) (`Bat`, `CD`, `Crowbar`, `Lighter`, `Garden Scissors`)
- EXIT down, left

### 34.7. Hallway

- EXIT right

#### 34.7.1. Computer Room

- PICK UP right computer
  - "3883"
    - **NOTE:** The hint for the passwords and numbers
- LOOK AT map above the computer
- OPEN (left) door

## 35. Core Room

- WALK TO elevator
  - "4"

## 36. Rooftop

- LOOK AT hatch
- OPEN elevator
  - "-3"

## 37. Core Control Room

- PICK UP computer
  - "Yes"
  - "Open/Close hatch"
  - "1"
  - "5"
  - "3"
  - "8"
  - "Enter password"
  - "Set temperature / Oxygen"
    - Using the arrows, set **both** to the highest level
    - EXIT with the "X" button on the control panel
- OPEN elevator
  - "-4"

## 38. Core Room

- **NOTE:** When the room is not in red light, return to the Core Control Room and close the hatch again
- OPEN left door

## 39. Station A

### 39.1. Hallway

#### 39.1.1. Computer Room

- EXIT right

### 39.2. Hallway

- USE `ID-Card` with card reader (of the big test room to the right)

#### 39.2.1. Test Room

- "Yes"
- The screen on the bottom left states: "Liberate Gas? Y=1 N=2
- The screen shows different animals and you need to enter the number of legs:
  1. 8
  2. 4
  3. 0
  4. 6
- ENTER

### 39.3. Hallway

- USE `Key` with panel (next to the elevator on the left)

## 40. Oil Platform

- OPEN door (on the right)

### 40.1. Radio Room

- OPEN radio
- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Lighter` with oil on the floor
- OPEN left door

## 41. Oil Platform

- WALK to the left

## 42. Southern Harrow Street

- OPEN door to enter the bar
