# Ex Machina - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 19.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/ex-machina-v1.2)

## 1. Street

- WALK east to the saloon
- PICK UP rotten fruit (from under the bush) (`Rotten Fruit`)
- USE door to Iron Horse

### 1.1. Iron Horse

- USE (empty) stool
- TALK TO operator
  - "Sunday Special."
  - "Order a Beer."
- UE automated waiter (`Beer`)
- PICK UP dirty mug (on the right) (`Mug`)
- USE `Rotten Fruit` on `Beer` (`Tainted Beer`)
- TALK TO operator
  - "Complain about Beer."
  - "Sunday Special." (`Note`)
- EXIT southwest (there is a hotspot named `Exit`)

## 2. Street

- LOOK AT `Note`
- ENTER Bell's Glassware

### 2.1. Bell's Glassware

- TALK TO Bell
  - "Note."
- USE (back) door

#### 2.1.1. Furnace Room

- USE cover (from the furnace)
- LOOK AT fire
- PICK UP blowpipe (`Blowpipe`)
- USE `Blowpipe` on `Mug` (`Blowpipe + Mug`)
- USE `Blowpipe + Mug` on fire
- EXIT east

### 2.2. Bell's Glassware

- PICK UP chisel (on the counter) (`Chisel`)
- EXIT south

### 2.3. Iron Horse

- PICK UP hammer (`Hammer`)
- USE stool
- TALK TO operator
  - "Order Water."
- USE automated waiter (`Glass of Water`)
- USE `Kerchief` on `Glass of Water` (`Wet kerchief`)
- USE `Hammer` on `Chisel` (`Hammer and Chisel`)

### 2.4. Bell's Glassware

#### 2.4.1. Furnace Room

- USE `Wet Kerchief` on molten glass
- PICK UP chunk of glass (`Key Encased in Glass`)
- USE `Hammer and Chisel` on `Key Encased in Glass` (`Clock Tower Key`)

## 3. Street

- USE `Clock Tower Key` on door to the big brown building
- ENTER door to the clock tower

### 3.1. Clock Tower

- PICK UP winding key (`Winding Key`)
- USE (upper) door

### 3.2. Copper & Clockwork

- PICK UP book (on the right shelf) (`Book`)
- PICK UP mechanism (on the shelf) (`Antikythera Mechanism`)
- LOOK AT `Book`
- EXIT south

### 3.3. Clock Tower

- USE (upper) door

#### 3.3.1. Outside Clock Tower

- PICK UP tool set (on the platform on the right) (`Tool Set`)

### 3.4. Copper & Clockwork

- USE `Tool Set` on `Antikythera Mechanism` (`Large Gears`)
- USE `Dagger` on `Watch` (`Set of Gears`)
- USE `Set of Gears` on automaton (next to the door)
- USE `Winding Key` on automaton
- USE door

#### 3.4.1. Laboratory

- TALK TO Tobias
- EXIT upstairs

### 3.5. Clock Tower

- USE (upper) door
- USE `Hammer and Chisel` on (upper) door **2 times**
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE (upper) door

#### 3.5.1. Outside Clock Tower

- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Dagger` on assassin
