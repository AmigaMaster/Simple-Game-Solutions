# ESPER: The Town on the Edge of Darkness - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 22.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Get the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/610-esper-the-town-on-the-edge-of-darkness)

## 1. Docks

### 1.1. Boat

- PICK UP hand gun (from the table) (`Handgun`)
- PICK UP EMF detector (on the cabinet) (`EMF Detector`)
- EXIT upstairs

## 2. Docks

- EXIT northeast

## 3. Town Square

- USE (right) door

### 3.1. Pub

- TALK TO girl
- EXIT south

## 4. Town Square

- USE door to the church
- Use door on the upper left

### 4.1. Hotel

- TALK TO man behind
- USE (right) door

#### 4.1.1. Room 105

- USE lamp (to switch it off)
- USE window
- USE bed (`Rag`)
- USE window

## 5. Town Square

### 5.1. Alley

- USE right window

### 5.2. Church

#### 5.2.1. Back Hall

- USE book (`Book`)
- LOOK AT `Book`
- EXIT east

#### 5.2.2. Office

- USE shovel (`Shovel`)
- USE `EMF Detector` on priest
- USE lower left corner of the desk (`Document`)
- LOOK AT `Document`
- EXIT west

#### 5.2.3. Back Hall

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- EXIT south

#### 5.2.4. Entrance Hall

- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Handgun` on kid
- USE door

### 5.3. Jail

- LOOK AT scratchings (on the wall)
- USE scratchings (`Chunk of concrete`)
- USE `Rag` on sheriff
- USE `Chunk of concrete` on sheriff
- LOOK AT yellow bag
- USE yellow bag (`Handgun`, `EMF Detector`, `Shovel`)
- EXIT south

## 6. Town Square

- EXIT northeast

### 6.1. Cemetery

- LOOK AT center tomb stone (Virginia's tomb)
- USE shovel on Virginia's grave

#### 6.1.1. Lab

- USE computer on the wall (`Report`)
- LOOK AT `Report`
- Walk down
- USE `Handgun` on tanks on the right
- EXIT west

#### 6.1.2. Dark Path

- EXIT northwest

### 6.2. Church

#### 6.2.1. Office

- USE lower left corner of the desk (`Dossier`)
- LOOK AT `Dossier`
- EXIT west, south, north

## 7. Town Square

- EXIT southeast

## 8. Docks

- Walk west
- "I don't think so..."
- ENTER boat (you are too late)
