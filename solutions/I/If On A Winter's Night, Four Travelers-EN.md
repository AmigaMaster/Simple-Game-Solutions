- LOOK AT painting
- LOOK AT chest of drawers

### 1.3. Bedroom

- Walk into the living room at the bottom right

### 1.4. Living Room

## 2. Act II - The slow vanishing of Lady Winterbourne

### 2.1. Bedroom

- LOOK AT glass decanter with laudanum near the bed
- LOOK AT armoire
- LOOK AT draped cloth
- LOOK AT hanging cloths
- LOOK AT picture frames
- LOOK AT left window
- WALK TO Hallway
- LOOK AT ball of yarn at the ground at the bottom of the bed
- LOOK AT right window
- WALK TO Hallway again
- LOOK AT armoire
- LOOK AT bed
- USE glass decanter with laudanum (envelope arrives)
- LOOK AT envelope
- EXIT room

### 2.2. Hallway 1st Floor

- USE heavy door
- TAKE ornate glass on the desk
- USE ornate glass on heavy door

### 2.3. Bedroom

- USE medicine near bed
- LOOK AT mirror
- USE harp
- LOOK AT photos  
  **NOTE:** Order of photos
  - Maman and I in paris, February 1923
  - Maman and I in Vienna, April 1926
  - Maman and I in New York, October 1925

### 2.4. Hallway 1st Floor

- LOOK AT Eos statue
- LOOK AT Mnemosyne statue
- LOOK AT Apollo statue
- WALK downstairs

### 2.5. Bedroom

- USE bed (envelope arrives)
- LOOK AT envelope

### 2.6. Hallway 1st Floor

- OPEN heavy door
- TAKE vase
- Walk downstairs

### 2.7. Parlour

- LOOK AT display cabinet
- TAKE fireplace pokers

### 2.8. Hallway 1st Floor

- USE fireplace pokers on vase fragments

### 2.9. Parlour

- LOOK AT clock
- LOOK AT bookshelf
  - LOOK AT *every* book in the bookshelf
- LOOK AT dead flowers
- USE fireplace pokers with display cabinet
- LOOK AT table clock (left to the stairs)
- LOOK AT bookshelf
  - LOOK AT "A treaty on clocks"  
    **NOTE:** Average delay of 7 minutes.
- LOOK AT flowers
- LOOK AT gramophone  
  **NOTE:** Recordings
  - You can hear three records
  - The records bring memories
  - Remember some other things where the order was important?
  - Remember the photos and travels
    1. "'Parigi, o cara' by Giuseppe Verdi."
    2. "'My New York' by Mal Hallett and his Orchestra."
    3. "'Three old Viennese dances' by Fritz Keisler."

### 2.10. Bedroom

- LOOK AT draped cloths (harp)
- USE bed
- LOOK AT envelope

### 2.11. Parlour

- USE display cabinet
- Exit right

### 2.12. Foyer

- LOOK AT clock
- TAKE clock key
  **NOTE:** Clock is stopped at three o'clock
- LOOK AT newspaper

### 2.13. Parlour

- USE table clock
  - SET 02:53 o'clock
- Walk to Foyer

### 2.14. Foyer

- EXIT to conservatory

### 2.15. Conservatory

- LOOK AT tea table and chairs
- LOOK AT bushes (bottom right of screen)

- LOOK AT white flowers
- LOOK AT bench
- LOOK AT pond

- LOOK AT table and chairs *again*
- LOOK AT pond *again*
- LOOK AT bench *again* (she says that there is something in the pond)

### 2.16. Foyer

- TAKE umbrella

### 2.17. Conservatory

- USE umbrella with pond

### 2.18. Foyer

- LOOK AT newspaper again

### 2.19. Hallway 1st Floor

- OPEN door to Henry's study

### 2.20. Parlour

- LOOK AT envelope

## 3. Act III - The nameless ritual

### 3.1. Auditorium

- Choose any disease **2 times**

### 3.2. Home

- LOOK AT jacket on bed
- Exit room

### 3.3. Floor

- KNOCK on door to Apartment B01

### 3.4. Home

- LOOK AT iron, lead and copper bowls in front of the mirror
- LOOK AT astral charts on the wall  
  **NOTE::** Mars, Venus and Saturn are aligned
- LOOK AT scribbles on the floor
- LOOK AT 'Beyond the Veil' by T. Neumann
- LOOK AT 'Fiery Summons' by H. Zhidkova (book on the shelf in the bottom right)
  "The Sun burns in golden flames. A violet mantle shall devour Mercury. Cast ye the symbol of Venus onto flames of emerald. And engulf in crimson all that belongs to Mars. A raging azure inferno for Jupiter. A black grave for Saturn."  
  This is related to the bowls and planets.
- LOOK AT 'Planetary correspondences' by H. Solomon  
  "The Sun shines on the Heliotrope. Mercury smells of fresh Hyssop. The visions of Wormwood rule over Mars. Venus is the sweetness of the Rose. Sage is the wisdom of Jupiter. The Hemlock is Saturn's blessing."
- LOOK AT wound man (poster on the wall)  
  - "The Sun rules the intellect. Venus makes the heart yearn.
  - Mercury is the hand in darkness. Mars wields the sword.
  - Jupiter hungers for power, and casts Saturn's genitals into the sea."
- LOOK AT herb jars
  - TAKE rose
- PUT rose in copper bowl (green)
- LOOK AT herb jars
  - TAKE hemlock
- PUT hemlock in lead bowl (black)
- TAKE wormwood
- PUT wormwood in iron bowl (red)
- TAKE athame from the desk at the right screen
- USE athame on yourself
  - Chest ("Venus")
  - Groin ("Saturn")
  - Right hand ("Mars")
- ENTER the magic circle

### 3.5. Home

- LOOK AT scribbles
- TALK TO Wound Man
- USE mirror
- TAKE lamp (on Wound Man)
- EXIT room (exit is at the same spot as in the real world but it requires the lamp tp appear)

### 3.6. Floor

- KNOCK on door to Apartment B01
- Exit downstairs

### 3.7. Library

- TALK TO librarian
- LOOK AT scroll
  - Search for the required book
  - When you come closer to a book, Samuel will say something and you hear a sound
  - LOOK at bookshelf to get the book
  - GIVE book to librarian
  - Repeat the same process for the other 4 books
  - Locations:
    1. North west of the librarian
    2. East of the librarian
    3. South of the librarian
    4. South east of the librarian
    5. West of the librarian

### 3.8. War Field

- Walk over the bridge
- TALK TO reverend Williams
- TALK TO each of the three soldiers
- TAKE medical supplies
- USE baptismal font
- TAKE canteen (left to the soldier without jaw on the sheet)
- USE baptismal font
- GIVE opium to soldier with pillow
- TAKE pillow
- USE pillow on soldier without jaw
- USE bullets with soldier
- WALK TO confessional

### 3.9. Auditorium

- You now have to listen to the people and remember their position and click them in the correct order
  1. I HAVE (far left), GIVEN (left to Dr. Hayward, a man standing on the stairs),  MY MIND (bottom), TO THE (2nd row on the right side, the first man), WATERS (right to you)
  2. I HAVE (bottom), CONSIGNED (right to you), MY SOUL (far left), TO THE (left to Dr. Hayward, a man standing on the stairs), FLAMES (2nd row on the right side, the first man)
  3. I KNOW (2nd row on the right side, the first man), SURRENDER (right to you), MY FLESH (bottom), TO THE (far left), BLADE (left to Dr. Hayward, a man standing on the stairs)
  4. AND I (left to Dr. Hayward, a man standing on the stairs), OFFER (2nd row on the right side, the first man), MY BLOOD (bottom), TO THE (right to you), MIRROR (far left)

### 3.10. Home

- TAKE invitation (from the bed)
- USE mirror
