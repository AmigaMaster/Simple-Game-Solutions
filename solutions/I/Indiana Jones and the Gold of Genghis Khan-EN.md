# Indiana Jones and the Gold of Genghis Khan - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 02.12.2021 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the game at the [Itch.io Website](https://laurahunt.itch.io/if-on-a-winters-night-four-travelers)

## 1. New York

### 1.1. Home

- PICK UP book (in the book shelf) (`Hemingway's book`)
- LOOK AT `Hemingway's book`
- LOOK AT Curtain's edge (the left curtain in the bedroom)
- PULL Curtain's edge (`Wallet`)
- OPEN left door
- Exit

### 1.2. College

#### 1.2.1. Class Room

- PICK UP Paperwork (in the shelf)
- PUSH Blue book (`Whip`)

## 2. Italy

### 2.1. Venice

#### 2.1.1. Bridge

- TALK TO Guard
- PICK UP Rhododendron (at the wall behind the tree) (`Rhododendron`)
- PICK UP Poppy (on the ground at the left small tree) (`Poppy`)
- USE `Puppy` with `Rhododendron` (`Mixture`)
- Exit west

#### 2.1.2. Marketplace

- TALK TO fruit seller (`Green apple`)
- USE `Green apple`
- OPEN `Green apple` (`Worm`)
- USE `Worm` with `Poisonous feed`(`Poison`)
- PICK UP Crumbs (`Crumbs`)
- USE `Crumbs` with `Mixture` (`Poisonous feed`)
- TALK TO guard
- GIVE `Hemingway's book` to guard
- PICK UP Crumbs (`Crumbs`)
- USE `Crumbs` with `Rhododendron` with `Crumbs` (`Pink feed`)
- Exit north through the small hallway

#### 2.1.3. Docks

- GIVE `Poison` to Seagull (`Feather`)
- PUSH Pole **2 times** (`Fish`)

#### 2.1.4. Marketplace

- PICK UP Document (`Card`)

#### 2.1.5. Bridge

- GIVE `Card` to Guard

#### 2.1.6. Harbor

- Enter Vendramin Palace (the building above the three gondolas)

#### 2.1.7. Vendramin Palace

- LOOK AT Ornament stone basin **2 times** (`Hammer`)
- USE `Hammer` with Drainage pipe
- USE `Feather` with Oil (`Lubricant`)

#### 2.1.8. Harbor

- Enter Courtyard at the right end of the screen above the wall

#### 2.1.9. Courtyard Area

- LOOK AT ladder
- PICK UP Screwdriver (on the bench) (`Screwdriver`)

#### 2.1.10. Harbor

- Enter Grassi Palace (the first building on the left)

##### 2.1.10.1. Grassi Palace

- USE `Screwdriver` with First Aid
- PICK UP Shears (`Rusty shears`)
- USE `Lubricant` with `Rusty shears` (`Oiled shears`)

#### 2.1.11. Courtyard Area

- USE `Oiled shears` with Bush (`Branch`)
- USE ladder

#### 2.1.12. Rooftops

- USE `Whip` with Clamp (`Clamp`)
- USE `Clamp` with `Hammer` (`Improvised hammer and chisel`)
- USE `Improvised hammer and chisel` with Left hatch
- USE `Improvised hammer and chisel` with Right hatch
- USE Right passage (where the hatch was before)

##### 2.1.12.1. Museum

- OPEN Front door
- Exit museum

#### 2.1.13. Harbor

- Enter Giacomo's Shop (left door)

##### 2.1.13.1. Giacomo's Shop

- LOOK AT chest
- USE `Notebook`  
  **NOTE:** Remember the symbols in the order Ability, Bravery, Strength
- LOOK AT chest
- CLICK ON the symbols:  
  3rd, 7th, 5th, 6th, 2nd, 8th, 4th, 1st, 9th
