# Indiana Jones and the Window of the Crystal Cursor DEMO - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 18.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/indy-and-the-crystal-cursors-demo-v1.2)

## 1. Barnett College

### 1.1. Catacombs

- USE `Wooden Beam` with crumbling

### 1.2. Basement

- PICK UP cog (`Cog`)

### 1.3. Hallway

- USE `Cog` with Marcus Brody (`Telegrams`)
- LOOK AT `Telegrams`
- EXIT northwest

## 2. Scotland

### 2.1. Lakeside

- EXIT southwest

### 2.2. Mountain

- TALK TO Guzzo
- USE cave mouth **2 times**

#### 2.2.1. Cave

- LOOK AT carvings
  - **NOTE:** Left/right, Hand, All directions, diagonal bottom left/top right
- EXIT cave

### 2.3. Mountain

- EXIT east

### 2.4. Lakeside

- ENTER cave

#### 2.4.1. Cave

- OPEN box
- EXIT cave

### 2.5. Mountain

- USE cave mouth

### 2.6. Lakeside

- USE truck

## 3. Barnett College

### 3.1. Street

- USE entrance

#### 3.1.1. Entrance Hall

- TALK TO Marcus Brody
- USE exit

### 3.2. Street

- USE Indy's car

## 4. Guzzo's Office

- LOOK AT drawer (from the large table) (`Scissors`)
- PICK UP loose paper (below the middle window) (`Schedule`)
- LOOK AT `Schedule`
- USE exit

## 5. New York City

### 5.1. Street

- USE `Scissors` with taped up door
- OPEN door
- USE cab

## 6. Barnet College

### 6.1. Street

- USE entrance

#### 6.1.1. Entrance Hall

- EXIT east

#### 6.1.2. Hallway

- LOOK AT noticeboard until Indy mentions Dr. Guzzo
- LOOK AT noticeboard until Idy mentions lost books
- EXIT northwest

#### 6.1.3. Entrance Hall

- USE stairs to library

#### 6.1.4. Library

- PICK UP ? (the turquoise books in the shelf behind the rope in the 3rd row) (`Lucas' Lost Script`)
- EXIT library (behind the bookshelf in the background)

#### 6.1.5. Entrance Hall

- USE `Lucas' Lost Script` with Marcus Brody
- LOOK AT `Lucas' Lost Script`
  - Scroll through the pages
  - **NOTE:** The details on the symbols and countries
- EXIT college

### 6.2. Street

- USE Indy's car

## 7. Scotland

### 7.1. Mountain

#### 7.1.1. Cave

- USE `Lucas' Lost Script` with carvings
  - "Okay, let's try this..."
  - "AA3,3AZ..."
  - "...Agadez..."
  - "G2E,1KS..."
  - "Kos"
  - "ED2,2LN..."
  - "London"
  - "Costa Rica"

## 8. London

- ENTER museum
- TALK TO Beth until you can select the following dialogue
  - "You can come too."
- USE bus

## 9. Agadez

### 9.1. Excavation Site

- PICK UP metal object (on the ground in the pile of dirt on the right side of the barrel) (`Lighter`)
- WALK behind the tent on the right side, Beth will call you

### 9.2. Dunes

- EXIT west

### 9.3. Excavation Site

- WALK behind the tent and to the left between the crane and water tower (between the two tents) Beth needs to walk here to find a shovel
- TALK TO Beth
  - "Can I have the shovel?" (`Shovel`)
- EXIT east

### 9.4. Dunes

- USE `Shovel` with soft ground (a hotspot in the bottom, sandy ground, on the right side of the large rocks) (`Four point Cursor`)

### 9.5. Excavation Site

- USE truck

## 10. Kos

### 10.1. Village

- EXIT east through the doorway

#### 10.1.1. House

- LOOK AT **every** barrel, pot and vase (`Membership Card`)
- EXIT east

#### 10.1.2. Village

- WALK TO path to the docks
- USE boat
  - "To the small dock on the other side of the mountain..."

### 10.2. Small Dock

#### 10.2.1. Dock

- EXIT west

#### 10.2.2. Path to Temple

- ENTER temple entrance

##### 10.2.2.1. Lava Pit

- PICK UP odd looking hand (in the left side of the room) (`Hand Shaped Cursor`)
- EXIT east
