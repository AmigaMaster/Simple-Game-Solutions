## 1. Front of Mansion

### 1.1. Mansion

- USE (left) door

## 2. Front of Mansion

- EXIT east

## 3. Clock Tower

- USE (second right) door

### 3.1. Merchant

- PICK UP empty bucket (`Empty bucket`)
- USE door

## 4. Clock Tower

- EXIT northeast

## 5. Pier

- USE door to the bar

### 5.1. Scumm Bar

- TALK TO drunkard
  - "If I am you..."
  - "Yes..."
  - "Yes..."
  - "So if you..."
  - "We got..."
- USE (right) door

#### 5.1.1. Kitchen

- USE `Empty bucket` on leaky barrel (`Bucket of grog`)
- USE (left) door

### 5.2. Scumm Bar

- USE `Bucket of grog` on drunkard (`Empty bucket`, `Mug`)
- TALK TO drunkard
  - "If I am you..."
  - "Yes..."
  - "Yes..."
  - "So if you..."
  - "We got..." (`20 pieces of eight`)

#### 5.2.1. Kitchen

- USE `Empty bucket` on leaky barrel (`Bucket of grog`)
- EXIT Scumm bar

## 6. Pier

- EXIT northwest

## 7. Lookout

- LOOK AT displaced brick (in the stones on the left at the fireplace)
- USE `Bucket of grog` on displaced brick (`Empty bucket`, `Y drink`)
- EXIT east

## 8. Clock Tower

- TALK TO Largo
- USE `20 pieces of eight` on Largo (`Voodoo key`)
- USE `Voodoo key` on door (the one right from the clock tower) (`G drink`, `Knife`)

### 8.1. Voodoo Shop

- USE coffin

## 9. Front of Mansion

### 9.1. Mansion

- USE `Knife` on painting
- LOOK AT hole (`R drink`)

## 10. Clock Tower

- USE (second right) door (`Potion`)

## 11. Front of Mansion

- USE door

## 12. Harbor

- USE door of the jail

### 12.1. Jail

- TALK TO Fafri
- USE door

## 13. Harbor

- USE door of the library

### 13.1. Library

- USE heap of books
- PICK UP book (`Book`)

### 13.2. Jail

- USE `Book` on Fafri
