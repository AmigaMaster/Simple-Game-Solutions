# Inside Monkey Island: Chapter 1 - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 29.12.2025 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/inside-monkey-island-chapter-1)

## 1. Ghost Ship

### 1.1. LeChuck's Cabin

- LOOK AT space under bed (`Pirate Key`)
- USE `Pirate Key` on drawer (`Saw`)
- EXIT cabin

### 1.2. Upper Deck

- TALK TO ghost pirate of little importance
  - Talk about everything
- EXIT north

## 2. Giant Monkey Head

- EXIT west

## 3. Beach

- EXIT west

## 4. Dancing Skeleton

- TALK TO skeleton
- EXIT east

## 5. Hanged Man

- PICK UP bunch of bananas (`Banana`)
- EXIT west

## 6. Dancing Skeleton

- EXIT west

## 7. Large Tree

- USE `Saw` on root covered in resin (`Twig covered in resin`)
- USE `Twig covered in resin` on strange hole (in the large tree) (`Drawing of a skeleton`)
- EXIT west

## 8. Dancing Skeleton

- USE `Drawing of a skeleton` on skeleton (`Bone`)
- EXIT south

## 9. Beach

- USE `Banana` on monkey (`Monkey`)
- EXIT east

## 10. Giant Monkey Head

- USE wooden gate

## 11. Ghost Ship

### 11.1. Upper Deck

- EXIT north

## 12. Harbor

- LOOK AT poster
- EXIT northwest

## 13. Mansion

- TALK TO guard
- USE `Monkey` on guard (`Keys to the jail`)
- EXIT east

## 14. Harbor

- USE narrow alley

## 15. Alley

- USE chest (`50 pezzi da otto`)
- EXIT east

## 16. Harbor

- USE door of the library

### 16.1. Library

- TALK TO man
- USE `50 pezzi da otto` on man (`LeChuck doll`)
- USE door

## 17. Harbor

- USE `Keys to the jail` on door of the jail

### 17.1. Jail

- TALK TO Fafri
- USE `LeChuck doll` on Fafri
