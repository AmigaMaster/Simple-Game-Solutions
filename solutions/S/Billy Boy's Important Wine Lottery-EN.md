# Billy Boy's Important Wine Lottery - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 31.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/billy-boys-important-wine-lottery)

## 1. Main Room

- PICK UP junk food (from the shelf) (`WhackDonalds Box`)
- LOOK AT `WhackDonalds Box` (`Captain Hook Figure`)
- EXIT west

## 2. Office

- PICK UP coffee cup (from the desk of Henery the VIII)
  - "I thought I'll pour you a new cup!" (`Empty Cup`)
- EXIT east

## 3. Main Room

- USE `Empty Cup` with coffee maker (`Cup of Coffee`)
- EXIT east

## 4. Hallway

- LOOK AT note (next to the door)
- USE hot dog pronto (`Hot Dog`, `Hot Dog`)
- USE `Cup of Coffee` with hot dog pronto (`Empty Cup`)
- USE (large) door
- TALK TO Ruby the robot
  - "I want a hot dog"
- PICK UP wire (`Wire`)
- EXIT southwest

## 5. Main Room

- TALK TO Sadie the cleaning lady
  - "THe hot dog pronto has messed up!"
- PICK UP tickets (from the jacket on the trolley) (`Sadie's Tickets`)
- PICK UP bag of garbage (`Garbage`)
- LOOK AT `Garbage` (`Last weeks Tickets`, `Pastilles`)
- LOOK AT `Pastilles`
- USE `Empty Cup` with coffee maker (`Cup of Coffee`)
- USE `Pastilles` with `Cup of Coffee` (`Laxative Coffee`)
- USE `Laxative Coffee` with Henery the VIII
- PICK UP Henery's wallet (`Money`)
- TALK TO a boy named Sue
  - "So, bought your tickets for the wine lottery yet?"
- USE `Last weeks Tickets` with tickets (on his desk) (`Sue's Tickets`)
- USE drawer (of the grey cupboard in the upper left corner of the room)
- LOOK AT drawer (`Fishing Pole`)
- USE `Fishing Pole` with `Wire`
- USE `Captain Hook Figure` with `Fishing Pole` (`Fishing Pole with Hook`)
- USE `Hot Dog` with `Fishing Pole with Hook` (`Fishing Pole with Bait`)
- USE `Fishing Pole with Bait` with drawer
- PICK UP tickets (from the desk in the lower left corner)
