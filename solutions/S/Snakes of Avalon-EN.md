# Snakes of Avalon - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|

Get the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/1357-snakes-of-avalon)

## 1. Bar

- USE `Wallet` on Bob
- USE pool table
- TALK TO poster (on the right wall) (`Potion`, `Thingamajig`)
- USE `Thingamajig` on `Potion`
- You get the money and your drink. While drinking you overhear a conversation about a murder.

## 2. Act I: A pint of Murder

### 2.1. Bar

- USE (bottom) table (beside the couple) (`Full bladder`)
- USE `Full bladder` on plant **2 times** (`Somekindof fish`)
- USE `Somekindof fish` on purse (of the woman) (`Number`)
- USE `Number` on phone (on the bar)
- USE `Number` on Bob
- TALK TO Bob
- WALK south
- TALK TO moose head
  - "Is Bob really your friend?"
  - "What are Bob's weak points?"
- TALK TO Bob
  - "You are jealous..."
  - "...of me not being a baldhead"

## 3. Act II: The first Victims

### 3.1. Bar

- WALK south
- USE drunkard
- WALK north
- PICK UP jar (from middle table) (`Honey jar`)
- PICK UP filthy glass (table on the right) (`Filthy glass`)
- USE `Somekindof fish` on pipes
- WALK south
- USE `Somekindof fish` on hole + steam
- USE (bottom) table
- USE `Filthy glass` on whispers (from the pipe)
- WALK north
- PICK UP cues (`Cue`)
- USE `Cue` on statue (`Can opener`)
- WALK left
- PICK UP Charlie (on the floor)

## 4. Act III: The Snakes bite back

### 4.1. Bar

- WALK TO northeast
- USE `Can opener` on TK-421 (`Red bulb`)
- LOOK AT `Red bulb`
- WALK southeast
- USE `Red bulb` on bulb (`Burned-out bulb`)
- PICK UP green bulb (from the floor) (`Green bulb`)
  - **NOTE:** The colors of the arrows on the floor `green , blue, red, blue, red, green, red`
- USE `Green bulb` on bulb (`Red bulb`)
- WALK west
- WALK south
- PICK UP old book (from the table) (`Old book`)
- TALK TO Moose
- USE `Old book` **2 times** (`Red crayon`)
- USE `Red bulb` on bulb
- WALK west
- Use `red crayon` on moose
- USE `Green bulb` on bulb (`Spoiled Charlie`)
- WALK southwest
- USE `Red crayon` on carton box
- USE "time machine"
  - "Sometime before..."
  - "Right before the blue bulb burned out."
- USE `Burned out bulb` on bulb
- WALK west **2 times** (`Skeleton key`)
- USE `Skeleton key` on WC
- USE the light bulbs to switch the colors like they were on the floor: `green , blue, red, blue, red, green, red`
- WALK northeast
- LOOK AT dirty boots
- USE WC

## 5. Act IV: The salty aftertaste of Revenge

- WALK south (`Full bladder`)
- USE `Full bladder` on `Honey jar`
- USE `Honey jar` on 3 cockroaches
- USE `Honey jar` on woman
