# Sydney Finds Employment - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 20.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/sydney-finds-employment)

## 1. Shonk Street

### 1.1. Alley

- PICK UP stick (from dumpster) (`Stick`)
- USE Terrence's bin (`Identification`)
- EXIT east

## 2. Shonk Street

- ENTER Runny Inn (it does not work)
- TALK TO bouncer
- USE `Identification` on bouncer
- ENTER Runny Inn

### 2.1. The Runny Inn

- TALK TO Mr. Smooth
- EXIT southwest

## 3. Shonk Street

- EXIT west

## 4. Bank Street

- ENTER bank

### 4.1. Bank

- PICK UP scissors (`Scissors`)
- USE `Scissors` on locked cupboard
- PICK UP dustpan (`Dustpan`)
- TALK TO Bipasha
- EXIT southeast

## 5. Bank Street

- EXIT east

## 6. Shonk Street

- ENTER Big Boy Publishing

### 6.1. Big Boy Publishing

- TALK TO Bipasha
- EXIT east

## 7. Shonk Street

- ENTER Pets Appreciation Center

### 7.1. Pet Appreciation Center

- TALK TO Peta
- USE `Dustpan` on on loose fur (in the cages on the right) (`Loose Fur`)
- EXIT north

## 8. Shonk Street

- EXIT east

## 9. Docks

- TALK TO Raj (`Curry`)

## 10. Shonk Street

### 10.1. The Runny Inn

- USE `Curry` on table
- USE `Scissors` on Sydney (yourself) (`Dangle Friend`)

### 10.2. Alley

- USE `Stick` on dumpster
- USE dumpster
- TALK TO Terrence
- USE `Loose Fur` on Terrence (`Wig`)
- USE Syd's bin (`Bucket`)

### 10.3. Big Boy Publishing

- USE `Wig` on Sydney
- USE elevator

#### 10.3.1. Elevator

- USE emergency button
- TALK TO emergency button
- PICK UP bar (under the mirror) (`Bar`)
- USE `Bar` on escape door (`BH Talkie`)

#### 10.3.2. Office

- TALK TO Sufjan
- PICK UP collar (from the head sculpture) (`Collar`)
- USE `Collar` on `Dangle Friend` (`Dangle with Collar`)
- USE `Dangle with Collar` on `Rope` (`Dangle with Collar/Rope`)
- LOOK AT head sculpture
- TALK TO Sufjan
  - "I'm feeling hot"
- USE fan
- PICK UP magazine (`Big Boy Mag`)
- EXIT south and EXIT building

### 10.4. The Runny Inn

- USE `Big Boy Mag` on Mr. Smooth (`Big Suit`)

### 10.5. Big Boy Publishing

- TALK TO Bipasha

## 11. Docks

- TALK TO Raj
- USE `Bucket` on Raj (`Slush Bucket`)
- USE `Slush Bucket` on water
- USE `Dustpan` on fisherman
- TALK TO fisherman
- USE `BH Talkie` on fisherman (`Wet Shoes`)

## 12. Shonk Street

### 12.1. Pet Appreciation Center

- USE private door (you can't enter)

### 12.2. Big Boy Publishing

- TALK TO Bipasha
  - "Can we talk about jobs..."
  - "Do you know the password..."

### 12.3. Pet Appreciation Center

- USE private door
  - "Cat Fancier" (`Ribbon`)

#### 12.3.1. Back Room

- PICK UP hair dryer (`Hair Dryer`)
- USE `Scissors` on (`Big Suit`) (`Suit`)
- USE `Scissors` on `Ribbon` (`Tie`)
- USE `Hair Dryer` on power socket
- USE `Wet Shoes` on hair dryer (`Shoes`)

## 13. Bank Street

- EXIT upstairs on the left side

### 13.1. Business Corp

- USE Mr. Corps (the red chair)
