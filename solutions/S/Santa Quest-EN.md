## Walkthrough

Put the following inventory items in the stocking in the corresponding bed

1. Boat
2. Chess Set
3. Book
4. Hobby Horse
5. Trumpet
6. Blocks
7. Teddy
8. Football
9. Racing Car
10. Soldiers
11. Paints
12. Tea Set
