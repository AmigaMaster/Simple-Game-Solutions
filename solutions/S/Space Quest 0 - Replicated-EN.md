## 1. Labion Orbital Station 10

### 1.1. Lower laboratory

- Walk right into the upper maintenance door
  - LOOK
  - GET CROWBAR (`Crowbar`) (2/2)
- LOOK MAN
- SEARCH MAN
- GET CARD (`Keycard`) (1/3)
- LOOK CONSOLE at the lower left corner
- PUSH BUTTON (2/5)
- USE `Keycard` in the top left corner and enter elevator
- PUSH THREE

### 1.2. Main Hallway 3rd Floor

- EXIT east

### 1.3. Outer Hall

- ENTER left door

### 1.4. Main Bridge

- SEARCH MAN near the right door
- GET CHIP (`Chip`) (1/6)
- LOOK CONSOLE at the lower left corner (2/8)
- LOOK MONITOR in the middle of the back wall
  - **NOTE:** The colors and types
    1. Purple = Conference Request
    2. Blue = Docking Procedure
    3. Orange = Distress Situation
    4. Green = End Transmission
- EXIT west

### 1.5. Outer Hall

- READ NOTE from the right door
- EXIT east

### 1.6. Unfinished Room

- LOOK CONSOLE on the back wall
- USE `Chip` (1/9)
- EXIT west **3 times**

### 1.7. Escape Pods Room

- OPEN DOOR from the right escape pod
- ENTER POD

### 1.8. Escape Pod

- PUSH POWER (1/10)
- GET DISK (`Virus Disk`) (1/11)
- F4 to open inventory and select the `Virus Disk`
- USE THROTTLE LEVER (1/12)
- PUSH ORANGE (1/13)

- LOOK LOG which has fallen onto the rock **repeat until you receive a buckazoid** (`Buckazoids`) (2/18)

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME

- LOOK HUT
- GET BOARD (`Board`) (1/21)

- TALK MAN

- GET SOUP at the fireplace (2/24)

- READ PAPER on the table
- GET GLUE from the table (`Glue`) (1/25)
- EXIT hut, EXIT west to the tree and RE-ENTER hut

- OPEN CHEST
- SEARCH CHEST

### 2.8. Jungle Pond

- EXIT north

### 2.9. Huge Rock

- TALK ROCK
- PUT `Glue` on `Fruit` (`Sticky Fruit`) (1/27)
- FEED STONE (1/28)
- ENTER door

#### 2.9.1. Cave

- **ATTENTION:** Do not fall down the chasm
- DRINK WATER from the pool (3/30)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME
- USE BOARD (`1/31`)
- **ATTENTION:** You need to talk exactly in the middle of the board to cross the chasm and you have to **STOP** directly after you crossed it
- GET BOARD from the other side (`Board`) (1/32)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME and DO NOT WALK ONTO THE HOLES
- Walk between the holes
- EXIT northeast through the door

### 2.10. Waterfalls

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME and do not leave the path
- EXIT west

### 2.11. Glass Tunnel

- EXIT west

### 2.12. Laboratory

- GET SLIME DEVIL from the pedestal, you need to stand at top of the pedestal (`Devil`) (3/33)
- GET CARD from the floor under the console to the right (`Passcard`) (2/35)
- PUSH BUTTON on the console (1/36)
- GET BEAKER from the shelves (`Beaker`) (1/37)
- EXIT east

### 2.13. Glass Tunnel

- USE `Passcard` on the left door (1/38)
- ENTER room

### 2.14. Bedroom

- SEARCH MAN
- GET TOWEL from his hands (`Towel`, `Hanger`) (2/40)
- SEARCH COUCH (`Scrap`) (2/42)
- READ `Scrap`
- SEARCH SHELF
- GET CARTRIDGE (`Blue Cartridge`) (1/43)
- Move to a different part of the shelf
- SEARCH SHELF
- GET CARTRIDGE (`Green Cartridge`) (1/44)
- PUSH BUTTON on the treadmill and wait until the message "that's enough"
- USE `Beaker` (`Sweat`) (1/45)
- USE shower (1/46)
- EXIT room

### 2.15. Laboratory

- USE CONSOLE (`Salt`) (1/47)
- USE `Passcard` on door to the north

### 2.16. Basement

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME
- LOOK GENERATOR with the open panel
- **ATTENTION:** Stay in the shadow of the generator
- GET ACTUATOR (`Actuator`) (2/49)
- Pay attention to the guard and make your way to the green dumpster while staying in the shadows and then to the shadow left to the stairs
- USE CROWBAR when the guard comes down (1/50)
- SEARCH GUARD
- GET ARMOR (`Armor`) (1/51)
- GET RIFLE (`Rifle`) (1/52)
- WEAR `Armor`
- USE ACTUATOR on the power generator (1/53)
- EXIT upstairs

### 2.17. Shuttle Bay

- LOOK DOOR from the lift on the right
- GET HOSE from the cruiser (1/54)
- TALK MAN
- LOOK GEAR on the right side of the cruiser
- USE `Devil` ON BOOT (1/55)
- EXIT west

### 2.18. Backside of Shuttle bay

- TALK MAN (1/56)
- LOOK DOOR on the left side (1/57)

### 2.19. Shuttle Bay

- ENTER lift
- PUSH TWO

### 2.20. Space Port

- EXIT west into the souvenir shop

#### 2.20.1. Souvenir Shop

- SELL `Orium` (`100 Buckazoids`) (1/58)
- SELL `Rifle` (`100 Buckazoids`) (1/59)
- LEAVE

### 2.21. Space Port

- EXIT east into the bar

#### 2.21.1. Bar

- NOTE: When you leave and re-enter the bar, the band changes
- BUY BRANDY at the bar
- GET BRANDY (`Brandy`) (1/60)
- BUY ALE
- GET ALE (`Ale`) (1/61)
  - **NOTE:** If the bartender cuts you off, walk back to the Bedroom and USE SHOWER

### 2.22. Backside of Shuttle Bay

- GIVE `Ale` to man (10/71)
- SEARCH MAN
- GET KEY (`Key`) (1/72)

### 2.23. Space Port

#### 2.23.1. Souvenir Shop

- SELL `Armor` (1/73)

### 2.24. Space Port

- TALK TO ALIEN, the blue one
- BUY DROID (`Droid`) (1/74)
- GET DROID, the green one
- SEARCH BOX next to the man behind the blue alien
- GET PARTS (`Parts`) (3/77)

- LISTEN (1/78)

## 3. Shuttle

### 10.8. Cloning Facility

#### 10.8.1. Entrance Hall

- LOOK CONSOLE
- EXIT east

#### 10.8.2. Storehouse

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME and do not exit east
- LOOK CARTRIDGE in the computer in the middle
- GET CARTRIDGE (`Red Cartridge`) (1/94)
- INSERT `Blue Cartridge` (1/95)
- EXIT west

#### 10.8.3. Entrance Hall

- **QUICK:** Hide behind the console at the entrance
- Wait until the guard returns from the top left door
- EXIT north west

#### 10.8.4. Security Room

- LOOK COMPUTER
  - EJECT DISK (`Security Disk`) (1/96)
  - INSERT `Virus Disk` (1/97)
  - EXIT

#### 10.8.5. Entrance Hall

- EXIT north east

#### 10.8.6. Pathway

- USE `Rope` with `Hanger` (`Hook`) (1/98)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME
- Walkt to the edge but do not fall down
- THROW `Hook`
- SWING
- JUMP at the right time (1/99)
- EXIT north

#### 10.8.7. Power Room

- LOOK DOOR
- LOOK SHAFT
- CLIMB

#### 10.8.8. Maze

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME
- LOOK CABLES
- You need to SAVE often here. Make your way through the maze
- UP the orange cable until your head is over the highest point
- RIGHT to the green cable
- UP green cable to the top
- USE SWITCH
- DOWN the green cable to the orange horizontal cable
- Your head needs to be exactly in the middle of the orange cable
- LEFT on the orange cable to the switch
- USE SWITCH
- RIGHT to the green cable
- DOWN the green cable, to the thick pink cable
- LEFT along the thick pink cable to the switch
- USE SWITCH
- LEFT to the other switch
- USE SWITCH
- RIGHT to the light green cable
- UP the light green cable and STOP at the sparkles
- WAIT until the sparkling stops
- UP the light green cable
- USE SWITCH
- DOWN, again with the timing between the sparkles to the curvy green cable
- RIGHT along the curved green cable until it straightens up
- USE SWITCH
- UP

#### 10.8.9. Production Room

- EXIT west

#### 10.8.10. Walkway

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME
- LOOK
- USE `Towel` with `Brandy` (`Cocktail`) (1/100)
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME
- Walk to the centre of the platform
- LIGHT `Towel` while the wind is blowing
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME
- EXIT west (1/101)

#### 10.8.11. Fighting Area

- Use A and Z keys for high and low attacks and try to win the fight. You do not need to block. (1/102)
