# Slug Princess - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 27.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/slug-princess)

## 1. Castle Entrance

- EXIT north

### 1.1. Throne Room

- LOOK AT right banner (`Thread`)
- EXIT east

## 2. Castle Entrance

- EXIT east

## 3. Forest Path

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
  - Do not walk past the 4th tree from the left
- LOOK AT undergrowth (`Thorn`)
- EXIT west, south

## 4. Rock Path

- EXIT southwest

## 5. Northern Lake

- LOOK AT loose stones (`Stone`)
- USE `Stone` right next to the lowest lily pad (it floats to the left)
- LOOK AT loose stones (`Stone`)
- EXIT south

## 6. Southern Lake

- WALK onto the fallen tree and EXIT north (you step onto the lily pad)

## 7. Northern Lake

- USE `Stone` left to the middle lily pad
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
  - Do not step on the small lily pad
- LOOK AT reed (`Reed`)
- USE `Reed` on small lily pad

### 7.1. Underwater

- LOOK AT boot (`Small Emerald`)
- EXIT east

## 8. Northern Lake

- USE `Thorn` on `Reed` (you require the `Thread` to do this) (`Axe`)
- LOOK AT loose stones (`Stone`)
- EXIT east

## 9. Rock Path

- LOOK AT bottom right berries (`Ruby Ring`)
- EXIT north **2 times**

## 10. Castle Entrance

### 10.1. Throne Room

- USE `Small Emerald` on King (30)
- USE `Ruby Ring` on king (70)

## 11. Forest Path

- USE `Axe` on handkerchief (`Handkerchief`)

## 12. Rock Path

- EXIT east

## 13. Tree Base

- WALK UP the vine

### 13.1. Tree Stem

- LOOK AT hole (`Silver Brooch`)
- WALK to the left branch
- USE `Handkerchief` on castle

## 14. Castle Entrance

### 14.1. Bedroom

- Walk onto nightstand
- USE `Stone` on crown
- USE `Axe` on crown (`Small Crown`)
- USE `Handkerchief` on window

### 14.2. Throne Room

- USE `Small Crown` on king (320)
- USE `Silver Brooch` on king (340)
- LOOK AT banner (`Thread`)

## 15. Forest Path

- USE undergrowth (`Torn`)

## 16. Tree Base

- EXIT south

## 17. Ground Depression

- USE `Thorn` on hole
- EXIT west **3 times**

## 18. Northern Lake

- USE loose stones (`Stone`)
- EXIT south

## 19. Southern Lake

- USE `Axe` on pink creature
- EXIT northeast, east

## 20. Rock Path

- LOOK AT bottom right berries (`Berry`)

## 21. Ground Depression

- USE `Berry` on hole
- USE `Stone` on hole

## 22. Tree Base

### 22.1. Tree Stem

- WALK to the left branch
- USE `Handkerchief` on small rock under the castle

## 23. Rock Path

- LOOK AT hole (on the top of the rock) (`Golden Statue`)

## 24. Northern Lake

- LOOK AT loose stones (`Gold Coin`)

## 25. Castle Entrance

### 25.1. Throne Room

- USE `Golden Statue` on king (389)
- USE `Gold Coin` on king (399)

## 26. Castle Entrance

- LOOK AT mud (`Silver Coin`)

## 27. Rock Path

- LOOK AT bottom right berries (`Berry`)

## 28. Tree Base

### 28.1. Tree Stem

- USE `Handkerchief` on rock in the bottom left corner

## 29. Southern Lake

- USE `Berry` on the ground in front of the pink creature
- EXIT southeast

## 30. Creature Path

- EXIT south

## 31. Cave Entrance

- USE `Handkerchief` on smoke

## 32. Treasure Cave

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- WALK east
- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Thread` on snake
- LOOK AT stone (`Stone`)
- LOOK AT thorn (`Thorn`)
- LOOK AT berry (`Berry`)
- WALK east
- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Stone` on monster
- WALK east
- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Thorn` on monster
- WALK east
- **QUICK:**
  - USE `Berry` on monster
- LOOK AT gold ingot (`Gold Ingot`)
- LOOK AT silver ingot (`Silver Ingot`)
- WALK TO the yellow spot
- LOOK AT gem (a little bit in the upper part of the white spot) **2 times** (`Gem`)
  - **NOTE:** You need to stand directly beside the brightest spot otherwise it will not work and the hotspot is very small

## 33. Ground Depression

- EXIT east

## 34. Umbrella Sales-Alien

- USE `Silver Coin` on umbrella sales-alien (`Umbrella`)
- EXIT northwest

## 35. Forest Path

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
  - Do not walk past the2nd tree from the right
- USE `Umbrella` on spider
- EXIT west

## 36. Castle Entrance

- EXIT west

## 37. Rainy Forest

- USE `Umbrella` on prince (the prince is following you now) (`Silver Coin`)
- EXIT east

## 38. Castle Entrance

### 38.1. Throne Room

- USE `Gold Ingot` on king (599)
- USE `Silver Ingot` on king (699)
- USE `Silver Coin` on king (700)
- USE `Gem` on king (2000)
