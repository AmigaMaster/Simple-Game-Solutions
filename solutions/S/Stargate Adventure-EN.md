## 3. Chulak

### 3.1. Stargate

### 3.4. Clearing

### 3.5. Wilderness

- LOOK AT `Map of Chulak`

### 3.6. Ruins

- TAKE backback (`Brush`, `Flashlight`, `Medalion`)

### 3.7. Town Entrance

#### 3.7.1. Marketplace

- GIVE `Flashlight` to fruit merchant
  - 3 Coins - "YES" (`Coins`)
- USE `Medalion` with fruit and artefact merchant
  - 1 Coin - "YES" (`Coins`)
- BUY rope for 3 Coins from merchant in the middle (`Rope`)
- BUY horn for 3 Coins from the merchant in the middle (`Horn`)

### 3.8. Ruins

- USE `Metal rod` with pillar
- USE `Rope` with small part of pillar
- USE tied rope with old tree
- TAKE rope (`Rope`)
- USE `Rope` with big part of pillar
- Climb down

#### 3.8.1. Riddle Room

- LOOK AT tablet on the left wall
- **Riddle:** Stone Blocks
  - You have to make a path
  - USE the colored buttons to move the stones
  - The purple button changes a block
  - Solution (P=purple, R=red, B=blue, G=gold, G=green):
  - ```txt
    --\
      |
      |
    /-+-\
    |   |
    ```
  1. P7, R2
  2. P7, B, G
  3. P, G
  4. P4, B2, R
  5. P7, R, B2, R, B
  6. P7, B, R
  7. P7, G
  8. P, G
  9. P3, R2
  10. P, G
- USE `Bottle full of water` with hole
- Go downstairs

#### 3.8.2. Cave

- TAKE artefact (`Artefact`)
- Walk upstairs

### 3.9. Town Entrance

- USE `Bottle` with funnel (`Bottle full of water`)

### 3.10. Ruins

#### 3.10.1. Cave

- TAKE shill harm (`Shill Harm tablet`)
- LOOK AT `Shill Harm Tablet` (`Tablet`, `Shill Harm Crystal`)
- Exit upstairs and climb up to the ruins
