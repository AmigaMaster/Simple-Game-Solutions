# SAW Deluxe - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 22.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/1215-saw-deluxe-remake-)

## 1. Toilets

- SWITCH TO Carl

> Carl

- LOOK AT `Piece of Paper`
- PICK UP plug (from the left urinal) (`Plug`)
- PICK UP axe (`Axe`)
- GIVE `Plug` and `Tape 2` to Allan
- SWITCH to Allan

> Allan

- LOOK AT sewer
  - USE `Plug` with tape recorder rope (`Recorder`)
- USE `Tape 1` with `Recorder`
- USE `Tape 2` with `Recorder`
  - **NOTE:** `Multiply your heads`
- TALK TO Carl
- USE lid of the tank (of the right toilet)
- PICK UP lid (`Lid of the Tank`)
- PICK UP bag (in the tank) (`Bag`)
- LOOK AT `Bag` (`Saw (without blade)`, `Rope with Hook`, `Piece of Mirror`, `Note`)
- LOOK AT `Note`
- LOOK AT washbasin
  - LOOK AT inscription
  - EXIT view
- USE `Piece of Mirror` with you
  - **NOTE:** Number `7`
- TALK TO Carl
  - "Carl, show me your neck, maybe you've got another number."
    - **NOTE:** Number `9`
- USE number box
  - `63`
  - ENTER
  - USE blank key (top right)
  - PICK UP envelope (`Envelope`)
  - EXIT
- LOOK AT `Envelope` (`Key 2`, `Message`)
- LOOK AT `Message`
- GIVE `Key 1` and `Key 2` to Carl
- SWITCH TO Carl

> Carl

- USE `Axe` with chain with padlock
- LOOK AT box
  - USE `Key 2` with lock
- LOOK AT open box
  - PICK UP tape (`Tape 3`)
  - EXIT
- GIVE `Tape 3` to Allan
- SWITCH TO Allan

> Allan:

- USE `Tape 3` with `Recorder`
- SWITCH TO Carl

> Carl

- LOOK AT open box
  - PICK UP tape (`Tape 3`)
  - USE valve
  - USE switch 1
  - USE switch 4
- USE wall tile
- PICK UP box (`Box`)
- USE `Key 1` with `Box` (`Note`, `Mobile Phone`, `Saw Blade`, `Stones`)
- LOOK AT `Note`
- USE `Stones` with (left) mirror
- PICK UP flashlight (`Flashlight without Batteries`, `Torn Piece of Paper`)
- LOOK AT Piece of Paper`
- LOOK AT open box
  - USE switch 1 (to turn it on)
- SWITCH TO Allan

> Allan

- LOOK AT washbasin
  - USE `Plug` with washbasin
  - USE (right) handle
  - EXIT
- LOOK AT sink
  - USE (right) handle
  - EXIT
- PICK UP floor tile (`Floor Tile`)
  - PICK UP brick (5th row, 2nd brick)
  - PICK UP paper (`Message`)
  - PICK UP drawing (`Torn Piece of Paper`)
  - EXIT
- GIVE `Torn Piece of Paper` to Carl
- SWITCH TO Carl

> Carl

- USE `Torn Piece of Paper` with `Torn Piece of Paper` (`Combined Papers`)
- LOOK AT `Combined Papers`
  - **NOTE:** `Up, down, down, up, down`
  - EXIT
- LOOK AT open box
  - SET the switches according to the `Combined Paper`:
    1. Down
    2. Up
    3. Up
    4. Down
    5. Up
  - EXIT
- **NOTE:** The arrow next to the word "exit" is pointing upwards
- LOOK AT arrow
- LOOK AT roof (above the arrow)
  - LOOK AT exit
  - EXIT
- GIVE `Saw Blade` to Allan
- GIVE `Flashlight without Batteries` to Allan
- SWITCH TO Allan

> Allan

- USE `Saw Blade` with `Saw (without blade)` (`Saw`)
- LOOK AT `Message`
  - **NOTE:** Something with a T
- USE tap (of the bathtub)
- USE `Lid of the Tank` with bathtub
- USE revolving valve (on the pipes in the corner)
- USE `Saw` with T-formed pipe (between the filled sink and broken toilet) (`T-formed pipe`)
- LOOK AT `T-formed pipe` (`Key 3`)
- GIVE `Rope with Hook` to Carl
- SWITCH TO Carl

> Carl

- USE `Axe` with pipe (the big vertical one)
- SWITCH TO Allan

> Allan

- USE `Floor Tile` with sewer
- LOOK AT `Recorder` (`Batteries`)
- USE `Batteries` with `Flashlight without Batteries` (`Flashlight with Batteries`)
- USE `Flashlight with Batteries` with dark exit
- SWITCH TO Carl

> Carl

- USE `Rope with Hook` with ladder (in the exit)
- USE rope
- SWITCH TO Allan

> Allan

- USE dirty water (`Dirty Box`)
- USE `Key 3` with `Dirty Box` (`Key 4`, `Note`)
- LOOK AT `Note`
- USE `Key 4` on you
- GIVE `Key 4` to Carl
- SWITCH TO Carl

> Carl

- USE `Key 4` on you
- USE ladder
