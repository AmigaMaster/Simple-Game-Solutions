# Snarl - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 21.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [itch.io](https://beyondbooleans.itch.io/snarl)

## 1. Diner

- ENTER rest room

## 2. Rest Room

- USE cleaning locker
- PICK UP electrical tape (`Electrical Tape`)
- EXIT rest room

## 3. Diner

- ENTER door to kitchen

## 4. Kitchen

- PICK UP towels (`Clean Towels`)
- PICK UP spatula (`Spatula`)
- EXIT kitchen

## 5. Diner

- USE `Clean Towels` on `Electrical Tape` (`Clean Towel`, `Makeshift Bandage`)
- USE `Makeshift Bandage` on wounded man
- USE `Spatula` on freezer (`Ice`)
- USE `Ice` on `Clean Towel` (`Ice Pack`)
- USE `Ice Pack` on wounded man

## 6. Outside

- WALK east
- TALK TO gray-skinned man
- EXIT west

## 7. Changed Diner

- LOOK AT diner floor (under the dog)
- USE wounded man
  - LOOK AT glowing growth
  - USE glowing growth
  - EXIT
- TALK TO detective Ben Drummle
  - "The injured man..."
  - "The floor over there..."
- TALK TO waitress (Sandra)
- TALK TO huge dog
  - "The floor around you..." (`Garm`)
- PICK UP piece of leathery flesh (from the floor) (`Piece Of Flesh`)
- PICK UP ornate chalice (`Empty Ornate Chalice`)
- USE `Garm` on book
- USE book
  - **NOTE:** The three new lines at the end completed the riddle
    ```
    Drink deeply, child.
    Drink of my words,
    drink oy my land,
    drink of my blood.
    Only then shall the
    way become clear,
    and your will shall
    command whoever
    slakes their thirst.
    ```
- USE `Garm` on barred doorway
- ENTER doorway

## 8. Study

- PICK UP piece of leathery flesh (`Piece Of Flesh`)
- TALK TO The Witness
- EXIT room

## 9. Changed Diner

- USE pedestal and figurines
  - PICK UP scroll (`Scroll`)
  - LOOK AT `Scroll`
  - **NOTE:**
    1. Peasant feeds soldiers & is beholden to the priest
    2. King is held account by scribe & keeps tight rein on priest
    3. Soldier is vulnerable to hunger & enforces will of king over scribes with steel
    4. Priest controls peasant & surrenders to king
    5. Scribe holds king to account & has no defense against soldier
  - PICK UP **all four** (one is attached to the pedestal) figurines (`King Figurine`, `Scribe Figurine`, `Priest Figurine`, `Soldier Figurine`)
  - LOOK AT figurine (on the pedestal)
    - **NOTE:** It carries a scythe, so it must be the peasant
    - You can simply guess the first combination with the peasant
    - Peasant against priest (top left) & soldier (top right)
    - King against scribe and priest (already in the top left, so king must be in the bottom right)
    - Scribe against king (already in the bottom right, so scribe in the bottom left)
    - USE `Empty Ornate Chalice` on pool of blood (`Chalice Filles With Blood`)
- "Try to comfort Martha."
- "Reason with Martha."
- ENTER shadowy archway

## 10. Statue Room

- PICK UP piece of leathery flesh (Piece of flesh`)
- USE `Hand Gun` on bronze bowl
- PICK UP writhing tongue (`Tongue`)

## 11. Changed Diner

- "Reason with Tom."
- "Appeal to emotion."
- USE `Tongue` on `Chalice Filles With Blood` (`Chalice With Blood & Tongue`)

## 12. Statue Room

- EXIT west

## 13. Chest Room

- PICK UP piece of leathery flesh (`Piece Of Flesh`)
- USE stone chest
  - USE **all four** `Piece Of Flesh` on indentation
- USE stone chest (`Black Sand`)

## 14. Changed Diner

- "Admonish Layton."
- "Placate Layton."
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE `Black Sand` on `Chalice With Blood & Tongue` (`Chalice With Sapphire Wine`)
- Ending 1: "Choose to drink the wine"
- To continue with the game: "Choose not to drink the wine."
- ENTER doorway

## 15. Study

- USE `Chalice With Sapphire Wine` on The Witness
  - Keep talking
  - PICK UP Scalpel (`Scalpel`)
- EXIT east

## 16. Changed Diner

- USE wounded man
  - USE `Scalpel` on glowing growth (`Shard Of Purple Glass`)
- LOOK AT `Shard Of Purple Glass`
- TALK TO detective Ben Drummle
- USE `Garm` on small stone pedestal
- USE `Shard Of Purple Glass` on brazier (`Heated Glass`)
- USE `Heated Glass` on freezer
