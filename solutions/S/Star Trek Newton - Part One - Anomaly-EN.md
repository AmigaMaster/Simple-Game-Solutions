# Star Trek Newton: Part One - Anomaly - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------
|   1.0   | 18.02.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/star-trek-newton-part-one-anomaly)

## 1. Deck 5

### 1.1. Left Hallway

#### 1.1.1. Your Quarter

- EXIT west

### 1.2. Left Hallway

- USE turbolift door
- ENTER quarters door

#### 1.2.1. Your Quarter

- USE storage cupboard controls
- PICK UP toolkit (`Toolkit`)
- LOOK AT `Toolkit` (`Tricorder`, `Spanner`)

### 1.3. Left Hallway

- USE `Spanner` on maintenance panel
- LOOK AT turbolift control conduits
- USE section 5 alpha power distribution terminal (the black one)
- USE `Spanner` on turbolift control conduits

#### 1.3.1. Your Quarter

- USE replicator control
- PICK UP freshly replicated water (`Glass of Water`)

### 1.4. Left Hallway

- USE `Glass of Water` on crewman D'Brell
- EXIT east

### 1.5. Right Hallway

- USE ladder

## 2. Deck 4

### 2.1. Hallway

- USE `Tricorder` on Lieutenant Nickolas Solomon
- USE Lieutenant Nickolas Solomon **2 times**
- ENTER sickbay

#### 2.1.1. Sickbay

- USE medical computer
- USE supply cupboard controls
- PICK UP vial of purple liquid (`Red Vial`)
- PICK UP vial of blue liquid (`Blue Vial`)
- PICK UP vial of red liquid (`Red Vial`)
- USE `Tricorder` on `Red Vial`
- USE medical computer
- EXIT north

### 2.2. Hallway

- USE ladder

## 3. Deck 5

### 3.1. Right Hallway

- ENTER recreation suite door

#### 3.1.1. Recreation Suite

- LOOK AT various books (shelf on the left)
- USE various books
- PICK UP emergency medical kit (`Hypospray`, `Yellow Vial`)
- EXIT room

## 4. Deck 4

### 4.1. Hallway

#### 4.1.1. Sickbay

- USE `Blue Vial` on mixing station
- USE `Yellow Vial` on mixing station

### 4.2. Hallway

- USE `Hypospray` on Lieutenant Nickolas Solomon

#### 4.2.1. Sickbay

- TALK TO Lieutenant Nickolas Solomon **2 times**
  - "I think someone..."
  - **NOTE:** Lieutenant Nickolas Solomon has to leave the room in order to progress in the game

## 5. Deck 5

### 5.1. Right Hallway

#### 5.1.1. Recreation Suite

- PICK UP personal communicator (`Personal Communicator`)
- USE `Personal Communicator` on Crewman John Fischer (yourself)

### 5.2. Right Hallway

- USE `Personal Communicator` on Crewman John Fischer

## 6. Deck 4

### 6.1. Hallway

#### 6.1.1. Sickbay

- EXIT west

### 6.2. Left Corridor

- USE `Personal Communicator` on Crewman John Fischer
- EXIT east

### 6.3. Right Corridor

- LOOK AT master systems display
- USE master systems display
- USE open access hatch
- USE turbolift shaft emergency ladder

#### 6.3.1. Shaft

- CLIMB to the top
- LOOK AT interesting blue conduits
- LOOK AT damaged yellow conduit
- EXIT down

### 6.4. Right Corridor

- PICK UP bar (`Yellow Bar`)
- USE access to main corridor
- EXIT west

### 6.5. Left Corridor

- TALK TO Crewman Alice Hartmann

### 6.6. Right Corridor

- USE `Spanner` on Crewman Alice Hartmann
- USE console (on the right wall)
- USE open access hatch
- USE turbolift shaft emergency ladder

### 6.7. Shaft

- CLIMB up
- USE `Yellow Bar` on open door

## 7. Bridge

- USE engineering station (on the right)
- USE communications station
- EXIT east (into the turbolift)
