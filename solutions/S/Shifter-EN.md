# Shifter - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 13.03.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/shifter-2012)

## 1. Day

### 1.1. City Gate

- TALK TO (left) guard
- TALK TO (right) guard
- TALK TO girl in the lower left (Pan)
- TALK TO girl with the purple hair (`Apple`)
- EXIT east (using the right arrow in the icon bar)

### 1.2. House Entrance

- TALK TO boy with the green hair (Moki)

## 2. Night

### 2.1. House Entrance

- TALK TO guard
  - "Your brother is looking for you Burr."
- EXIT west

### 2.2. City Gate

- TALK TO Moki
  - "Why are you sitting outside?"
- TALK TO guard
  - "I found your brother..."

## 3. Day

### 3.1. City Gate

- TALK TO girl with the purple hair
  - "Do you know that poor bloke with the green hair?"

### 3.2. House Entrance

- TALK TO Moki
  - "That purple haired girl seems to like you." (Blizz)
- SHIFT INTO Blizz
  - Hair color: Purple
  - Night work: Bath house
  - Name: Blizz

## 4. Night

### 4.1. House Entrance

- TALK TO guard

### 4.2. City Gate

- TALK TO Moki
- SWITCH TO yourself

### 4.3. House Entrance

- TALK TO Blizz
  - "I think Moki likes you a lot too..."

## 5. Day

### 5.1. City Gate

- TALK TO Pan
  - "Your friend told me you found a treasure..."
- USE `Apple` on Pan (`Key`)

### 5.2. House Entrance

- USE `Key` on Moki

## 6. Night

### 6.1. City Gate

- TALK TO Ishtar
  - "I heard you cloud get me some papers..."

### 6.2. House Entrance

- TALK TO Blizz
  - "Ishtar sent me." (`Package`)

### 6.3. City Gate

- USE `Package` on Ishtar (`Papers`)

## 7. Day

### 7.1. City Gate

- SHIFT INTO Pan
  - Father: Burr
  - Food: Apples
  - Daytime Activity: She helps her grandmother

### 7.2. House Entrance

- TALK TO Moki
  - "Hi Moki!"
  - "My mother?"

### 7.3. City Gate

- SHIFT INTO Burr (the left guard)
  - Night Activity: He gets drunk in the bar
  - Children: 2
  - Most exciting thing: Captured an enemy soldier in the city
- USE `Papers` on right guard
