# Snow Queen: After The Apocalypse - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 09.03.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/snow-queen-after-the-apocalypse)

## 1. Bedroom

- PICK UP (middle) pillow (`Pillow`)
- PICK UP flower branch (from the vase) (`Frozen Rose`)
- EXIT south

## 2. Main Hall

- EXIT west

## 3. Portal Room

- TALK TO Morgus
- LOOK AT stained glass
- EXIT east

## 4. Main Hall

- EXIT west

## 5. Study

- PICK UP empty jar (`Large Empty Jar`)
- PICK UP sleep powder (`Sleep Powder`)
- USE `Icy Touch` with thick ice (in the window)
- OPEN window
- PUSH first grey button
- PUSH red button
- PUSH lever
- EXIT south

## 6. Portal Room

- PICK UP pieces of broken glass (`Shards of colorful Glass`)
- PICK UP wisps
- TALK TO Morgus
  - "How can I catch the wisps?"
- USE `Frozen Rose` with `Large Empty Jar` (`Jar with a Rose inside`)
- USE `Jar with a Rose inside` with wisps (`Jar o' Wisps`)

## 7. Main Hall

- EXIT north

## 8. Garden

- TALK TO Aine
- TALK TO Sylda (at the tree on the right)
- LOOK AT **all** large sheet of ice (on the right side)
  - **NOTE:** One of them has stains and scratches on it
- PUSH large sheet of ice (the one with the scratches)
- PICK UP shards of ice (`Shard of ice`)
- USE `Shard of ice` with `Pillow` (`Ripped Pillow`)
- OPEN `Ripped Pillow` (`Soft Goose Feather`)
- GIVE `Soft Goose Feather` to Sylda
- GIVE `Jar o' Wisps` to Sylda
- GIVE `Shard of colorful Glass` to Sylda
- GIVE `Sleep Powder` to Sylda
- TALK TO Aine

## 9. Wall of Snow

- USE `Icy Touch` with wall of snow
- USE entrance to the camp

## 10. Camp

- USE `Shard if ice` with large tent
- USE hole (`Dress`)
- USE `Sleep Powder` with `Northern Wind`
- WALK TO lighted are in the northeast

### 10.1. Christmas Tree

- USE `Icy Touch` with power generator
- USE `Shards of colorful Glass` with `Northern Wind`
- USE `Jar o' Wisps` with glass shards
- EXIT south
