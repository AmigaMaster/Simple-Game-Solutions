## 1. Archiving Room

- USE cupboard (on the right)
- PICK UP harddrive (`Harddrive`)
- LOOK AT jacket
  - **NOTE:** `E. Miller`
- USE jacket (`Note`)
- LOOK AT `Note`
  - **NOTE:** The underlined letters `OEESE`
- PICK UP key (from the left cupboard door) (`Key`)
- LOOK AT `Key`
  - USE button (`Keys`)
  - EXIT
- USE `Harddrive` on computer
- USE computer
  - USE harddrive
    - LOOK AT label
      - **NOTE:** Secondary drive `11`
    - USE the four switches `Up (1), up (2), down (0), up (8)` (1+2+0+8=`11`)
    - EXIT
  - ENTER `usersearch`
  - `miller`
    - **NOTE:** His username `millerlite`
  - `usersearch`
  - `gibson`
  - EXIT
- USE `Keys` on drawer (at the top left desk)
- USE drawer (`Folder`)
- LOOK AT `Wallet`
  - PICK UP multitool (`Multitool`)
  - EXIT
- LOOK AT `Baseball Cap`
  - LOOK AT ? (a small string in the left)
  - USE `Multitool` with wire (`Wire`)
- LOOK AT `Folder`
  - USE `Wire` on lock
  - PICK UP contents (`Disk`)
  - EXIT
- LOOK AT notice
  - **NOTE:** All passwords are 8 letters, built from two four letter words. Certain characters may be replaced by numbers.
- LOOK AT note
- USE terminal
  - Username `millerlite`
  - Password:
    - Remember the hint: Two four letter words
    - Looking at the `Note`, and seeing the underlined letters, the words are `rope` and `ease`
    - Remember the hint: Several letters may be replaced by numbers
    - `r0p33a53`
  - Email
    - Spam
    - Switch to the last email
    - CLICK on the link
    - CLICK on the http address
- Choose as you like
