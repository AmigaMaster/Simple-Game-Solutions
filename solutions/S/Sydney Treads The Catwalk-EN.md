# Sydney Treads The Catwalk - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 22.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/sydney-treads-the-catwalk)

## 1. Business Corps

### 1.1. Office

- EXIT southeast

### 1.2. Reception

- LOOK AT photo frame (on her desk)
- EXIT southwest

## 2. Syd's Alley

- **NOTE:** You find the spot in the middle of the map
- USE package (left to Syd's mail in the trashcan) (`Package`)
- USE `Scissors` on `Package` (`Stuffed Elephant`, `Card`)
- USE Terrence's dumpster
- TALK TO Terrence
- USE `Card` on Terrence (`Card from Peta`)
- EXIT east

## 3. Business Corps

### 3.1. Reception

- USE `Stuffed Elephant` on Bipasha
- TALK TO Bipasha

## 4. Raj's Diner

- **NOTE:** You can find this location on the west side on the map
- TALK TO Raj **5 times**
- PICK UP glass (`Glass`)
- TALK TO Melanie
- EXIT west

## 5. Fashion Show

### 5.1. Street

- TALK TO Bernie
- EXIT east

## 6. Syd's Alley

- USE Terrence's dumpster
- TALK TO Terrence
  - "You wouldn't know how to get into the fashion show?"
- Walk to the right and select the boxing hall on the lower left.  

## 7. Boxing Hall

- TALK TO Mel
- PICK UP boxing gloves (`Boxing Gloves`)
- TALK TO boxer
- EXIT north

## 8. Business Corps

### 8.1. Reception

- TALK TO Bipasha
  - "Do you have any friends in the fashion industry?"
- USE `Glass` on water fall (`Glass of Water`)

## 9. Boxing Hall

- USE `Glass of Water` on boxer (`Glass`)
  - **REPEAT** these steps until the boxer had three glasses of water

## 10. Raj's Diner

- USE `Boxing Gloves` on Raj (`Terrence`, `Mel`)
- TALK TO Melanie
  - "Do you know where Raj is?"

## 11. Hospital

- TALK TO Raj
- PICK UP matches (`Matches`)

## 12. Fashion Show

### 12.1. Show

- TALK TO Claire DeValet
- USE `Card from Peta`on `Matches` (`Charred Card`)
- TALK TO Claire DeValet
- EXIT northwest

## 13. Business Corps

### 13.1. Reception

- TALK TO Bipasha
  - "You'd make a great model!"

## 14. Raj's Diner

- TALK TO Melanie
  - "Have you ever considered modelling?"

## 15. Hospital

- TALK TO Raj
  - "What can you tell me about Dr. Biggs?"
  - Ask about Dr. Biggs and he’ll tell you he want’s to be with Bipasha again. Leave the hospital and go to Bipasha.

## 16. Business Corps

### 16.1. Reception

- TALK TO Bipasha

## 17. Hospital

- TALK TO Raj
  - "Raj...about Bipasha..."

## 18. Raj’s Diner

- TALK TO Melanie
  - "I made sure Raj got back okay for you."

## 19. Fashion Show

### 19.1. Show

- EXIT upstairs

### 19.2. Cellar

- LOOK AT pipe
- PICK UP skeleton arm (`Bone`)
- USE `Bone` on pipe
- TALK TO pipe
- USE rat **3 times** (`Rat`)
- PICK UP handkerchief (`Handkerchief`)
- USE `Handkerchief` on Sydney (`Handkerchief with Snot`)
- USE `Charred Card` on `Handkerchief with Snot` (`Handkerchief with Message`)
- USE `Handkerchief with Message` on `Rat` (`Rat with Message`)
- USE `Rat with Message` on pipe

### 19.3. Backstage

- EXIT northeast

### 19.4. Show

- PICK UP rope (`Pulley`, `Rope`)

## 20. Raj’s Diner

- TALK TO Raj
- TALK TO Melanie

## 21. Fashion Show

### 21.1. Backstage

- USE clothes

### 21.2. Cellar

- TALK TO Sydney
  - "We're all out of ideas."
- USE closed door

## 22. Hospital

- PICK UP dentures (`Dentures`)

## 23. Raj's Diner

- USE `Dentures` on sea rope
- PICK UP life saver (`Life Saver`)

## 24. Fashion Show

### 24.1. Cellar

- USE cobweb
- USE `Pulley` on hook (behind the cobweb)
- USE `Rope` on `Life Saver` (`Life Saver with Rope`)
- USE `Life Saver with Rope` on pulley

### 24.2. Backstage

- EXIT northeast
- USE clothes
