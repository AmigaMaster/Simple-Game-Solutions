## 1. Walkthrough

- LOOK AT TV
- USE (TV) button (on the console below)
- USE (lightning) button (under the lightning sign)
- USE (TV) button (You talk to the alien)
- TALK TO purple critter
- USE console
  - EXIT console view
- USE slot (of the console at the green wall)
- TALK TO purple critter
  - "What's the big green wall here?"
  - "How can yer open the air tube?"
  - "Can I have yer card?"
- PICK UP windshield critter (`Critter`)
- USE `Critter` on panel (on the left wall)
- USE compartment (`Paper`)
- LOOK AT `Paper`
  - **NOTE:** The torn codes
  - EXIT view
- USE console (at the alien tank)
  - Remember the codes from the `Paper`
  - The code is `7258` (When you number the symbols from left to right and top to bottom)
- TALK TO purple critter
  - "I got yer air flowin'"
  - "Can I have yer card?" (`Card`)
- USE `Card` with slot
- PICK UP device (`Device`)
- TALK TO Tater
- USE `Device` on poster
- USE (TV) button
  - "Yer have to turn the tater back to normal!"
- WALK right
- TALK TO girlie
