## 1. Part 1: Rays of Hope

### 1.1. Forest

- USE yourself (`Sports Magazine`) (1/1)
- LOOK AT `Sports Magazine` **2 times** (1/2)
- LOOK AT foliage at the lower right side (shape of a heart)
- USE foliage
- PICK UP shiny object between the foliage (`Fish Hook`) (2/4)
- EXIT east

### 1.2. Plateau

- EXIT north west

### 1.3. Cave

- USE hole in the left tree (`Can`) (2/6)
- LOK AT `Can`
- USE `Can` with fireplace (`Black Can`) (3/9)
- LOOK AT `Black Can`

### 1.4. Forest

- USE `Black Can` with light ray (`Termite Food`) (10/19)

### 1.5. Plateau

- USE `Termite Food` with pole behind the termite hill (7/26)
- PICK UP cane (`Cane`) (1/27)

### 1.6. Forest

- EXIT west

### 1.7. Small Waterfall

- USE `Cane` with `Fish Hook` (`Fishing Rod`) (3/29)
- USE `Fishing Rod` with handle (`Handle`) (4/33)
- USE `Handle` with stuck axe (`Axe Blade`) (3/36)
- USE `Handle` with resin (`Sticky Stick`) (2/38)
- USE `Sticky Stick` with `Axe Blade` (`Rusty Axe`) (1/39)
- USE `Rusty Axe` with river (8/47)

### 1.8. Cave

- USE `Rusty Axe` with stones from the fireplace (`Axe`) (10/57)

### 1.9. Plateau

- EXIT south

### 1.10. Big Waterfall

- USE `Axe` with vine (`Handle`) (7/64)
- USE vine **3 times** (`Dry Vine`) (2/66)
- USE `Dry Vine` with waterfall (`Wet Vine`) (2/68)
- ENTER waterfall

### 1.11. Behind the Waterfall

- LOOK AT heart-shaped thing at the lower left stone
- PICK UP heart-shaped thing (`Hard Chewing Gum`) (1/69)
- USE `Hard Chewing Gum` with yourself (`Old Chewing Gum`) (5/74)
- USE `Old Chewing Gum` with bowl next to the entrance (3/77)
- USE `Wet Vine` with bowl (2/79)
- EXIT north west

### 1.12. Cliff

- Walk into the transporter to get tips
- USE `Axe` with small tree (1/80)
- USE `Fishing Rod` with red rock (`Iron Ore`) (2/82)
- USE `Magazine` with green X in front of the entrance (1/83)
- USE `Iron Ore` with magazine on the ground (1/84)
- TALK TO loose stone above the entrance (3/87)
- USE `Handle` with stone on the X (`Iron Powder`) (2/89)
- USE `Iron Powder` with teleporter (4/93)
- Walk through the passage
- USE sign (5/98)
- Walk into the teleporter (7/105)
- EXIT east

### 1.13. Slow Motion

- USE balloon as soon as its handle is in reach (3/108)
