# Space Adventure - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 25.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

## 1. Cave

- USE crack (in the wall, left from where you stand) (`Rock`)
- EXIT east

## 2. Western Lake

- EXIT east

## 3. Eastern Lake

- EXIT east

## 4. Pillars

- USE stone right of the pillars (`Pail`)

## 5. Western Lake

- USE `Pail` on lake (`Pail with Water`)
- LOOK AT `Pail with Water`
- USE `Pail with Water` (right-click) (`Fish`) (5/5)
- EXIT east **3 times**

## 6. Ramp

- USE `Rock` on big rock on the right of the guards
- ENTER tunnel

## 7. Tunnel

- EXIT east

## 8. Tubes

- EXIT up

## 9. Cliff

- EXIT east

## 10. Huts

- ENTER left hut

### 10.1. Left Hut

- PICK UP log (`Log`)
- EXIT south

## 11. Huts

- ENTER right hut

### 11.1. Right Hut

- TALK TO alien (`Microchip`)
- PICK UP stone (from the shelf on the right) (`Stone`)
- USE `Microchip` on `Stone` (`Stone with Microchip`)
- USE blue pot (`Keycard`)
- EXIT south, east

## 12. Space Ship

- USE `Keycard` on ship (5/10)
- EXIT east

## 13. Flat Desert

- USE `Log` on cracks
- USE hole

## 14. Tube Room

- USE `Stone with Microchip` on opening of the tube
