## 1. Walkthrough

- PICK UP space seashell (`Space Seashell`)
- USE space cauldron (`Ladle`)
- TALK TO spaceball star
  - positively
  - negatively
  - apathetically (`Spaceball Cap`)
- USE `Spaceball Cap` on washer/dryer
  - "Hot wash cycle"
  - "Hot dry cycle" (`Shrunken Spaceball Cap`)
- PICK UP note (from the fridge) (`Note`, `Magnet`)
- LOOK AT `Note`
  1. Have breakfast
  2. Call your space father
  3. Water space garden
  4. Take Helix for a walk
- USE space locker
  - Breakfast (Cutlery)
  - Call (Telephone earphone)
  - Water (Flower)
  - Walk (Cat)
- USE space locker (`Wee-Ceptacle`)
- USE `Wee-Ceptacle` on spaceman (yourself)
- USE `Space Seashell` on spaceman
- USE `Wee-Ceptacle` on spaceman
- USE `Magnet` on `Ladle`
- USE `Ladle` on coin (`Coin`)
- USE `Coin` on vending machine (`Prophgalactic`)
- USE `Wee-Ceptacle` on `Prophgalactic` (`Wee Balloon`)
- USE `Shrunken Spaceball Cap` on `Wee Balloon` (`Fake Ducky`)
- USE `Fake Ducky` on freezer
- USE `Fake Ducky` on space witch
