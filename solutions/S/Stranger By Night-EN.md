# Stranger By Night - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 28.03.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/stranger-by-night)

## 1. Hallway

- PICK UP note (from the wall) (`Note`)
- LOOK AT `Note`
- USE northern door
- LOOK AT letters on the wall
- USE northern door

## 2. Living Room

- TALK TO Amber until she stops responding
- EXIT right

## 3. Stairs

- USE stairs

## 4. Top Floor Hallway

- USE right door

### 4.1. Library

- LOOK AT book (a white book on the second shelf from the bottom in the third cabinet from the right: "The selected works of W. Shakespeare")
- PICK UP book (`The selected works of W. Shakespeare`)
- LOOK AT `The selected works of W. Shakespeare` (`Paper`)
- LOOK AT `Paper`
  - **NOTE:** `Damn V`
- EXIT left

## 5. Top Floor Hallway

- EXIT south

## 6. Stairs

- EXIT left

## 7. Living Room

- LOOK AT blood
- LOOK AT letters on the wall
- EXIT south

## 8. Hallway

- EXIT right

## 9. Dining Room

- USE statues (on the cabinet on the left)
  - You need to push the buttons in the correct order
  - EXIT
- EXIT south

## 10. Top Floor Hallway

### 10.1. Library

- LOOK AT book (a book with red and blue spots on the third shelf from the bottom in the second cabinet from the right: Book about mythology)
- PICK UP book (`Mythology Book`)
- LOOK AT `Mythology Book`
  - READ all articles and note their Roman names

## 11. Dining Room

- USE statues
  - Remember `Damn V` from the `Paper`
  - D = Diana = `Artemis`
  - a = `Apollo`
  - m = Mercury = `Hermes`
  - n = Neptune = `Poseidon`
  - v = Venus = `Aphrodite`
  - USE the buttons `3, 4, 2, 5, 1`
- USE cabinet
- USE drawer (`Key`)

## 12. Top Floor Hallway

- USE `Key` on lower left door
- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE lower left door

### 12.1. Bedroom

- **QUICK:**
  - PICK UP baby oil (from the cabinet on the left) (`Baby Oil`)
  - USE `Baby Oil` on door latch
  - USE door latch
- PICK UP diary (`Diary`)
- LOOK AT `Diary`
- USE door

## 13. Top Floor Hallway

- PICK UP glass shard (`Glass Shard`)
- USE upper left door

### 13.1. Study

- `Glass Shard` on tapestry
- USE safe
  - There were some numbers in the `Diary`
  - Amber's mothers birthday on the "next day"
  - ENTER `061644` (`Locket`, `Paper (2)`)
- LOOK AT `Paper (2)`
- USE `Paper (2)` on `Paper` (`Map`)
- LOOK AT `Map`
  - EXIT
- USE `Pen` on `Map`
- EXIT east

## 14. Dining Room

- LOOK AT moose head
- USE moose head
- LOOK AT moose head
- USE `Glass Shard` on `Locket` (`Amethyst`)
- USE `Amethyst` on moose head
