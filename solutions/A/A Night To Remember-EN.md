# A Night To Remember - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 19.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/a-night-to-remember-2009)

## 1. Hall

- TALK TO officer (Bartholomew)
- TALK TO Jacques (behind Bartholomew)
- TALK TO couple (Bridget and Jacob)
- WALK upstairs
- USE top right door

## 2. Corridor

- USE right door

### 2.1. Victors Bedroom

- USE right door
- USE left door **2 times**

## 3. Hall

- USE middle back door

## 4. Corridor

### 4.1. Victors Bedroom

#### 4.1.1. White Room

- USE dead body (`Pocket Knife`, `Journal`)
- LOOK AT `Journal`
- USE chair
- USE `Pocket Knife` on chair (`Chairleg`)
- USE dead body
- USE door
- USE `Chairleg` on Thomas
- USE door

### 4.2. Victors Bedroom

- USE left door

## 5. Hall

- USE top left door

### 5.1. Simple Room

- USE note (on the table) (`Diagrams`)
- LOOK AT `Diagrams`
- USE right door

## 6. Hall

- USE bottom right door

### 6.1. Lounge

- PICK UP book (from the floor) (`Victor's Journal`)
- LOOK AT `Victor's Journal`
- USE left door

## 7. Hall

- USE door under the left stairs

### 7.1. Storage Room

- LOOK AT boxes (`Cane`)
- LOOK AT indentation in the floor
- USE `Cane` on indentation

### 7.2. Secret Passage

- WALK downstairs and to the left

#### 7.2.1. Pentagram Room

- USE Jacob
- USE Bridget
- USE Barty
- TALK TO Barty
- TALK TO Bridget

## 8. Hall

- USE bottom left door
- USE bottom right door
- USE door under the right stairs

## 9. Cell

- PICK UP key (`Key`)
- LOOK AT `Key`
- USE `Key` on brick in the back wall (third row from the bottom, the brick looks slightly different from the others) (`Brick`)
- USE `Brick` on chains (of the bed) (`Chains`)
- USE `Key` on cell door
- USE right door

## 10. Passage

- USE `Cane` on rocks (the biggest pile on the ground near the edge)
- USE `Chains` on rocks
- USE chains

## 11. Stalactite Cave

- USE `Chairleg` on center stalactite (the one with the crack)
- USE stairs

## 12. Stairways Cave

- USE lever
- USE (right) stairs

## 13. Hall

### 13.1. Storage Room

- USE secret passage

### 13.2. Secret Passage

- EXIT left

### 13.3. Hieroglyphs Chamber

- LOOK AT hieroglyphs
- USE dead body (`Mirror`)
- USE `Mirror` on floor (above the dead body)
- WALK to the right above the stairs (you will hear a click)
- WALK back to the left (another click)
- WALK to the right below the stairs (click)
- WALK back to the left (another click)
- USE dead body (`Assistant`)
- USE `Assistant` on ground below the stairs
- WALK to the right above the stairs

## 14. Kitchen

- PICK UP knife (`Knife`)
- PICK UP wood (the black spot on the ground below the fridge) (`Wood`)
- USE `Knife` on `Wood`
- USE right door
