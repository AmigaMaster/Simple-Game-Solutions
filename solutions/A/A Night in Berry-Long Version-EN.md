## 1. Village Street

### 1.1. Inn

#### 1.1.1. Living Room

- EXIT east

#### 1.1.2. Kitchen

- PICK UP purple flowers (`Purple Flower`)
- PICK UP gleaming leaves (`Gleaming Leaves`)
- PICK UP pink flowers (`Pink Flower`)
- EXIT west

#### 1.1.3. Living Room

- EXIT south

#### 1.1.4. Dining Room

- EXIT upstairs

#### 1.1.5. Attic

- PICK UP book on botany (`Sage`, `Bay Leaves`, `Valerian`)
- EXIT downstairs, west

#### 1.1.6. Living Room

- PICK UP tankard (`Tankard`)

#### 1.1.7. Kitchen

- USE `Tankard` on basin (`Tankard of Water`)
- USE `Pink Flower` on kitchen knife (`Minced Roots`)

#### 1.1.8. Living Room

- USE `Tankard of Water`with fire
- PICK UP tankard (`Tankard of hot Water`)
- USE `Minced Roots` on `Tankard of hot Water` (`Valerian Tea`)
- USE `Valerian Tea` on Ambroix

#### 1.1.9. Attic

- USE bed (`Rag`)

#### 1.1.10. Living Room

- PICK UP inkwell (`Inkwell and Feather`)
- USE `Inkwell and Feather` on `Rag`
- EXIT north

## 2. Village Street

## 3. Back Street

## 4. Back Street

## 6. Back Street

### 6.1. Grandmother's House

- ENTER inn

### 7.1. Inn

#### 7.1.1. Attic

- USE handkerchief on writing (`Rag`)

#### 7.1.2. Living Room

- USE `Rag` on fire
- PICK UP shovel (`Shovel`)
- USE `Shovel` on fire
- PICK UP rag (`Ashes`)

## 8. Garden

- USE `Ashes` on grandmother
- TALK TO grandmother **3 times**
- USE pot (under the bench) (`Slice of Meat`)
- USE `Slice of Meat` on grandmother
- TALK TO grandmother **2 times**

## 9. Village Street

### 9.1. Inn

#### 9.1.1. Kitchen

- USE terracotta pot (`Peppercorns`)

## 10. Village Street

- EXIT north

## 11. Forest

- WAIT until the right wolf walks down
- WALK north until the upper wolf appears
- WAIT until he sleep
- WALK east to the next wolf
- WAIT until he sleeps
- EXIT northeast

## 14. Southern Path

### 16.1. Left Bay

## 17. Village Street

### 17.1. Inn

#### 17.1.1. Dining Room

- USE altar piece
  - CLICK on `III`, `VII`, `IX`
  - PICK UP keys (`Keys`)

## 18. Village Street

- USE door to the hostel
  - USE key hole
  - USE door **2 times**
  - When the eye is visible:
    - USE `Keys` on keyhole
- USE door of the hostel
- PICK UP keys (`Keys`)
- USE `Keys` on door to the barn

### 18.1. Barn

- PICK UP pruning shears (`Pruning Shears`)
- PICK UP wick (`Wick`)
- PICK UP handle (in the shelf) (`Handle`)
- USE coach
- EXIT east

## 19. Village Street

### 19.1. Inn

#### 19.1.1. Living Room

- PICK UP candles (`Candle`)
- USE `Wick` on fire (`Lighted Wick`)

### 19.2. Barn

- USE `Lighted Wick` on `Candle` (`Lit Candle`)
- WALK left
  - USE floor (in the middle, it sounds hollow)
  - LOOK AT floor
  - PICK UP musket (`Musket`)
  - PICK UP stick (`Stick`)
  - Left from the floor, look for a nail which looks loose
  - PICK UP loose nail (`Nail`)
  - USE blanket (at the top above the the hollow floor) (`Powder`)
  - EXIT

## 20. Village Street

- USE `Powder` on `Musket`
- USE `Nail` on `Musket`
- USE `Stick` on `Musket`
- USE `Musket` on brawl (`Broken Musket`)

### 20.1. Inn

#### 20.1.1. Kitchen

- PICK UP flask (`Smelling Salts`)

## 21. Village Street

- USE `Smelling Salts` on injured guard
- TALK TO injured guard

## 22. Back Street

- USE `Handle` on well
- USE well
- PICK UP basket (`Basket`)
- LOOK AT `Basket`(`Giblets`)

## 23. Garden

- USE closed door (to open it)
- USE `Basket` on latrine
- USE post (of the arbor) to climb up the house

## 24. Rooftop

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE `Giblets` on wolf
- USE `Giblets` on garden (on the ground)
- USE `Giblets` on middle (the wolf follows the trail, the scene switches to the garden view)

## 25. Garden

- USE `Giblets` on middle
- USE `Giblets` on bottom right (in front of the latrine)
- USE `Giblets` on top right (the wolf follows the trail)
- USE post
- USE open door
