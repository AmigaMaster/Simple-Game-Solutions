## 1. Palace

### 1.1. Hall

- EXIT upstairs and to the east

### 1.2. Corridor

- PICK UP goozon fruit (`Goozon Fruit`)
- USE `Key` on door (the door behind the second tree)
- ENTER door

#### 1.2.1. Your Room

- PICK UP pen (`Pen`)
- OPEN closet
- PICKUP bag (`Margin Units`)
- LOOK AT `Margin Units`
- EXIT west

### 1.3. Upper Corridor

- EXIT west

### 1.4. Hall

- EXIT east

### 1.5. Courtyard

- EXIT north

### 1.6. Dining Room

- PICK UP glass (`Glass`)
- USE door (to the north)
- ENTER door (to the north)

#### 1.6.1. Kitchen

- TALK TO cook
  - "knife"
  - "bye"
- EXIT south, west, south

### 1.7. Hall

- EXIT south

## 5. Council of Science

### 5.1. Archive

- TALK TO scientists (the one on the left)

- EXIT into the lower west level

- TALK TO (left) student (`Construction plans (tank)`)

- TALK TO (right) student
- USE `Construction plans (tank)` on (left) student
- EXIT east

- TALK TO (left) scientists (`Form (application)`)

- USE `Form (application)` on Brafok (He won't sign)

- USE `Object` on Trof (the souvenir trader)
- TALK TO Krak (at the fish stand)

  - "herb" (`Kra herb`)

### 35.1. Fortune Teller

- USE `Margin Units` on table
  - "me"
  - "king"
  - "brother"
  - "margin"

  - USE `Glass Jar (Substance)` on goblet
  - ask anything
- USE Ugal (`Margin Units`)
- EXIT tent

## 36. Market

- TALK TO Krak
  - "rod" (`Fishing Rod`)

## 37. Temple of Fertility

- USE `Mop` on water (`Mop (Wet)`)

### 37.1. Temple West Wall

- USE hole

### 37.2. Basement

- USE `Mop (Wet)` on dirt (on the right wall)
- EXIT upstairs **2 times**

### 37.3. Balcony

- USE `Knife` on `Grot's Suit` (`Piece of Leather`)
- USE `Piece of Leather` with `Mug (Margin)` (`Sticky Leather`)
- USE `Sticky Leather` on `Fishing Rod` (`Sticky Fishing Rod`)
- USE `Sticky Fishing Rod` on Eye of Geltz (`Eye of Geltz`)

### 37.4. Basement

- USE `Eye of Geltz` on relief
- USE `Lantern` on walkway

### 37.5. Maze of Fertility

- **RIDDLE:** Maze
  - You have three different colored statues with a different shape of the base
  - LOOK AT the pedestals to get a hint which statue should fit
  - Each pedestal has effect on the lower ones
  - Floor 1
    - USE `Blue Statue` on top pedestal
    - USE `Blue Statue` on bottom left pedestal
    - USE `Grey Statue` on top right pedestal (**NOTE:** It does not change anything on this level but on the lower level)
    - USE `Blue Statue` on bottom left pedestal
    - EXIT downstairs
  - Floor 2
    - USE `Grey Statue` on top pedestal
    - USE `Blue Statue` on right pedestal
    - USE `Grey Statue` on top pedestal
    - EXIT upstairs
  - Floor 1
    - USE `Luminescent Statue` on middle pedestal
    - EXIT downstairs
  - Floor 2
    - EXIT downstairs
  - Floor 3
    - USE `Grey Statue` on left pedestal
    - EXIT upstairs
  - Floor 2
    - USE `Grey Statue` on bottom pedestal
    - USE `Blue Statue` on bottom left pedestal
    - USE `Grey Statue` on bottom pedestal
    - EXIT upstairs
  - Floor 1
    - USE `Blue Statue` on bottom pedestal
    - USE `Luminescent Statue` on bottom right pedestal
    - EXIT downstairs **2 times**
  - Floor 3
    - EXIT downstairs

### 37.6. Chamber

- PICK UP object
