# Adventure: The Inside Job - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 29.11.2021 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get the game for free at the [Adventure Game Studio Website](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/site/games/game/1439-adventure-all-in-the-game)

## 1. Kyrandia

- USE `CommKey` on the portal door to the left

## 2. Discworld II (PTG-DW2-073)

- USE `CommKey` on the portal door to the left

## 3. Rex Nebular (MP-RN-056)

- PICK UP VGA jumpsuit clip-on on the floor (`VGA jumpsuit clip-on`)
- LOOK AT bottom right corner (door)
- USE door to Dr. Slache's office (`sedative`)
- USE `CommKey` on office door

## 4. King's Quest 1 (SOL-KQ1EGA-016)

- TALK TO dwarf next to the tree **4 times**
- USE `VGA jumpsuit clip-on` on dwarf
- PICK UP yellow rope (`EGA rope`)
- PICK UP carrots from the field (`low-res carrot`)
- USE `CommKey` on portal door

## 5. Discworld II (PTG-DW2-073)

- USE `sedative` with `low-res carrot` (`drugged low-res carrot`)
- USE `drugged low-res carrot` with ground

## 6. Kings Quest VII

### 6.1. Cave Entrance

- USE `CommKey` with cave entrance

### 6.2. Cave

- TALK TO Ichabod **3 times**
- USE `CommKey` on door (`Doll`, `ResConverter`)

## 7. Indiana Jones and the Fate of Atlantis

- USE `CommKey` on the right door

## 8. Leisure Suit Larry 7 (SOL-LSL7-39000)

- USE `flashcards` on walrus
- USE `CommKey` on portal door

## 9. Spindrift (VG-S-009)

- USE `flashcards` on sticky patch in the bottom part of the screen (`two flashcards`)

## 10. Leisure Suit Larry 7 (SOL-LSL7-39000)

- USE `two flashcards` with walrus
- PICK UP bowling ball (`SVGA bowling ball`)
- USE `CommKey` on portal door

## 11. Day of the Tentacle (LA-DOTT-6252070)

- USE `ResConverter` on `SVGA bowling ball` (`VGA bowling ball`)
- USE `VGA bowling ball` on the small cabinet where the globe is placed
- USE `CommKey` on door

## 12. Space Quest IV

- TALK TO Ichabod

## 13. Kings Quest VII

- WALK TO the right
- TALK TO Leenah
- TALK TO Seventy-Three **3 times**
- TALK TO Ms. Fortune **2 times**
- USE `CommKey` on Arcade door (`low-res black boots`)

## 14. Leisure Suit Larry 3

- USE `CommKey` on door

## 15. Storage Room

- PICK UP pliers (`pliers`)
- USE `pliers` on hinges from the left door
- USE back door (`doorknob`)
- USE `doorknob` on shelf
- PICK UP can (`Friction-B-Gone spray-on lubricant`)
- USE `Friction-B-Gone spray-on lubricant` on hinges
- USE `pliers` on hinges (`oxygen mask`)

## 16. Space Quest

- USE chamber (on the right)
- USE `low-res black boots` on chamber

## 17. Inherit the Earth

- USE the doors of both buildings to the top left and bottom left
- USE `CommKey` on door from the right building

## 18. Conquest of the Longbow

- EXIT east **2 times**

## 19. Your Room

- USE top door
- TALK TO Suzy-Ann **2 times**
- USE `CommKey` on Suzy-Ann
- USE `CommKey` on yourself
- USE `CommKey` on left door (`magic marker`)

## 20. Sidonius

### 20.1. Hut

- PICK UP hammer (`small hammer`)
- USE `small hammer` on lock (`metal canister`)
- PICK UP chest **2 times** (`pill bottle`, `letter`)
- LOOK AT `letter`  
  **NOTE:** Signed by Vivi
- USE `metal canister` on yourself
- USE `pill bottle` on Joey
- USE `letter` on Joey
- LOOK AT pole  
  **HINTS:**
  - The letter is from Vivi
  - The letter is from VIVI
  - Note that the letters are capitalized
  - Think in numbers
  - Think in roman numbers
  - VI = 6, so enter 6 6 as the combination
- WALK downstairs

### 20.2. Basement

- LOOK AT bed
- USE `pill bottle` with toenail clippings (`bottle with toenails`)
- USE `bottle with toenails` with `Doll` (`Voodoo doll`)
- USE right door

### 20.3. Laboratory

- USE `magic marker` on floor in front of you
- TALK TO Siderius **3 times**
- USE `Voodoo doll` on Siderius
- USE `CommKey` on trap door
- USE machine (the red button)
- USE `small hammer` on jamming machine
