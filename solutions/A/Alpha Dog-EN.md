# Alpha Dog - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 14.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [Adventure Game Studio](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/1240-alpha-dog)

## 1. Front

> Dog

- EXIT east

## 2. Backyard

- USE broom
- JUMP container
- USE piece of wood
- JUMP container
- EXIT west

## 3. Front

- EXIT to the side of building (on the grass right from the tree)

## 4. Side of Building

- CLIMB tree
- SWITCH TO Stan

> Stan

- WALK under the tree
- SWITCH TO Frank

> Frank

## 5. Backyard

- USE container
- USE look inside the container
- USE dog leash (`Dog Leash`)
- USE container
- EXIT west

## 6. Side of Building

- CLIMB tree
- USE `Dog Leash` on Stan (to pull him onto the tree)
- SWITCH TO Stan

> Stan

- RETRACT (into your carapace)
- SWITCH TO dog

> Dog

- THROW Stan
- USE hole (in window)

## 7. House

### 7.1. Kitchen

- SWITCH TO Frank

> Frank

## 8. Side of Building

- CLIMB tree (down)

## 9. Backyard

- USE container
- USE look inside container (`Cracker`)
- ENTER ventilation slot

## 10. Ventilation Shaft

### 10.1. Entrance

- EXIT east

### 10.2. Spiderweb

- USE `Cracker` on spider
- USE needle (`Needle`)
- EXIT east
- Walk further to the right to find your friends below again. Put the needle in the cable that’s attached to the lamp.

## 11. House

### 11.1. Kitchen

- USE `Needle` on cable (on the lamp)
  - **NOTE:** The light must be switched off
- SWITCH TO dog

> Dog

- USE switch (on the left) to turn the light on (a fuse will burn)
- USE little door (in the door on the right) **2 times**

### 11.2. Storage Room

- USE (right) door

### 11.3. Torture chamber

- USE (right) door

### 11.4. Cages

- USE second to last cage in the bottom row
- SWITCH TO Stan

> Stan

- WALK under the panel
- RETRACT
- SWITCH TO dog

> Dog

- JUMP Stan
- SWITCH TO Frank

> Frank

### 11.5. Kitchen

- EXIT west, go outside, USE container and walk to the side of the building

## 12. Side of Building

- CLIMB tree
- USE hole

## 13. House

### 13.1. Kitchen

- USE leash
- USE little door

### 13.2. Cages

- CLIMB dog
- USE panel
