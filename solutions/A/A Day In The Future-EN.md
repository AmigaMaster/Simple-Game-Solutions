## 1. Apartment

- USE cupboard (to open it)
- LOOK AT cupboard **3 times** (1/1)
- PICK UP orange sweater (`Sweater`) (1/2)
- USE `Sweater`
- USE toolbox (`Screwdriver`) (1/3)
- USE toolbox (`Pliers`) (1/4)
- USE pants (shelf above the sweater) (`Pants`) (1/5)
- USE `Pants` (`ID card`) (2/7)
- USE computer screen **2 times** (`CD`) (1/8)
  - OPEN emails (on the right)
    - READ all emails
    - BACK
  - OPEN files (on the left)
    - OPEN all files
      - **NOTE:** `User Name: ouv2317156 Password: 56172`
    - BACK
  - MENU
  - QUIT
- USE button next to the door on the right of the cupboard
- ENTER door

### 1.1. Bathroom

- PICK UP dental floss (from the sink) (`Dental Floss`) (1/16)
- EXIT bathroom

## 2. Apartment

- USE `Screwdriver` on ventilation shaft (in the top left corner) (`3 Screws`) (4/12)
  - LOOK AT ventilation shaft
  - USE `Pliers` on `Screwdriver` (`Useless Screwdriver`, `Magnet`) (4/15)
  - USE `Dental Floss` on `Magnet` (`Magnet on a String`) (1/16)
  - USE `Magnet on a String` on ventilation shaft **4 times** (`Coin`) (4+10/30)
  - USE bed
- USE control panel (on the right door)
  - USE `ID Card` on card reader
  - "1.Open / Close door"
  - "Here are your 5cts..."
  - "1.Open / Close door"
  - Quit
- EXIT east

## 3. Garage

- USE car
  - USE screen
