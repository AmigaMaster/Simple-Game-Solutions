## 24. The Adventure

Now there are two possible ways to continue

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 25. The Long Path

- USE console
  - "Thanks for..."
  - "...that scale of yours that you let me have."

### 25.1. Labyrinth

- Walk west until you reach a way to the North
- North
- East to the triangular pillar
  - **NOTE:** The markings on the pillar: There are  four rows,each with a mirrored number: `9`, `8`, `2`, `7`
- South
- East
- North to the pillar and push as many buttons as the numbers from the first pillar
- North

#### 25.1.1. Garden

- TALK TO Gus
  - Exit the detail scene and re-enter - **repeat this until Gus helps you**

### 25.2. Suzy-Ann's Room

- USE desk under the window **2 times**

#### 25.2.1. Video Game

- Walk east until you find a large rock
- FIGHT rock
- PICK UP rocks
- North
- KNOCK at the door to the house

##### 25.2.1.1. House

- PICK UP seeds (`bag of seeds`)
- PICK UP tea cup (`tea cup`)
- PICK UP candle (`candle`)
- USE `bag of seeds` with bird (which actually is a jay) (`jay`)
- USE `tea cup` with woman (`full tea cup`)
- PRESS 1
- USE woman
  - She asks who we are
  - SELECT `full tea cup` (which is "T")
  - SELECT `jay` (which gives us "T. J." - our name)
  - Now she asks who to call
  - SELECT `rock`
  - SELECT `fist` ("Rockfist", which is Sledge)
- End of optional part, continue with "The Mega Game" below

## 27. Lasol VII

### 27.1. Teleporter

- If there are green creatures, try to PICK UP one - it will make a noise. If there is another creature,
  - USE `casette player` with the second creature
- EXIT north

### 27.2. Mountain Pass

- USE `casette player` with the second creature
- USE `casette player` with droid
- EXIT north

### 27.3. Underground

#### 27.3.1. Elevator

- PICK UP rock (`rock`)
- PICK UP rebar (`long piece of rebar`)
- PICK UP rubber belt from the generator (`small rubber belt`)
- EXIT northwest

#### 27.3.2. Hallway

- Try to exit southwest
- USE `small rubber belt` with `rock` (`sling`)
- USE `sling` with sunlamp
- USE `long piece of rebar` with plant **5 times**
- EXIT southwest

#### 27.3.3. Office

- PICK UP fruit (`weird-looking fruit`)
- USE computer
  - LOOK AT (cycle through the verbs with the mouse wheel or right mouse button) sticky note
    **NOTE:** `R. R. down 3, right 5`
  - EXIT the view
- USE book shelve - the right shelf, in the third row th the very right side
  - You can turn pages by using the right arrow, read all pages
    **NOTE:** Sticky Notes
    - 1 note at  (G)rues
    - 2 notes at (O)rbs
    - 1 note at  (R)ipburger
    - 1 note at  (V)ivern
    - 1 note at  (Y)erka
    - Password `GROOVY`
- USE Computer
  - Enter `groovy` with your keyboard
  - Read all entries
  - Exit the computer
- TALK TO LeMort
- **QUICK:** Swap minds with fish

### 27.4. Clearing

- If you did not pick up the fruit in the office, you can PICK UP the fruit here

### 27.5. Portal

- USE `weird-looking fruit` with critter

## 28. Arcade Sequence

- It is totally random and you must have a little bit of luck here but you can SKIP the sequence when you fail
