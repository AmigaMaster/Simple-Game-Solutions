# Dada: Stagnation In Blue - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 06.03.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/dada-stagnation-in-blue)

## 1. Lighthouse

### 1.1. Inside Lighthouse

- TALK TO man **2 times**
- USE door

## 2. Lighthouse

- EXIT west

## 3. Bridge

- LOOK AT bridge (`Diamonds`)
- USE bridge

## 4. Lake

- PICK UP piece of lake (the ripped part in the lake) (`Piece of Lake`)
- EXIT south

## 5. Bridge

- EXIT west

## 6. Woman

- TALK TO woman
- USE `Piece of Lake` on woman (`Tears`)
- EXIT east

## 7. Lake

- TALK TO lake
- USE `Diamonds` on boat

## 8. Room

- TALK TO man
- USE `Tears` on man (`Knife`)
- EXIT south **2 times**, east

## 9. Lighthouse

- USE door

### 9.1. Inside Lighthouse

- USE `Knife` on man

## 10. Foetus

- TALK TO foetus
