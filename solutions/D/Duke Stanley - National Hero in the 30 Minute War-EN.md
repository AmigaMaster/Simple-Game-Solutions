## 1. Walkthrough

- USE `Lock Picks` on `Handcuffs`
- LOOK AT circuit box (walk to the right of the red machine)
- USE circuit box
  - USE `Handcuffs` on transformer (left of the switches)
- PICK UP red wire (on the left of the red machine beside the elevator) (`Wire`)
- USE control panel (to the left on the pipes on the left)
  - "3. Close Silo Doors"
- USE wheel (on the pipe to the right)
- USE `Wire` on broken wires (left of the panel)
- USE panel (riht to the wires)
- USE panel (of the red machine)
