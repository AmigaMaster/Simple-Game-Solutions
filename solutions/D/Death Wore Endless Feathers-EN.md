# Death Wore Endless Feathers - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 27.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

## 1. Sector H

- USE Diamond Keeper's Door
- ENTER apartment door

### 1.1. Apartment

#### 1.1.1. Entrance Hallway

- PICK UP wooden board (`Wooden Board`)
- ENTER door

##### 1.1.1.1. Khato's Apartment

- TALK TO Khato
- PICK UP multitool (on the toolbox, you cannot pick it up at the moment)
- EXIT east

#### 1.1.2. Entrance Hallway

- EXIT west

#### 1.1.3. Left Entrance Hallway

- ENTER kitchen

##### 1.1.3.1. Kitchen

- USE `Silver Dollar` on vending machine
- PICK UP cola (`Cola Can`)
- EXIT east

#### 1.1.4. Entrance Hallway

- EXIT upstairs

#### 1.1.5. Second Floor Hallway

- TALK TO Buster
- ENTER roof (upstairs)

#### 1.1.6. Roof

- EXIT west

### 1.2. Diamond Keeper's

#### 1.2.1. Diamond Keeper's Roof

- PICK UP stone (from the crate) (`Stone`)
- USE `Stone` on floodlight
- USE aerial (on the left)
- USE shadow (on the right)
- ENTER inside

#### 1.2.2. Apartment

- PICK UP towel (`Towel`)
- USE lock (on the cupboard)
- USE `Towel` on monkey
- USE `Wooden Board` on nails
- WALK west
- PICK UP green spraycan (`Spraycan`)
- WALK east
- PICK UP wooden board (`Wooden Board`)
- PICK UP nails (`Nails`)
- EXIT apartment

#### 1.2.3. Diamond Keeper's Roof

- EXIT south, east

### 1.3. Apartment

#### 1.3.1. Second Floor Hallway

- USE `Spraycan` on Buster
- USE `Cola Can` on Buster (`Cola: All Shook Up`)

#### 1.3.2. Entrance Hallway

##### 1.3.2.1. Khato's Apartment

- USE `Cola: All Shook Up` on Khato
- PICK UP multitool (`Multitool`)

#### 1.3.3. Entrance Hallway

- EXIT west

#### 1.3.4. Hallway

- ENTER (right) door

##### 1.3.4.1. Your Room

- PICK UP booklet (`Member's Booklet`)
- LOOK AT `Member's Booklet`
  - **NOTE:** The security code `5634`
- USE computer

### 2.1. Home

  - "Change the Top Ractive"

### 2.3. Street

- ENTER right archway

### 2.4. Home

- ENTER house

## 3. Sector H

- EXIT west

## 4. Street to Subway

- ENTER subway

## 5. Subway

- TALK TO Ollie

## 6. Ming's Takeout Street

- ENTER Slice + Byte

### 6.1. Slice + Byte

- USE `Wooden Board` on mousehole
- USE `Nails` on wooden board
- TALK TO Pizzaserv 9000
- PICK UP green circuit board (`Subserviant AI`)
- ENTER kitchen

#### 6.1.1. Kitchen

- TALK TO Baketron Ultra
- WAIT until he opens his mouth, then **QUICK:**
  - PICK UP red circuit board (`Surly AI`)
- USE `Subserviant AI` on Baketron Ultra
