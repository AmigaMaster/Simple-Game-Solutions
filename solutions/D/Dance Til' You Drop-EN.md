## Mildred and Syd

- TALK TO Mildred

- USE phone **2 times**
  - "Tyler I'm in trouble!!"
  - "Tyler, sweety, do you happen to know the combination to my safe?"
- USE (YMCA) poster
- LOOK AT poster (next to the water tank)
  - **NOTE:** His birthday is on the second Thursday in July and he will be 36.
- USE safe
  - **RIDDLE:** Combination
  - Info from the poster beside the water tank: You will be 36, your birthday is on the second Thursday in July
  - July = `7`
  - Get a calendar from 1984 and check the second Thursday was the `12`th
  - Info from the Intro: Is is 1984, minus 36 years = `48`
  - ENTER `7 12 48` (`Gun`)
