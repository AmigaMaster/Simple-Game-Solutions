## 1. Lighthouse

### 1.1. Inside Lighthouse

- TALK TO man
- PICK UP light bulb (`Red Light Bulb`)
- USE chimney

## 2. Lighthouse

- EXIT west

# Rudolph

- TALK TO Rudolph
- USE `Red Light Bulb` on Rudolph (`Tears`)
- EXIT east

## 3. Lighthouse

- USE `Tears` on snow-cursors (on the left side)
- PICK UP knife (`Knife`)
- USE fireplace

### 3.1. Inside Lighthouse

- USE `Knife` on stocking
