# Danny Dread Is On Call - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 15.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [MediaFire](https://www.mediafire.com/file/zmmmzgyyzzj/OnCall.zip)

## 1. Crossroads

- TALK TO clown (`Balloon`)
- USE `Balloon` on `Key` (`Balloon with Key`)
- USE `Balloon with Key` on grassy area (next to the bush) (`Piece of String`)
- TAKE patch of dirt (under the bush) (`Mud`)
- EXIT east

## 2. Farm

### 2.1. Outside

- TALK TO little Tyke
- LOOK AT window
- USE `Mud` on little Tyke
- **QUICK:**
  - USE window (`Bottle of Bourbon`)
- EXIT west, north

## 3. Doc's House

- USE pot (`Empty Cup`)
- USE `Empty Cup` on coffee machine (`Coffee Cup`)
- LOOK AT small gap (between the chamber and the red canisters)
- TAKE ruler (`Ruler`)
- USE `Ruler` on gap (`Ping Pong Bat`)
- EXIT west

## 4. Crossroads

- USE `Bottle of Bourbon` on `Coffee Cup` (`Spiked Coffee Cup`)
- USE `Spiked Coffee Cup` on clown
- TAKE nose (`Clown's Nose`)
- USE `Clown's Nose` on `Piece of String` (`Nose and String Combo`)
- `Nose and String Combo` on `Ping Pong Bat` (`Completed Toy`)

## 5. Farm

- USE `Completed Toy` on little Tyke
- TAKE sheep (`Sheep`)

## 6. Doc's House

- USE `Sheep` on door (to the machine)

## 7. Crossroads

- EXIT south

## 8. Doc's House

- TALK TO trapped Bella
- TALK TO Doctor
  - "Have you thought about reconfiguring the machine?"
- KICK door (to he machine)
