## 1. Murder Scene

- TALK TO Marilyn Morrison
- TALK TO Dame McBombshell
- EXIT east

## 2. Yahtzee Store

- TALK TO Generic Employee
- EXIT east

## 3. Shelter

- LOOK AT catalogue shelter (`Fresh Pumpkin`)
- EXIT east

## 4. Cabin

- LOOK AT door
  - **NOTE:** The colors above and under the numbers. There were 4 red ribbons around the tree, 5 pink mushrooms at the stall, 2 yellow crosses at the shelter, number 8 next to the door
  - `4528`
- USE door

### 4.1. Inside Cabin

- USE skeleton (`Yahtzemploy ID`)
- LOOK AT note
- USE `Fresh Pumpkin` on plinth
- USE door

## 5. Cabin

- EXIT west **2 times**

## 6. Yahtzee Store

- USE `Yahtzemploy ID` on Generic Employee Show (`Pen`)

## 7. Cabin

### 7.1. Inside Cabin

- USE `Pen` on pumpkin
- USE Fox's head (`Googly Eyes`)
- USE `Googly Eyes` on pumpkin

## 8. Yahtzee Store

- USE catalogue pile (`Yahtzeebrand Catalogue`)

## 9. Murder Sce

- USE `Yahtzeebrand Catalogue` on warlock
