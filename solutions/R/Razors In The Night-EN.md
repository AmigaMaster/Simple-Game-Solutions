# Razors In The Night - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------
|   1.0   | 13.04.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/razors-in-the-night)

## 1. Bloody Street

- EXIT east

## 2. Street Corner

- PICK UP fishing pole (`Fishing Pole`) (3/3)
- EXIT south

## 3. Rock Shop and Restaurant Street

- USE door to rock shop
- ENTER rock shop

### 3.1. Rock Shop

- PICK UP guitar (on the right) (4/7)
- PICK UP string (`String`) (3/10)
- EXIT shop

## 4. Rock Shop and Restaurant Street

- EXIT north, west **2 times**

## 5. Stop Crossroad

- EXIT north

## 6. Cinema Street

- TALK TO man in the booth
  - Ask for cops
  - Give me a free ticket?
  - Offer food
    - **NOTE:** You need to leave with the man saying: Bring me some food.
- EXIT north

## 7. Bus Stop

- EXIT west

## 8. Butcher and Arcade Street

- EXIT east (in front of the butcher shop)

## 9. Alley

- TALK TO bum
- USE dumpster (5/15)
  - LOOK AT dumpster **4 times**
  - USE dumpster (`Food`) (3/18)
- EXIT west, south, east

## 10. Cinema Street

- USE `Food` on man in the booth (`Movie Ticket`) (3/21)
- ENTER cinema

### 10.1. Cinema Showroom

- EXIT south, and return here
- USE seats until you find glasses (`Goofy Disguise Glasses`) (5/26)
- EXIT south, north, west, northwest (cutscene and you automatically exit south again)

## 11. Street

- EXIT east

## 12. Main Street

- WALK to cemetery
- PICK UP wallet and badge (`Wallet and Badge`) (4/30)
- USE `Wallet and Badge` (`ATM Card`) (3/33)
- EXIT southeast (on the street on the east side)

## 13. Arcade and Butcher

- EXIT south, east, south, south

## 14. Motel

- USE office door
- ENTER office

### 14.1. Office

- USE `Wallet and Badge` on manager
  - "I am working undercover!" (4/37)
- PICK UP brochure (from the rack on the left) (`Brochure`) (3/40)
- EXIT south, and return here
- USE lost + found box **7 times** (`Car Keys`) (3/43)
- EXIT south, north **3 times**

## 15. Bus Stop

- TALK TO lady (on the bench) (`Cookies`) (3/46)
- EXIT east, south, west

## 16. Hooker Corner

- LOOK AT billboard (on the left side) **2 times**
- EXIT northeast

## 17. Street

- USE `Cookies` on Santa (4/50)
- PICK UP kettle (`3 Bucks`) (3/53)
- EXIT east, southeast, south

## 18. Bus Stop

- ENTER 99 Cents Shop (3/56)

### 18.1. Cents Shop

- EXIT south, west, northwest

## 19. Main Street

- EXIT east on the right side near the bridge

## 20. Bar Street

- EXIT south

## 21. Parking Lot Street

- TALK TO parking attendant
  - **NOTE:** If he doesn't want to talk, EXIT west and return here
  - Talk about everything
- LOOK AT clipboard (in his booth)
- USE `Car Keys` (5/61)
- While he's away:
  - LOOK AT clipboard (34/65)
- EXIT west

## 22. Rock Shop and Restaurant Street

- USE `Goofy Disguise Glasses` on yourself (3/68)
- ENTER restaurant (3/71)
  - **NOTE:** You may have to enter twice and watch the same cutscene twice.

### 22.1. Restaurant

- WAIT for the waiter
- PICK UP fish (`Fish`) (3/74)
- USE `Hairspray` on candle on the bottom table (4/78)
- EXIT southwest (3/81)

## 23. Rock Shop and Restaurant Street

- EXIT east, north, north, west

## 24. Main Street

- EXIT southeast

## 25. Butcher and Arcade Street

- ENTER arcade

### 25.1. Arcade

- USE `3 Bucks` on arcade machine next to the pinball (`2.50 Bucks`)
  - LOOK AT high scores (4/85)
- USE `2.50 Bucks` on fortune telling machine `2 Bucks`
  - "What is my fortune?" (4/89)
- USE `2 Bucks` on "The Claw" (`Bear`) (`1.50 Bucks`) (4/93)
- PICK UP bear (3/96)
- EXIT south

## 26. Butcher and Arcade Street

- USE door to the butcher

### 26.1. Butcher

- TALK TO butcher
- PICK UP crowbar (`Crowbar`)
- USE `Crowbar` on `Bear` (`Bear with Crowbar`) (4/100)
- USE `Fish` on meat grinder (`Bent Hook`) (8/108)
- EXIT south (3/111)

### 26.2. Arcade

- TALK TO Skelter
- EXIT and re-enter the arcade
- USE `1.50 Bucks` on arcade machine
  - LOOK AT Skelter's score
  - LOOK AT Crumb's score (4/115)
    - **NOTE:** `8122`
- EXIT south **2 times**

## 27. Bus Stop

- USE `1 Buck` on payphone
  - Call helpline to order some crack (5/120)
- EXIT east

## 28. Cinema Street

- USE ATM machine
  - USE `ATM Card` on green slot
  - ENTER pin (the highscore from the arcade) `8122` (`1 Buck`) (9/129)
- EXIT northwest

## 29. Bus Stop

### 29.1. Cents Shop

- EXIT northeast

#### 29.1.1. Hallway

- USE men's room door

##### 29.1.1.1. Men's Room

- USE closed door
- USE `Crowbar` on grating (in the ceiling) (6/135)

##### 29.1.1.2. Air Ventilation Shaft

- EXIT north **4 times**
- LOOK AT grate
  - USE `String` on `Fishing Pole` (`Fishing Pole with String`) (4/139)
  - USE `Bent Hook` on `Fishing Pole with String` (`Fishing Pole`) (3/142)
  - USE `Fishing Pole` on beer (5/147)
