# Rowen Goes To Work - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 09.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/rowen-goes-to-work)

## 1. Controls

- Left click: move
- Right click: Show inventory screen
- Hold left mouse button: brings up the verb coin

## 2. Bedroom

- EASTER EGG:
  - TALK TO yourself
- OPEN door
- EXIT east

## 3. Kitchen

- OPEN cabinet (under the sink) (`Pins`)
- OPEN fridge (`Anti Super Glue`)
- OPEN cabinet (under the lamp) (`Book of Inner Strength`)
- USE `Book of Inner Strength` in Roger (`Inner Strength`)
- USE (second) door
- ENTER (second) door

## 4. Bathroom

- USE `Pins` on window (`Disgusting Pancake`)
- EXIT west

## 5. Bedroom

- USE `Inner Strength` on drawer
- LOOK AT drawer (`How To Find Keys`, `Pen`, `Paper`)
- USE `How To Find Keys` on Rowen
- USE `Pen` on `Paper` (`Masterpiece`)

## 6. Kitchen

- USE `Anti Super Glue` on sofa
- USE `How To Find Keys` on Rowen

## 7. Bathroom

- USE `Disgusting Pancake` on toilet
- USE `How To Find Keys` on Rowen
- USE `Masterpiece` on picture (`Poker Card`)
- LOOK AT sink (`Soap`)

## 8. Bedroom

- USE `Poker Card` on cat clock (`Big Blue Cup`)

## 9. Kitchen

- USE `Soap` on front door (`Soap Key`)
- USE `Soap Key` on microwave

## 10. Bedroom

- USE (left middle) drawer

## 11. End Screen

- USE `Big Blue Cup` anywhere

## Logo

- USE "VZARE" Text

## Living Room

- You can only close an open the door
