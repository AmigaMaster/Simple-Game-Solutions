Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/unexpected-quest)

## 1. Main Menu

- USE 14.5.23 (`Letter`)

## 2. Your Room

- LOOK AT `Letter`
- **NOTE:** Use the menu and click on the OK button. Otherwise you cannot change you cursor to use the door
- USE door

## 3. Street

- **ATTENTION:** Do not cross the street beside the crosswalk
- WALK via the crosswalk to the upper side
- USE school door

### 3.1. School

- TALK TO teacher
- USE door

## 4. Street

- USE tree (`Love`)
- USE `Love` on `Letter` (`Love Letter`)
- USE school door
