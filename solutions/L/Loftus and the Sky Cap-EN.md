# Loftus and the Sky Cap - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 21.06.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Get free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/loftus-and-the-sky-cap)

## 1. Loftus' Place

### 1.1. Workshop

- USE mug (`Mug`)
- EXIT upstairs

#### 1.1.1. Inessa's Loft

- USE `Mug` on sink (`Mug of Water`)
- EXIT downstairs

### 1.2. Workshop

- USE `Mug of Water` on fire
- USE key (from the fireplace) (`Key`)
- USE `Key` on safe
- USE blueprint (`Blueprints`)
- LOOK AT `Blueprints`
- USE special vial (from above the fireplace) (Stardust 1/1)
- EXIT workshop

## 2. Loftus' Place

- LOOK AT sign
- USE rocks (below the sign) to find more (Stardust 1/2)
- EXIT west

## 3. Path

- EXIT west

## 4. Cross Roads

- TALK TO guard dog
- ENTER forest

## 5. Forest

- USE rock (on the ground left of where you stand after entering the forest) (`Rock`)
- USE sparkle (below the bottom tree on the left) (Stardust 1/3)
- USE cave
- EXIT north

## 6. Cross Roads

- EXIT west

## 7. Village Square

- ENTER bookstore

### 7.1. Bookstore

- TALK TO Alfonso
- USE `Blueprints` on Alfonso
- TALK TO Alfonso
  - "Ask about objectives."
  - "Find a way to read stuff."
  - "What are you too busy doing?"
- EXIT bookstore

## 8. Village Square

- EXIT west

## 9. Alley

- USE broom (`Broom`)
- LOOK AT drain pipe (on the left)
- USE `Rock` on bump (of the pipe)
- USE object (`Object`)
- EXIT east

## 10. Village Square

### 10.1. Bookstore

- USE `Object` on Alfonso (`AWR`)
- USE `AWR` on book (in the left shelf) (`String`)

## 11. Village Square

- TALK TO Chicken Farmer
  - "Ask about objectives."
  - "Find feathers"
- EXIT east, north

## 12. Path

- USE Penelope

## 13. Loftus' Place

- USE Penelope (`Penelope`)

## 14. Village Square

- USE `Penelope` on Chicken Farmer
- TALK TO Chicken Farmer
  - "Ask about objectives."
  - "Find feathers"
- USE `Broom` on basket (in the tower)
- USE basket (`Basket`)
- USE `Basket` on chicken pen (`Basket of Chickens`)

## 15. Forest

- USE `AWR` on sign

### 15.1. Cave

- TALK TO cave monster (Bridget)
- USE `Basket of Chickens` on Bridget
- USE basket of plucked chickens (`Plucked Chickens`)
- USE pile of feathers (`Feathers`)

## 16. Village Square

- TALK TO door knocker
- USW `AWR` on sign (above the door)

### 16.1. Herman's Magic Store

- TALK TO Herman
  - "Ask about objectives."
  - "Find cauldron."
  - "So can you lend me the cauldron?"
- EXIT store

## 17. Forest

### 17.1. Cave

- TALK TO Bridget
  - "Would you mind escorting me..."
  - Keep pushing until she follows you

## 18. Cross Roads

- "No..."
- "Another guard dog..."

## 19. Village Square

- TALK TO Bridget

### 19.1. Herman's Magic Store

- While Herman plays with his Yo-yo:
  - USE fairy dust (the bags in the shelf) (`Satchel of Fairy Dust`)

## 20. Village Square

- USE `Satchel of Fairy Dust` on Bridget (`Cauldron`)

## 21. Forest

### 21.1. Cave

- USE Bridget's underpants (`Bridget's Underpants`)
- USE `Mug` on rock pool (Stardust 1/4)

## 22. Loftus' Place

### 22.1. Workshop

#### 22.1.1. Inessa's Loft

- Open inventory, then select MERGE
  - MERGE `String` on `Bridget's Underpants` (`Tied Underpants`)
