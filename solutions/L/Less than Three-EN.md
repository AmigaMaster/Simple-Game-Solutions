## 1. Walkthrough

- USE rune (on the floor)
- TALK TO Vani
  - "Can you step on one of the glowing runes on the floor?"
- USE (other) rune
- PICK UP dagger (on the ground under the sword, **not** the one under the painting) (`Knife`)
- LOOK AT `Knife` (`Knife (Steel Dagger)`)
- USE `Knife (Steel Dagger)` on sack (in the lower left corner) (`Dagger`)
- LOOK AT `Dagger` (`Dagger (Tongue of Flame)`)
- USE `Dagger (Tongue of Flame)` on ice crystal (on the floor to the right) (`Dagger`)
- LOOK AT `Dagger` (`Dagger (Urguat Hunter's Knife)`)
