# Magsic - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 25.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from the [AGS homepage](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/606-magsic)

## 1. Before you begin

- Each puzzle saves its progress and once you began, the solutions are different
- SAVE before each puzzle / room so you can try for your own and if you are not able to solve the puzzle, load the saved game and try this solution

## 2. Ear

- EXIT north into the hole

### 2.1. Inside Ear

- PICK UP egg
- USE pig
- PICK UP pig
- USE rocket
- WALK into rocket
- USE ramp
- USE door

## 3. Landing Platform

- EXIT up (via the vine on the right side)

## 4. Volcano

- PICK UP note sheet
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE the handles on both wheels in the following order: Left, right, left, right **3 times** to play the correct notes
- USE red tentacle on the left side
- USE red tentacle on the right side
- USE red tentacle on the right side **again** (there are now 4 tentacles)
- USE short rightmost tentacle
- USE left tentacle
- USE long right tentacle
- USE single tentacle
- USE single tentacle **again**
- USE rightmost tentacle
- USE single tentacle
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- **QUICK:**
  - CLICK on all tentacles **several times** to extend them do this until all of them are fully extended
- EXIT east

## 5. Wire

- EXIT north

## 6. Nest

- TALK TO flower
  - **NOTE:** The notes
- EXIT south

## 7. Wire

- WALK onto the wire to get to the other side
- EXIT northeast onto the slide

## 8. Slide

- USE left path **2 times**, right

## 9. Drum

- PICK UP note sheet
- Number the screws from left to right, then use them in the following combinations to get the correct notes
  1. 1, 4, 2, 5
  2. 1, 4
  3. 2
  4. 5
  5. 3

## 10. Wire

- WALK to the left and EXIT south

## 11. Chalk Board

- TALK TO man
- Connect the dots. There is only one way to do it and you can only connect dots in the correct order:
  1. Lowest to the right of the man
  2. Up right
  3. Up left
  4. Left, the second one above his hand
  5. Up
  6. Right
  7. Down right
  8. Down
  9. Right
  10. Up
  11. Up left
  12. Left
  13. Left down
  14. Down
  15. Back to the starting point
- EXIT north into the chalk painting

### 11.1. Cave

- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- Remember the notes from the nest, you need to put them on the sheet by clicking in the upper or lower part of the notes
  1. Down **2 times**
  2. Up **5 times**
  3. Up **2 times**
  4. Up **3 times**
  5. Up **2 times**
- USE piano
- PICK UP note sheet
- EXIT southeast, west, north

## 12. Nest

- TALK TO flower
- EXIT south

## 13. Wire

- EXIT east

## 14. Tree

- PICK UP note sheet
- WALK to and USE the fruits in the following order
  1. lower left
  2. upper left
  3. top
  4. upper right
  5. lower right
- **QUICK:**
  - WALK to the water edge **2 times**
  - WAIT until the big missile hits the monster
- EXIT west

## 15. Wire

- CLICK on the right side of the wire
  - There now is a small red/white square which you need to navigate inside the path of the wire to the note sheet
  - CLICK onto the note sheet to pick it up
- EXIT northeast onto the slide

## 16. Slide

- USE left path **all the time** (to get rid of the white bird)
- Do it again for at least **two times** and choose the paths to the white bird until it flew away completely
- USE right **2 times**, left, right at the very bottom to get the melody

## 17. Drum

- USE screws 1, 4, 2, 5, 4

## 18. Wire

- EXIT southwest

## 19. Chalk Board

- If haven't been here, a stone with legs will flee
- FOLLOW the stone west, west

## 20. Volcano

- EXIT east **2 times**

## 21. Tree

- Now a stone will flee from here, follow it west **2 times**, southwest

## 22. Landing Site

- There now should be three stones, the largest at the bottom, the smallest at the top
  - **NOTE:** If they are in the wrong order, they will leave and you need to collect them again in the following order
  1. Chalk Board
  2. Nest
  3. Tree
- EXIT northwest (you can now reach the entrance thanks to he stones)

## 23. Dripstone Cave

- WALK to the southeast
- WALK to the left
- WALK to southeast **again**
- WALK to the left **again** (scorpions will block your path)
- USE orange light in the southeast

## 24. Bells

- **VERY QUICK:**
  - USE egg until it breaks
- PICK UP note sheet
- USE bells 1, 4, 3, 5, 2 (when numbered from left to right)
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE the following legs
  1. upper left
  2. middle right
  3. lower left
  4. middle left
  5. upper left
  6. middle right
- PICK UP note sheet
- USE (left) bell to call a pig
- When you move left, the pig will move right and vice versa:
  - Position the pigs tail above the circles on the ground then USE its nose to fertilize the spot. Do this for all four circles.
- TALK TO cricket
- TALK TO next cricket and repeat until there are four crickets
- USE crickets in the following order: 3, 1, 3, 4, 2
- PICK UP note sheet
- USE bell 5, 3, 4, 2, 1
- ENTER tube

## 25. Tree

- WALK to the upper left fruit
- USE upper left fruit (it hits the remote)
- WALK into water
- PICK UP note sheet
- EXIT east

## 26. Sea

- WALK onto the grey stone
- USE **all 8** melodies (the icons in th menu bar)
- EXIT west **2 times**, southwest

## 27. Chalk Board

- TALK TO man (the chalk is now red)
- CONNECT the dots
  1. Begin with dot in the lower right corner
  2. Up **2 times**
  3. left
  4. up left
  5. left
  6. down **2 times**
  7. up left into the top left corner
  8. follow the next five dots counter-clockwise
  9. long way to the lower right
  10. down left
  11. left
  12. down right until the last dot and connect to the first one
- USE guitar drawing (1st note)
- EXIT west

## 28. Wire

- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- WALK onto the wire
  - You now have to complete the hard way all the way from the left to the right without leaving the grey wire with your cursor
- EXIT east

## 29. Tree

- WALK into water
- USE rim of glass (2nd note)
- USE remote (3rd note)
- WALK into water
- USE rim of glass (4th note)
- EXIT east

## 30. Sea

- WALK onto rock
- USE blue flute (5th note)
- LOOK AT ear
