# Magsic II - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 26.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from the [AGS homepage](https://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/play/game/615-magsic-ii)

## 1. Before you begin

- Each puzzle saves its progress and once you began, the solutions are different
- SAVE before each puzzle / room so you can try for your own and if you are not able to solve the puzzle, load the saved game and try this solution

## 2. Sea

- TALK TO pink thorn at the right plant **2 times**
- USE right plant to climb on it
- USE bubble
- USE left plant
- USE bubble
- USE small right plant
- USE bubble
- PICK UP music sheet
- USE purple (1), red (5), green (2), orange (4), blue (3)
- Keep USING the five colored figures
  - From time to time there will be a fishing hook in the water
  - **QUICK:**
    - USE figure which is under the fishing hook
  - Repeat this until all five figures are gone
  - If the fishing hooks does not appear, try to jump from some plants, eventually it will appear
- USE yellow egg (you pick it up)
- USE red plant
- USE chest
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- PICK UP music sheet
- USE chest
- **VERY QUICK:**
  - USE bubbles `C D C B C`
  - **NOTE:** IF you miss one or more bubbles, close and reopen the chest to try again
- EXIT up (via the sunbeam)

## 3. Beach

- PICK UP fruit (from a tree)
- EXIT east

## 4. Sandcastle

- USE **both** small circles in the sand
- Keep putting fruits into all circles on the ground
- ENTER castle

## 5. Castle Bridge

- USE water (while still having some fruit)
- **QUICK:**
  - EXIT north by walking over the monster

### 5.1. Castle Backside

- PICK UP music sheet **several times** (until you got it)
- EXIT east

### 5.2. Duck

- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- WALK to the right path (the duck moves to the right side (front view))
- EXIT south (duck poos)

### 5.3. Castle Backside

- PICK UP mirror
- USE a hidden hotspot behind the monster (to hang the mirror on the wall in the hallway)
- WALK to the upper window (your reflection appears in the mirror)
- EXIT east

### 5.4. Duck

- WALK up in the middle path (duck moves to the center)
- EXIT south

### 5.5. Castle Backside

- PICK UP red weed
- EXIT east

### 5.6. Duck

- WALK up in the left path (duck moves to the left)
- EXIT south

## 6. Sandcastle

- USE yellow path to the left (to jump onto it)
- EXIT west

## 7. Beach

- PICK UP music sheet (in the right tree)
- EXIT north

## 8. Hill

- EXIT south

## 9. Beach

- EXIT west

## 10. Cave Entrance

- ENTER cave

### 10.1. Cave

- USE green pipe to enter it

#### 10.1.1. Pipe

- PICK UP music sheet
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE the colored buttons: red, green (1st fly), blue, yellow, green (2nd fly) , red, green, green (3rd fly), blue, green (4th fly) and yellow and green (5th fly)
- USE green tunnel on the right side to exit

### 10.2. Cave

- WALK to the left (the bubble rises)
- WALK the left (the bubble lowers)
- adjust the bubble height by walking to the left and right so the light beam reflects on the sprout on the left, so the sprouts keeps growing until it has leaves
- USE vine
- EXIT west

### 10.3. Mancave

- TALK TO man
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE the white point in the middle (the lowest one) and from there the upper right one above, then continue to connect all points to the right, counter-clockwise until you have connected all dots
- USE head of monster
- Repeatedly USE the head of the monster until the music sheet stays visible
- PICK UP music sheet
- EXIT east

### 10.4. Cave

- USE the same technique to make the sprout grow until the vine has leaves
- USE vine
- EXIT east

## 21. Rocks

- PICK UP rock
- USE rock on iceberg **4 times**
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE

  1. Tiniest
  2. now from left to right

## 22. Forest

- USE grey stone on the left side
- USE fruit on monster
  - **NOTE:** If you put fruits on all circles on the ground, the monster will follow the trail and crashes the stone wall at the sandcastle
- EXIT west **2 times**

## 23. Sandcastle

- EXIT west and climb up the tree

## 24. Cave Entrance

### 24.1. Spider Web

- PICK UP music sheet
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE the spiders in the following order, beginning with the left one, then clockwise:
  1. Spider on the left: outer thread
  2. Inner thread
  3. Middle thread, walking inwards
  4. Inner thread
  5. Center thread, walking inwards
  - USE big spider in the center
- EXIT south

## 25. Cave Entrance

- PICK UP music sheet (hardly visible in the tree)
- EXIT south

## 26. Tentacle Cave

- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- EXIT east without touching the tentacles

## 27. Cave Entrance, Left Side

- USE the buttons yellow **three times**, green, yellow, green **three times**, yellow, green **2 times**
- EXIT southwest

## 28. Tentacle Cave

- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- EXIT southwest without touching the tentacles, then east

## 29. Beach

- PICK UP music sheet (in the right tree)
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- PICK UP fruit
- USE big circle to put a fruit in it
- USE bell
- USE **five** fruits on big circle
- USE bell
- USE **three** fruits on big circle
- USE bell
- EXIT east

## 30. Sandcastle

- EXIT east

## 31. Desert

- PICK UP music sheet
- **NOTE:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- USE **all 8** pieces of music (in the top menu) to play them
- EXIT west, enter castle

## 32. Sandcastle

- EXIT west **2 times**

## 35. Cave Entrance
