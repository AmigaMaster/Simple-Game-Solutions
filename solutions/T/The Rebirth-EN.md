## 1. Office

- USE antique radio
- PICK UP bust (`Bust`)
- PICK UP bottle opener (in the small cabinet) (`Bottle Opener`)
- PICK UP plant (on the right side) (`Wire`)
- USE `Bottle Opener` on button panel
- USE door controls (`Broken Fuse`)
- USE `Wire` on `Broken Fuse` (`Fuse`)
- USE `Fuse` on door controls
- WALK outside
- USE door lock
- TALK TO assistant
- USE `Bust` on assistant
- USE assistant
