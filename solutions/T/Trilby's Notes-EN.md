### 4.1. Ground Floor

### 4.2. Stairwell

  - TAKE PILL
- Walk up to first floor

### 4.3. First Floor

#### 4.3.1. Hallway

- LOOK WALL
- Walk to smoke alarm
- LIGHT MATCH
- Walk east
- OPEN DOOR

### 4.4. Back Yard

- LOOK
- LOOK TABLE
- LOOK CROWBAR
- TAKE CROWBAR (`Crowbar`)
- Walk to ladder
- CLIMB

### 4.5. Ground Floor

#### 4.5.1. Dining Room

- **NOTE:** You need to be in the nightmare version of the dining room. Exit and re-enter the room until it changes.
- USE CROWBAR ON BOARDS
- EAT PILL
  - **NOTE:** If ate the pill after entering the room, you need to walk around until the room changes again an take the pill before opening the door.
- OPEN DOOR

#### 4.5.2. Convention Room

- LOOK TABLE
- LOOK BOTTLE
- LOOK CHISEL
- LOOK MANACLES
- Walk to table in the top right corner
- TAKE BOTTLE (`Speedy Cola`)
- Walk over to left table
- TAKE CHISEL (`Chisel`)
