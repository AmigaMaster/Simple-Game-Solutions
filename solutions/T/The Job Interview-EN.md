## 1. Office

- First Question
  - Choose as you like
- Adventure Question
  - "Yes" (Otherwise, the game ends here)
- PICK UP piece of paper (at the strange device) (`Note`)
- PICK UP pencil (from desk) (`Pencil`)
- PICK UP red thingie (from desk) (`Red Bulb`)
- USE `Red Bulb` on strange device
- USE strange device
  - USE red bulb
- LOOK AT blackboard
  - **NOTE:** The colors and values:
    - Red = A-B
    - Yellow= A/C
    - Blue = B-C
- PICK IP writing pad (from desk) (`Writing Pad`)
- USE `Pencil` on `Writing Pad`
  - **NOTE:** `425`
- USE cupboard
  - ENTER combination from the `Writing Pad` `425`
- OPEN cupboard
- LOOK AT cupboard (`Green Bulb`)
- USE `Green Bulb` on strange device
- USE strange device
  - USE green bulb
- PICK UP letter (from desk) (`Letter`)
- USE window (`Jellybaby`)
- PICK UP piece of paper (from the plant) (`Piece of Paper`)
- LOOK AT `Piece of Paper`
- PICK UP wooden stick (from the blackboard) (`Stick`)
- LOOK AT blackboard
  - **NOTE:** The mathematical equations and the hints for each line when you look at the blackboard several times
    - `1*1*2*3*5*?` = A = Fibonacci sequence: Each number is the sum of the two preceding ones = `8`
    - `17*13*11*7*?` = B = Prime numbers countdown = `5`
    - `18*9*10*5*6*3*?` = C = Variation of the hailstone sequence: Divide by 2, then Add 1 = `4`
  - Together with the hint from the "red world" we noq have:
    - Red    = `8-5` = `3`
    - Yellow = `8/4` = `2`
    - Blue   = `5-4` = `1`

- USE buttons (on the corner of the desk)
  - USE red **3 times**
  - USE yellow **2 times**
  - USE blue
- PICK UP yellow thingie (`Yellow Bulb`)
- USE `Yellow Bulb` on strange device
- USE strange device
  - USE yellow bulb
- LOOK AT blackboard (you cannot understand the calculations)
- USE `Jellybaby` (`Chewed Jellybaby`)
- USE `Chewed Jellybaby` on `Stick` (`Stick with sticky end`)
- USE `Stick with sticky end` on piece of paper (at the bird above the door) (`Piece of Paper`)
- USE `Piece of Paper` on `Piece of Paper` (`Two Pieces of Paper`)
- USE `Two Pieces of Paper` on window
  - **NOTE:** Two circles with a smaller circle in the middle, a big circle with a line across
- USE cupboard
  - Left: Skip the left symbol
  - Middle: Two big circles with a smaller circle in the middle
  - Right: Big circle with a line across
  - Left: Try all symbols until the lock opens
- OPEN cupboard
- LOOK AT cupboard (`Blue Bulb`)
- USE `Blue Bulb` on strange device
  - USE blue bulb
