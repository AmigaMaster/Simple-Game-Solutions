# Trial of the Schnellersparrow - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 16.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/trial-of-the-schnellersparrow-v3.0)

## 1. Control Room

- EXIT left

## 2. Furnace Room

- TALK TO Dr. Fuchs
- PICK UP wrench (below Dr. Fuchs, a bit to the left) (`Wrench`)
- EXIT up the ladder

## 3. Flow Regulator

- PICK UP note (on the flow regulator) (`Blueprint 1`)
- USE `Wrench` on flow regulator
- EXIT down

## 4. Furnace Room

- TALK TO Dr. Fuchs

## 5. Bunk

- PICK UP postcard (on the wall above the top bed) (`Postcard`)
- LOOK AT under bed
- PICK UP under bed (`Blueprint 2`)
- PICK UP Swiss cheese (on the barrels on the left) (`Swiss Cheese`)
- PICK UP knife (`Knife`)
- PICK UP PICK UP cured meats and dry foods (on teh barrel) (`Goose Fat`)
- USE `Goose Fat` on Dr. Fuchs
- USE `Swiss Cheese` on yourself
- EXIT through left door, right

## 6. Control Room

- PICK UP **both** blueprints (`Blueprint 3`, `Blueprint 4`)
- LOOK AT `Blueprint 3`
- LOOK AT `Blueprint 4`
- PICK UP pistol ammunition (the red box on the middle barrel) (`Pistol Ammunition`)
- USE barrel (`Pliers`)
- USE hub (left of the barrel)
  - USE controls (`Welding Torch`)
  - EXIT detail view
- USE tabulator (in the lower right corner)
  - PICK UP punchcard (`Torn Punchcard`)
  - EXIT detail view
- PICK UP resin (on top of the tabulator) (`Resin`)
- PICK UP flaregun (on a barrel in the lower left corner) (`Flaregun`)

## 7. Furnace Room

- USE `Resin` on furnace (`Soft Resin`)
- USE `Soft Resin` on crack (where the steam comes out)
- USE toolbox (behind the crack) (`Pencil`, `Leather Tool`)
- EXIT right

## 8. Control Room

- USE tabulator
  - USE `Torn Punchcard` on `Postcard` (`Punchcard and Postcard`)
  - USE `Pencil` on `Punchcard and Postcard` (`Punchcard with markings`)
  - USE `Leather Tool` on `Punchcard with markings` (`Punchcard`)
  - USE `Punchcard` on tabulator

## 9. Deck

- LOOK AT telescope
- USE `Pliers` on telescope (`Brass Tube`)
- PICK UP magnesium (on the ground beside the telescope) (`Magnesium`)
- USE `Welding Torch` on `Brass Tube` (`Brass Casing`)
- USE `Pliers` on `Pistol Ammunition` (`Black Powder`)
- USE `Black Powder` on `Brass Casing`
- USE `Magnesium` on `Brass Casing` (`Flaregun Ammunition`)
- USE `Flaregun Ammunition` on `Flaregun` (`Loaded Flaregun`)
- EXIT right

## 10. Crows Nest

- USE `Knife` on rigging (on the left side)
- LOOK AT mist
- USE `Loaded Flaregun` on mist
