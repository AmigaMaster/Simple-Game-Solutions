# Trance-Pacific - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 19.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/trance-pacific-v1.02)

## 1. Waiting Room

- USE pen-holder (`Pen`)
- USE (left) door
- TALK TO (left) door **5 times**
- TALK TO Nick
- USE open drawer (`Dictaphone`, `Scrap of Paper`)
- LOOK AT `Scrap of Paper`
- USE `Dictaphone` on Eve
- TALK TO device
  - "61993"
- MEMO (The "M"-Icon in the menu) (left) door
  - ERASE (the left of the three lower buttons)
  - ACTIVATE
    - "I must keep the combination secret"
  - INFUSE
    - "I can reveal the combination"
    - "I can reveal how to open the door"
  - EXIT memo
- TALK TO (left) door
- TALK TO Nick
- USE `Scrap of Paper` on Nick
- USE (left) door

## 2. Hallway

- TALK TO steward
- LOOK AT pipe (the big brown one)
- TALK TO steward
  - "What's in that pipe?
- TALK TO Grace
- EXIT top left

## 3. Bar

- TALK TO woman
- MEMO woman (`Glass`)
  - INFUSE
    - "My hair looks absolutely gorgeous"
  - REPLICATE
    - "I saw two stewards acting strangely"
- TALK TO woman
  - "I've finished doing your hair"
- EXIT right

## 4. Hallway

- TALK TO Grace
  - "We've found something out about the steward"
- MEMO Grace
  - INFUSE "I saw two stewards acting strangely"
- TALK TO Grace
  - "Can you see where the stewards were?"
- FOLLOW Grace

## 5. Room C505

- USE **all** crates
- TALK TO Grace
  - **NOTE:** Grace notices that something is out of place
- MEMO Grace
  - INFUSE "I know exactly how this room looked before"
- TALK TO Grace
- USE box
- TALK TO Grace
- USE light switch
- USE planks (`Torch`)
- USE `Torch` on Grace
- TALK TO Grace
- MEMO Nick
  - REPLICATE "It wasn't the captain the barman was talking to"
- TALK TO Nick
- MEMO Bartender
- LOOK AT `Manual`
- MEMO Bartender
  - ERASE "I must keep the operation secret"
  - INFUSE "Grace is in her store room"
- TALK TO Grace
- TALK TO barman
- TALK TO Eve

## 6. Captain's Office

- USE `Glass` on Eve
- MEMO captain **2 times**
