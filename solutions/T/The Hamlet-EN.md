# The Hamlet - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author
|:-------:|------------|-------
|   1.0   | 16.05.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de)

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/the-hamlet)

## 1. Ranch

- PICK UP note (from the door) (`Note`)
- USE `Note` with `Allan's Letter`
- KICK DOWN front door

### 1.1. Hallway

- OPEN door to office

#### 1.1.1. Office

- SEARCH (left) drawer (of the desk) (`Letter Shred`)
- LOOK AT `Letter Shred`
- EXIT east

### 1.2. Hallway

- EXIT south

## 2. Ranch

- EXIT east

## 3. Abandoned House

- OPEN tool shed
- SEARCH tool shed interior **2 times** (`Knife`, `Axe`)
- EXIT east

## 4. Old House

- EXAMINE windows
- KNOCK ON windows
- EXIT east

## 5. House

- RING doorbell (left of the door)
- EXIT west

## 6. Ranch

### 6.1. Hallway

- OPEN door to living room

## 7. Ranch

- EXIT east

## 8. House

- OPEN door

### 8.1. Entrance Hallway

- USE `Axe` with (right) door

#### 8.1.1. Basement

- SEARCH bodies (`Second Letter Shred`)
- USE `Second Letter Shred` with `Letter Shred` (`Business Letter`)
- EXAMINE `Business Letter`
- EXIT upstairs

### 8.2. Entrance Hallway

- EXIT south, west

## 9. Old House

- EXIT northeast

## 10. Barn

- **ATTENTION:** SAVE YOUR GAME HERE
- HIDE in hay bale
- When the man is standing on the left under the bale:
  - CLIMB ladder
  - Avoid stepping on a squeaky board. To do this, click on the red back wall to walk to the left side
  - USE `Knife` with hanging bale
