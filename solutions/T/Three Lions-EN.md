# Three Lions - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 30.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [Gamejolt](https://gamejolt.com/games/3lions/1002938)

## 1. Wall
- PICK UP tin (Above the Oasis poster) (`Paste`)
- EXIT east

## 2. Front of Shops

- USE door to Fags and Rags

### 2.1. Fags and Rags

- LOOK AT bundle (on the ground)
- PICK UP bundle
- TALK TO shopkeeper
  - "Please can I have one of those old newspapers?"
- EXIT south

## 3. Front of Shops

- EXIT east

## 4. Front of The Three Lions

- PICK UP poster (behind the rubbish) (`Magic Eye`)
- ENTER door to The Three Lions

### 4.1. The Three Lions

- LOOK AT detritus
- USE `Pound` on fruit machine (`Change`)
- USE jukebox
- PICK UP compact disc (`Compact disc`)
- TALK TO Landlord (`Shiv`)
- USE `Shiv` on `Magic Eye` (`Shiny paper`)
- EXIT south

## 5. Front of The Three Lions

- EXIT east

## 6. Front of Recruitment Office

- TALK TO youth
  - Talk about stickers
- EXIT east

## 7. Bus Stop

- ENTER door to Odds 'n Sods

### 7.1. Odds 'n Sods

- LOOK AT sword
- LOOK AT tomato
- LOOK AT note (between the tomatoes)
- TALK TO Lady
- EXIT south

## 8. Bus Stop

- TALK TO Scotsman

## 9. Front of Shops

- ENTER door to Fat Pigs Cafe

### 9.1. Fat Pigs Cafe

- LOOK AT ketchup
- TALK TO chef
  - "Can I have a Bellybuster Butty please?"
- USE `Change` on chef (`Megabap`, `Ketchup`)
- EXIT south

### 9.2. Fags and Rags

- USE `Compact disc` on stereo
- USE stereo
- USE bundle (`Supplement`)
- USE `Shiv` on `Supplement` (`Cutout`)
- USE `Paste` on `Cutout` (`Sticky cutout`)
- USE `Sticky cutout` on `Shiny paper` (`Stone Sticker`)

## 10. Bus Stop

- USE `Megabap` on Scotsman
- USE dartboard (`Shearer sticker`)

### 10.1. Odds 'n Sods

- USE `Ketchup` on Lady (`Sword`)

## 11. Front of Recruitment Office

- USE `Sword` on youth (`Seaman sticker`)
