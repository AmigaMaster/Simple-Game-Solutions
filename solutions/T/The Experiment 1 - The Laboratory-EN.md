## 1. Living Room

- LOOK AT `Call Card` (in your inventory) **2 times**
- LOOK AT books (in the cupboard) **3 times** (`Envelope`)
- LOOK T `Envelope` (`Card`, `Phone numbers`)
- LOOK AT `Phone numbers`
  - **NOTE:** John Rian Mason is an anagram of Marian Johnson
  - Split the Name in syllables and put the numbers from the phone numbers behind them
    - John (3)
    - Ri (9)
    - An (5)
    - Ma (2)
    - Son (6)
  - Now, order them to get the correct name again: Ma (2), Ri (9), An (5), John (3), Son (6)
- USE phone
  - ENTER `555 29536`

## 2. Warehouse

- USE secret door

### 2.2. Elevator

- USE buttons
  - USE `Card` on card reader
  - The display says: `Please enter code`
  - USE 2 (C)
  - USE 6 (O)
  - USE 3 (D)
  - USE 3 (E)
  - USE #

### 2.3. Underground Elevator Hallway

- USE door

### 2.4. Underground Hallway

- USE left door

#### 2.4.1. office

- USE console (on the cabinet)
  - The game is similar to Mastermind but you have unlimited tries
  - Plug in the first four colors and with the red button, check how many are correct
  - Then replace the first color with a different color and check if the correct cables change in order to get the correct colors
  - When you have all correct cables, switch their position until you have the solution
