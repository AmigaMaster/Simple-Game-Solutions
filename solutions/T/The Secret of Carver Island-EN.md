# The Secret of Carver Island - Walkthrough

| Version | Date       | Author                                    |
|:-------:|------------|-------------------------------------------|
|   1.0   | 16.01.2026 | Amiga Master (AmigaMaster-NG@t-online.de) |

Download the free game from [archive.org](https://archive.org/details/the-secret-of-carver-island)

## 1. Emulation Notes

- You ned a DOS environment to play this game. It runs well with [DOSbox-X](https://dosbox-x.com).
- You can use some sort of MIDI-Mapper to get modern General MIDI output with support for soundfonts, for example [VirtualMIDISynth](https://coolsoft.altervista.org/en/virtualmidisynth#download)

## 2. Part 1: Uncovering the Secret

### 2.1. Town

#### 2.1.1. Clock Tower

- ENTER the lower door on the right side of the clock

##### 2.1.1.1. Voodoo Shop

- TALK TO voodoo fortune teller
- LOOK AT shelf
- PICK UP shelf (`Knot Book`)
- LOOK AT `Knot Book`
- USE couch (`Quarter`)
- EXIT shop

#### 2.1.2. Clock Tower

- USE `Quarter` on lotto machine (`Lottery Ticket`)
- EXIT north under the clock

#### 2.1.3. Museum

- USE door

##### 2.1.3.1. Entrance Hall

- TALK TO woman (Barbara)
- USE bookshelf (on the left side of the stairs) (`Goya Book`)
- LOOK AT `Goya Book`
- EXIT west, east

#### 2.1.4. Clock Tower

- ENTER the lower door on the left side of the clock

##### 2.1.4.1. Principals Office

- PICK UP box of toothpicks (from the table) (`Toothpicks`)
- EXIT east

#### 2.1.5. Clock Tower

- EXIT northeast

#### 2.1.6. Viewpoint

- LOOK AT (left) stone wall
  - **NOTE:** `A sailor is a man I trust...`
- EXIT northeast (you need to click on an area inside the archway, not at the screen border to exit this scene)

### 2.2. Map

- WALK the path leading to the east, then at the branch, west

#### 2.2.1. Ol' Harold

- USE door

##### 2.2.1.1. Training Room

- EXIT west

#### 2.2.2. Ol' Harold

- EXIT south

### 2.3. Map

- WALK south, then west an north to the northern harbor

### 2.4. Harbor

#### 2.4.1. Pier

- ENTER cafeteria

##### 2.4.1.1. Cafeteria

- TALK TO lady (`Pink Tissue`)
- TALK TO Flo (`Empty Salt Shaker`)
- EXIT west

#### 2.4.2. Pier

- EXIT west

### 2.5. Map

- WALK to the most eastern spot

#### 2.5.1. Forest

- TALK TO girl (Maria)
- USE `Lottery Ticket` on girl (`Guava`)
- USE `Empty Salt Shaker` on big rock in the middle (`Salt Shaker`)
- PICK UP flowers from the blue patch right above the salt stone (`Blue Flower`)
- EXIT west

#### 2.5.2. Pier

##### 2.5.2.1. Cafeteria

- USE `Guava` on Flo
- USE `Salt Shaker` on Flo
- USE `Toothpicks` on `Pink Tissue` (`Small Umbrella`)
- USE `Small Umbrella` on Flo (`Drink`)

#### 2.5.3. Ol' Harold

##### 2.5.3.1. Training Room

- USE `Drink` on Harold

## 3. Part 2: The Search Begins

### 3.1. Harbor

#### 3.1.1. Pier

##### 3.1.1.1. Prison

- TALK TO other prisoner (Brian)
- TALK TO Mr. Cannon
- TALK TO Brian
- USE prison door to exit

#### 3.1.2. Pier

- EXIT west

### 3.2. Town

#### 3.2.1. Clock Tower

##### 3.2.1.1. Voodoo Shop

- TALK TO voodoo fortune teller

#### 3.2.2. Clock Tower

- TALK TO boy (Anthony)

#### 3.2.3. Museum

##### 3.2.3.1. Entrance Hall

- TALK TO Barbara
  - "Would you like to be on my crew?"
  - "Do you have any idea..."
  - "It's really important..."
  - "Oh, come on,..."
  - "But I am a skilled sailor!"
  - "How can I prove..."
  - "I can recite the sailor's vow!"
  - "A sailor is a man I trust..."
  - "How can I prove..."
  - "I can name any knot there is!"
- USE `Art Supplies` on yourself
- TALK TO Barbara
  - "Check out this cool tattoo!" (`Keys to Carver Spirit`)

### 3.3. Harbor

#### 3.3.1. Cafeteria

- TALK TO Brian
- TALK TO lady (Arianne)
- USE `Goya Book` on Arianne

### 3.4. Forest

- TALK TO boy (Mikey)

### 3.5. Town

#### 3.5.1. Clock Tower

- USE door on the right side of the lotto machine

##### 3.5.1.1. General Store

- TALK TO shopkeeper (`School Paper`)
- LOOK AT `School Paper` (`Weather Forecast`)
- USE `Art Supplies` on `Weather Forecast` (`Faked Weather Forecast`)
- EXIT shop

#### 3.5.2. Clock Tower

- USE `Faked Weather Forecast` on Anthony

##### 3.5.2.1. Voodoo Shop

- TALK TO voodoo fortune teller
  - "...seasickness?"
- USE `Art Supplies` on `Blue Flower` (`Red Flower`)
- USE `Red Flower` on voodoo fortune teller (`Seasickness Pills`)

### 3.6. Forest

- USE `Seasickness Pills` on Mikey

### 3.7. Town

#### 3.7.1. Clock Tower

##### 3.7.1.1. General Store

- USE `Key's to Carver Spirit` on shopkeeper

## 4. Part 3: The Island of Misfit Teachers

### 4.1. Southeastern Beach

- TALK TO woman (Mrs. Hume) (`Chewed Gum`)
- EXIT west

### 4.2. Map

- WALK to the northwest part of the island

### 4.3. Office

- TALK TO woman (Mrs. Gillen) (`Book`)
- LOOK AT `Book` (`Red Clay`, `Bookmark`)
- LOOK AT `Bookmark`
- PICK UP candle (from the table) (`Melted Candle`)
- EXIT east

### 4.4. Map

- WALK TO the center north

### 4.5. Jungle

- TALK TO woman (Mrs. Agudelo) (`Lead Weight`)
- EXIT south

### 4.6. Map

- WALK TO the southwest

### 4.7. Southwestern Beach

- TALK TO man (Mr. Sands) (`Eggplant`)
- USE `Lead Weight` on fire (`Molten Mass of Lead`)
- EXIT north

### 4.8. Map

- WALK TO the northeast

### 4.9. Village

- LOOK AT door
- USE `Chewed Gum` on door (`Shaped Gum`)
- USE `Melted Candle` on `Shaped Gum` (`Wax Covered Gum`)
- USE `Red Clay` on `Wax Covered Gum` (`Clay Mold`)
- USE `Molten Mass of Lead` on `Clay Mold` (`Lead Key`)
- USE `Lead Key` on door

#### 4.9.1. Hut

- PICK UP seltzer bottle (`Empty Seltzer Bottle`)
- EXIT hut

### 4.10. Southeastern Beach

- USE `Empty Seltzer Bottle` on sea (`Seltzer Bottle with Ocean Water`)
- USE `Eggplant` on `Seltzer Bottle with Ocean Water` (`Lemonade from Yesterday`)

### 4.11. Map

- USE ship

### 5.1. Harbor

- "I've come to wish you good luck..."
